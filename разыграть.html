разыгра́ть (10831)


Etymology

раз- (raz-) + | игра́ть (igrátʹ)

Verb

разыгра́ть • (razygrátʹ) pf (imperfective разы́грывать)

  1. to play, to perform
  2. to raffle off, to draw
  3. (colloquial) to play a trick, to play a practical joke, to pull one's leg

ра-зыг-ра́ть
