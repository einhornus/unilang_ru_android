босоно́гий (30602)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *bosonogъ, equivalent to босо́й (bosój, “bare”) + | нога́ (nogá, “foot”) + | -ий (-ij).

Adjective

босоно́гий • (bosonógij) (no comparative)

  1. barefoot, barefooted

Synonyms

  • босо́й (bosój)
