Noun

ремесло́ • (remesló) n inan (genitive ремесла́, nominative plural ремёсла, genitive plural ремёсел)

  1. trade, craft (skilled practice)
  2. handicraft
  3. (colloquial) profession, occupation
  4. (derogatory) uncreative work, unimaginative work, work according to a template

Derived terms

  • реме́сленник (reméslennik)
  • реме́сленный (reméslennyj)
