забра́ло (29061)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic, from Proto-Slavic *zabordlo. Doublet of забороло (zaborólo), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun[edit]

забра́ло • (zabrálo) | n | inan (genitive забрала, nominative plural забрала, genitive plural забра́л)

  1. visor (part of a knight's helmet)

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

забра́ло • (zabrálo)

  1. neuter singular past indicative perfective of забра́ть (zabrátʹ)
