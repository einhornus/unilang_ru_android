забра́ло (29063)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic, from Proto-Slavic *zabordlo. Doublet of заборо́ло (zaborólo), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun

забра́ло • (zabrálo) n inan (genitive забра́ла, nominative plural забра́ла, genitive plural забра́л)

  1. visor (part of a knight's helmet)

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

забра́ло • (zabrálo)

  1. neuter singular past indicative perfective of забра́ть (zabrátʹ)
