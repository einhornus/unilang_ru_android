пиала́ (29199)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Persian پیاله | (pyâle).

Noun[edit]

пиала́ • (pialá) | f | inan (genitive пиалы, nominative plural пиалы or пиалы, genitive plural пиа́л)

  1. piyāla, a small bowl
