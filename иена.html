ие́на (26824)


Etymology

Borrowed from Japanese 円 (en, “yen, circle”).

Noun

ие́на • (ijéna) f inan (genitive ие́ны, nominative plural ие́ны, genitive plural ие́н)

  1. (numismatics) yen
