жева́ть (3270)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *žьvati (“to chew”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǴiewH-.

Verb[edit]

жева́ть • (ževátʹ) | impf (perfective пожева́ть)

  1. to chew, to munch
  2. to ruminate, to masticate
  3. to repeat with tiresome monotony, to repeat something monotonously

Related terms[edit]

  • жвала (žvala)
  • жва́чка (žváčka)
  • жвачный (žvačnyj)
