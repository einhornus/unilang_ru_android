эфир (5261)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ancient Greek αἰθήρ (aithḗr, “upper air”).

Noun[edit]

эфир • (efír) | m | inan (genitive эфи́ра, nominative plural эфи́ры, genitive plural эфи́ров)

  1. ether (organic chemistry: a compound containing an oxygen atom bonded to two hydrocarbon groups)
  2. (physics) ether
  3. (alchemy) ether
  4. (broadcasting) air
    в эфи́ре ― v efíre ― on air
    прямо́й эфир ― prjamój efír ― live broadcast (literally, “direct ether”)

Related terms[edit]

  • прямо́й эфир (prjamój efír)
  • эфи́рный (efírnyj)
