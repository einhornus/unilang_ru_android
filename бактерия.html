бакте́рия (10491)


Noun[edit]

бакте́рия • (baktérija, baktɛrija) | f | inan or f | anim (genitive бакте́рии, nominative plural бакте́рии, genitive plural бакте́рий)

  1. bacterium

Usage notes[edit]

  • With the exception of scientific or medical works, this noun is typically treated as inanimate.

Related terms[edit]

  • бактериа́льный (bakteriálʹnyj, baktɛriálʹnyj)
  • бактериофаг (bakteriofág, baktɛriofág)
