со (84)


Preposition[edit]

со • (so)

  1. with (+ instrumental case)
  2. off, from (+ genitive case)

Usage notes[edit]

This is a variant of с (s) before words beginning with certain awkward consonant clusters such as мн- (mn-), вс- (vs-), and сн- (sn-).
