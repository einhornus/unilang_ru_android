порок (5816)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *porokъ.

Noun[edit]

порок • (porók) | m | inan (genitive поро́ка, nominative plural поро́ки, genitive plural поро́ков)

  1. vice (bad or undesirable habit)
  2. flaw, defect, blemish
Related terms[edit]
  • опоро́чить (oporóčitʹ)
  • поро́чный (poróčnyj)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

порок • (pórok) | f | inan | pl
