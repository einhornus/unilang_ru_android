жара (6325)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *žārь, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷher- (“to heat”). Cognate with Hindi गर्म (garma) and Hindi गर्मी (garmī), Albanian zjarr, Old Armenian ջեր (ǰer), Latin fornāx, Old Norse varmr, Old Prussian goro (“hearth; stove”).

Noun[edit]

жара • (žará) | f | inan (genitive жары́, uncountable)

  1. heat, hot weather, hot spell
Synonyms[edit]
