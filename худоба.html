худо́ба (20309)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic худо́ба (xudoba), from Proto-Slavic *xudoba, from *xudъ + | *-oba. Equivalent to худо́й (xudój) + | -оба (-oba).

Noun[edit]

худо́ба • (xudobá) | f | inan (genitive худо́бы, uncountable)

  1. leanness, scragginess
