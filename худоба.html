худоба (20311)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic худоба (xudoba), from Proto-Slavic *xudoba, from *xudъ + | *-oba. Equivalent to худо́й (xudój) + | -оба (-oba).

Noun

худоба́ • (xudobá) f inan (genitive худобы́, uncountable)

  1. leanness, scragginess
