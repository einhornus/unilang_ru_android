помя́ть (14946)


Etymology

по- (po-) + | мять (mjatʹ)

Verb

помя́ть • (pomjátʹ) pf (imperfective мять)

  1. to rumple, to crumple, to wrinkle, to fumble, to crease
  2. to dent
  3. (colloquial) to press, to squeeze

по-мя́ть
