боров (30589)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *borvъ.

Noun

бо́ров • (bórov) m anim (genitive бо́рова, nominative plural бо́ровы, genitive plural борово́в)

  1. castrated male pig (usually fattened)
  2. (colloquial) obese man

Noun

бо́ров • (bórov) m inan (genitive бо́рова, nominative plural борова́, genitive plural борово́в)

  1. flue, breeching, chimney intake

Etymology 2

Noun

бо́ров • (bórov) m inan pl

  1. genitive plural of бор (bor)

Noun

боро́в • (boróv) m inan pl

  1. genitive plural of бор (bor)
