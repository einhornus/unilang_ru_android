боров (30587)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *borvъ.

Noun[edit]

боров • (bórov) | m | anim (genitive бо́рова, nominative plural бо́ровы, genitive plural бо́ровов)

  1. castrated male pig (usually fattened)
  2. (colloquial) obese man

Noun[edit]

боров • (bórov) | m | inan (genitive бо́рова, nominative plural бо́рова, genitive plural бо́ровов)

  1. flue, breeching, chimney intake

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

боров • (bórov) | m | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of бор (bor)

Noun[edit]

боров • (boróv) | m | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of бор (bor)
