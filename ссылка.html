ссы́лка (3731)


Etymology[edit]

In the meaning "exile", from ссыла́ть (ssylátʹ, “to exile”) + | -ка (-ka); in the meaning "reference", from ссыла́ть(ся) (ssylátʹ(sja), “to refer to”) + | -ка (-ka) .

Noun[edit]

ссы́лка • (ssýlka) | f | inan (genitive ссы́лки, nominative plural ссы́лки, genitive plural ссы́лок)

  1. reference
  2. (computing) link
  3. (historical) exile, banishment (the state of being banished from one's home or country)
