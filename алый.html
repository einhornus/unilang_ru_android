а́лый (3762)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic алъ (alŭ, “bright red; crimson”), from a Turkic source - compare Bashkir ал (al, “bright red”), Turkish al (“crimson”).

Adjective

а́лый • (ályj) (comparative (по)але́е or (по)але́й)

  1. crimson, scarlet, vermilion
  2. reddish, blood-red, ruby-colored

Synonyms

  • кра́сный (krásnyj), пунцо́вый (puncóvyj)

Related terms

  • алеть (aletʹ)
