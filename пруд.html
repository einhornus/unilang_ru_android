пруд (4520)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic прудъ (prudŭ, “stream, pressure, dam”), from Proto-Slavic *prǫdъ. Cognate with Polish prąd (“flow, current”), Slovak prúd (“flow, current”) and Slovene prod (“gravel”).

Noun[edit]

пруд • (prud) | m | inan (genitive пруда́ or пруда́, nominative plural пруды́, genitive plural прудо́в)

  1. pond

Synonyms[edit]

  • озерцо́ | n (ozercó)
