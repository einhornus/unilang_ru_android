столи́ца (1183)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *stolica. Synchronically analysable as стол (stol) + | -ица (-ica).

Noun[edit]

столи́ца • (stolíca) | f | inan (genitive столи́цы, nominative plural столи́цы, genitive plural столи́ц)

  1. capital (city designated as such)
