бокс (6109)


Noun

бокс • (boks) m inan (genitive бо́кса, nominative plural бо́ксы, genitive plural бо́ксов)

  1. (sports) boxing
  2. (medicine) box (isolation ward in a hospital)
  3. box cut (men’s flattop hairstyle in which the hair at the temples and nape of the neck is shaved off)
  4. small box (such as for computer disks)

Related terms

  • боксёр (boksjór)
  • бокси́ровать (boksírovatʹ)
  • полубокс (poluboks)
