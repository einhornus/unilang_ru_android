злость (3642)


Etymology[edit]

злой (zloj) + | -ость (-ostʹ)

Noun[edit]

злость • (zlostʹ) | f | inan (genitive зло́сти, nominative plural зло́сти, genitive plural зло́стей)

  1. spite, malice, rage, anger, grudge (a strong feeling of displeasure, hostility or antagonism towards someone or something)

Synonyms[edit]

  • зло́ба (zlóba)
