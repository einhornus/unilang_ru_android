фы́ркнуть (7703)


Etymology[edit]

фы́ркать (fýrkatʹ) + | -нуть (-nutʹ)

Verb[edit]

фы́ркнуть • (fýrknutʹ) | pf (imperfective фы́ркать)

  1. to snort, to sniff
  2. to sniff scornfully, to scoff, to grouse, to grumble
  3. (colloquial) to chuckle, to chortle, to snicker

Related terms[edit]

  • фырканье (fýrkanʹje)
