гло́хнуть (17111)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *glъxnǫti.

Verb

гло́хнуть • (glóxnutʹ) impf

  1. to grow/to become deaf
    Perfective: огло́хнуть (oglóxnutʹ)
  2. to fade out, to die away, to subside (of a noise)
    Perfective: загло́хнуть (zaglóxnutʹ)
  3. to stall (of an engine)
    Perfective: загло́хнуть (zaglóxnutʹ)
  4. to become choked with weeds
    Perfective: загло́хнуть (zaglóxnutʹ)

Derived terms

  • загло́хнуть (zaglóxnutʹ)
  • огло́хнуть (oglóxnutʹ)
