вилок (6338)


Etymology 1

Noun

вило́к • (vilók) m inan or m anim (genitive вилка́, nominative plural вилки́, genitive plural вилко́в)

  1. (colloquial) head of cabbage
  2. (low colloquial, animate) weak, wretched man; pussy
