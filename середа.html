середа́ (25824)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *serda. Doublet of среда́ (sredá).

Noun[edit]

середа́ • (seredá) | f | inan (genitive середы, nominative plural середы, genitive plural серёд)

  1. (low | | colloquial) Wednesday
  2. (low | | colloquial) middle
