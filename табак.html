таба́к (3605)


Etymology

From French tabac, from Spanish tabaco, from Arabic طَبَاق | (ṭabāq) or Taíno [Term?]. See tobacco for more.

Noun

таба́к • (tabák) m inan (genitive табака́, nominative plural табаки́, genitive plural табако́в)

  1. tobacco
    де́ло таба́к! ― délo tabák! ― things are in a bad way; things are in a sorry state

Related terms

  • таба́чный (tabáčnyj)
  • табачо́к (tabačók)
  • табаке́рка (tabakérka)
