поля́к (5738)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Polish Polak.

Noun[edit]

поля́к • (polják) | m | anim (genitive поля́ка, nominative plural поля́ки, genitive plural поля́ков, feminine по́лька or полячка)

  1. Pole, Polish man
  2. Polack
Synonyms[edit]
  • лях (ljax) (historical, can be slightly derogatory)
  • пшек (pšek) (derogatory)

Related terms[edit]

  • Польша | f (Pólʹša)
  • по́льский | m (pólʹskij)
