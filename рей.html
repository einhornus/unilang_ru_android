рей (27439)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Dutch ra.

Noun

рей • (rej) m inan (genitive ре́я, nominative plural ре́и, genitive plural ре́ев)

  1. yard, a crossways-mast on a ship

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

рей • (rej)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of ре́ять (réjatʹ)
