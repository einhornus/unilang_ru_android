си (6147)


Noun[edit]

си • (si) | n | inan (indeclinable)

  1. (music) B (musical note)
    си диез ― si dijéz/diéz ― B sharp
    си бемоль ― si bemólʹ ― B flat
  2. (music) ti (musical note)
  3. (England) C, c (letter of the Latin alphabet)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (letter of the Latin alphabet): цэ (cɛ)
