ка́ра (7672)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kara (“punishment”), from Proto-Indo-European *kor-. Cognate with Old Irish kaire (“blame, disapproval”).

Noun[edit]

ка́ра • (kára) | f | inan (genitive ка́ры, nominative plural ка́ры, genitive plural ка́р)

  1. penalty, punishment, retribution
Related terms[edit]
  • кара́тель (karátelʹ)
  • кара́тельный (karátelʹnyj)
  • кара́ть (karátʹ)
