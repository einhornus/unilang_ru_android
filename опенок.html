опенок (18596)


Etymology[edit]

о- (o-) + | пень (penʹ, “stump”) + | -ок (-ok), literally "somethong growing about a stump"

Noun[edit]

опёнок • (opjónok) | m | inan (genitive опёнка, nominative plural опята or опёнки, genitive plural опят or опёнков)

  1. honey fungus (Armillaria), a genus of edible parasitic mushroom with a yellow cap and thin stalk that live on trees and woody shrubs, especially Armillaria mellea, that usually grow in groups on tree roots, stumps, and felled trees.
