пеший (9775)


Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from a Proto-Slavic *pěšь derivative of Proto-Indo-European *pṓds.

Adjective[edit]

пеший • (péšij)

  1. unmounted
  2. pedestrian

Related terms[edit]

  • пешехо́д (pešexód)
  • пешко́м (peškóm)
