скворе́ц (5665)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *skvorьcь, from *skvьrь- (“scream”)

Noun[edit]

скворе́ц • (skvoréc) | m | anim (genitive скворца́, nominative plural скворцы́, genitive plural скворцо́в)

  1. starling

Synonyms[edit]

  • шпак (špak)
