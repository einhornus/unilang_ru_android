кли́кать (17942)


Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *klìkati.

Verb[edit]

кли́кать • (klíkatʹ) | impf (perfective кли́кнуть)

  1. (colloquial) to call (someone)
Related terms[edit]
  • клич (klič)
  • клик (klik)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from English click.

Verb[edit]

кли́кать • (klíkatʹ) | impf (perfective кли́кнуть)

  1. to click (to press the button on a mouse when using a computer)
Related terms[edit]
  • клик (klik)
