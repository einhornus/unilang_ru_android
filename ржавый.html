ржа́вый (4283)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic ръжавъ (rŭžavŭ), from Proto-Slavic *rъďavъ, from *rъďa + *-avъ. Equivalent to ржа́ (rža) + | -авый (-ávyj).

Adjective[edit]

ржа́вый • (ržávyj)

  1. rusty (affected by rust)
