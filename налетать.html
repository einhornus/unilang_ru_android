налета́ть (11288)


Etymology

на- (na-) + | лета́ть (letátʹ)

Verb

налета́ть • (naletátʹ) impf (perfective налете́ть)

  1. to fly (upoń, against), to come flying (to)
  2. (colloquial) to bump (into), to run (into), to hit, to collide (with)
  3. to raid
  4. (wind) to get up
  5. (colloquial) to fall (on, upoń), to swoop down (upoń), to rush (at, upoń)

Related terms

  • налета́й! (naletáj!)
  • налётчик (naljótčik), налёт (naljót)

Verb

налета́ть • (naletátʹ) pf

  1. to have flown, to have a flying time (of), to have covered (a distance of)
