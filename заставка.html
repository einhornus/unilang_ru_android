заста́вка (29942)


Noun

заста́вка • (zastávka) f inan (genitive заста́вки, nominative plural заста́вки, genitive plural заста́вок)

  1. headpiece, headband
  2. signature tune, theme song
  3. (graphical user interface) splash screen

за-ста́в-ка
