отрабо́тать (6134)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | рабо́тать (rabótatʹ)

Verb[edit]

отрабо́тать • (otrabótatʹ) | pf (imperfective отраба́тывать)

  1. to work off (usually a debt)
    отрабо́тать кармический долг ― otrabótatʹ karmíčeskij dolg ― to work off the karmic debt
  2. to work (for a certain period of time)
    отрабо́тать пять дней ― otrabótatʹ pjatʹ dnej ― to work for five days
  3. to improve, to polish up (through testing by identifying and eliminating flaws)
  4. to complete, to perfect (a certain task)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (to improve, polish up): усовершенствовать | pf (usoveršénstvovatʹ)
