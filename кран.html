кран (3990)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch kraan or from Low German kran. Cognate to German Kranich (“crane”)

Noun

кран • (kran) m inan (genitive кра́на, nominative plural кра́ны, genitive plural кра́нов)

  1. (engineering) valve (a ball valve, a plug valve, or similar rotating device controlling flow through a pipe)
  2. faucet, tap, spigot
  3. crane (machinery)

Related terms

  • краник (kranik), крановщик (kranovščik), крановщица (kranovščica), стоп-кран (stop-kran), автокран (avtokran)
  • крановой (kranovoj)

кран
