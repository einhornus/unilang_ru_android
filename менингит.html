менинги́т (19153)


Etymology

Ultimately from New Latin meningitis, from Ancient Greek μῆνιγξ (mêninx, “membrane”).

Noun

менинги́т • (meningít) m inan (genitive менинги́та, nominative plural менинги́ты, genitive plural менинги́тов)

  1. meningitis
