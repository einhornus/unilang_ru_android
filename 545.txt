***mime.html
<head> <meta charset='utf-8'> </head> <body><h1> mime (11392)</h1><br><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p>From Old English mīma ("a mime") from <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin" class="extiw" title="w:Latin">Latin</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="la">mimus</i>, from <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Greek" class="extiw" title="w:Ancient Greek">Ancient Greek</a></span> <i class="polytonic mention" lang="grc">μῖμος</i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="grc-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">mîmos</span>, <span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">imitator, actor</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>. Reinforced in Middle English by <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_language" class="extiw" title="w:French language">French</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr">mime</i>.
</p>
<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">mime</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">mimes</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>A form of acting without words; pantomime.</li>
<li>A pantomime actor.</li>
<li>A classical theatrical entertainment in the form of farce.</li>
<li>A performer of such a farce.</li>
<li>A person who mimics others in a comical manner.</li>
<li>Any of various papilionid butterflies of the genus <span class="biota"><a href="https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Chilasa" class="extiw" title="wikispecies:Chilasa"><i>Chilasa</i></a></span> or <i>Papilio</i>, that mimic other species in appearance.</li></ol>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Related_terms">Related terms</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<ul><li><span class="Latn" lang="en">pantomime</span></li></ul>
<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Verb">Verb</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">mime</strong> (<i>third-person singular simple present</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en 3 | s | pres-form-of" lang="en">mimes</b>, <i>present participle</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en pres | ptcp-form-of" lang="en">miming</b>, <i>simple past and past participle</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en past | and | past | ptcp-form-of" lang="en">mimed</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>To mimic.</li>
<li>To act without words.</li>
<li>To represent an action or object through gesture, without the use of sound.</li></ol>
