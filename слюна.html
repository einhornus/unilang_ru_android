слюна́ (5062)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *slina.

Noun[edit]

слюна́ • (sljuná) | f | inan (genitive слюны́, uncountable)

  1. saliva, spittle

Related terms[edit]

  • сплю́нуть (spljúnutʹ)
