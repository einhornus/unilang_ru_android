стоя́ть (97)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic стоꙗти (stojati), from Proto-Slavic *stojati (“to stand”), from Proto-Indo-European *stoh₂-.

Verb[edit]

стоя́ть • (stojátʹ) | impf (perfective постоять)

  1. to stand, to be situated
  2. to stop

Usage notes[edit]

  • Not to be confused with most of the conjugation forms pertaining to стоить (stóitʹ).
