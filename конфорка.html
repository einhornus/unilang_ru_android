конфо́рка (32091)


Noun[edit]

конфо́рка • (konfórka) | f | inan (genitive конфо́рки, nominative plural конфо́рки, genitive plural конфо́рок)

  1. (of a stove) ring, burner
  2. (of a samovar) crown, top ring
