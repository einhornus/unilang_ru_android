воро́ний (17562)


Etymology

воро́на (voróna) + | -ий (-ij)

Adjective

воро́ний • (vorónij)

  1. (relational) crow; crow's; corvine
    воро́ний глаз ― vorónij glaz ― Paris (type of plant) (literally, “crow's eye”)

Related terms
