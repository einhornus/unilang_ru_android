ти́кать (12172)


Etymology 1[edit]

Onomatopoeic. From the tick-tack sound of a clock.

Verb[edit]

ти́кать • (tíkatʹ) | impf (perfective тикнуть)

  1. to tick (of a clock)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from Ukrainian тікати (tikáty).

Verb[edit]

ти́кать • (tikátʹ) | impf

  1. (regional, | Ukraine) to run away
