угро́бить (17085)


Verb

угро́бить • (ugróbitʹ) pf (imperfective гро́бить)

  1. (colloquial) to kill, to do to death, to give the business
  2. (colloquial) to throw away, to burn up, to send down the drain

Related terms

  • гроб m (grob)
