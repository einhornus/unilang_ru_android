ста́вить (986)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic ставити (staviti), from Proto-Slavic *staviti (“to place, put”).

Verb[edit]

ста́вить • (stávitʹ) | impf (perfective поста́вить)

  1. to put, to place, to set, to stand, to station
  2. (a compress) to apply, to put on
  3. (a play) to put on stage, to stage, to produce, to present
  4. (gambling) to wager, to place a bet, to stake
  5. (a question) to raise, to put

Related terms[edit]

  • ста́вка (stávka)
  • ста́вня (stávnja), ста́вень (stávenʹ)
  • ста́вленник (stávlennik)
