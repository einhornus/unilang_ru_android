ля́згать (12595)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from лязг (ljazg) + | -ать (-atʹ).

Verb[edit]

ля́згать • (ljázgatʹ) | impf (perfective ля́згнуть)

  1. to clank, to clang
    он ля́згает зуба́ми ― on ljázgajet zubámi ― his teeth are chattering

Related terms[edit]

  • лязг (ljazg)
