го́дный (10105)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *godьnъ, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰedʰ- (“to unite, be associated, suit”). Cognate with English good.

Adjective

го́дный • (gódnyj) (comparative (по)годне́е or (по)годне́й)

  1. fit, suitable, well-suited, valid, apposite, appropriate, relevant (для (dlja) + genitive or к (k) + dative or на (na) + accusative, for/to something)

Derived terms

  • го́дность (gódnostʹ)
  • приго́дный (prigódnyj)

го́д-ный
