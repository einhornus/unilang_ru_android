пото́м (83)


Etymology 1[edit]

по (po, “after”) + | то́м (tom, “that”)

Adverb[edit]

пото́м • (potóm)

  1. afterwards, afterward, after that
  2. then
  3. later
    Я э́то сделаю пото́м.
    Ja éto sdélaju potóm.
    I will do that later.

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

пото́м • (pótom) | m | inan

  1. instrumental singular of пот (pot)
