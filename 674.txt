<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">poster</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">posters</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>A picture of a celebrity, an event etc., intended to be attached to a wall.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">He has <b>posters</b> of his favorite band, sports teams and holiday resorts up.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>An advertisement to be posted on a pole, wall etc. to advertise something.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">I saw a <b>poster</b> for the film on the side of a bus.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">Internet</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> One who posts a message.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Some <b>posters</b> left the online message board after the squabble.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">ice hockey<span class="ib-comma">,</span> slang</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A shot that hits a goalpost instead of passing into the goal.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">We got three <b>posters</b> in the third and lost.</i></div></dd></dl></li></ol>
