ку́брик (13106)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch koebrug.

Noun

ку́брик • (kúbrik) m inan (genitive ку́брика, nominative plural ку́брики, genitive plural ку́бриков)

  1. (nautical) sleeping quarters, sleeping compartment
  2. (nautical, historical) berth deck
  3. (military) barracks room, cubicle
  4. (informal) room (in a dormitory, bunkhouse etc.)

Derived terms

  • кубриковый (kubrikovyj, adjective)
  • кубричный (kubričnyj, adjective)
