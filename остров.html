о́стров (1051)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *ostrovъ, from Proto-Indo-European *strow-.

Noun[edit]

о́стров • (óstrov) | m | inan (genitive острова, nominative plural острова, genitive plural острово́в)

  1. island
  2. isle

ост-ров
