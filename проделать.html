проде́лать (5319)


Etymology[edit]

про- (pro-) + | де́лать (délatʹ)

Verb[edit]

проде́лать • (prodélatʹ) | pf (imperfective проде́лывать)

  1. to make (a hole), to break through (a hole)
  2. to do (a trick)
  3. to carry out (a task; work)
