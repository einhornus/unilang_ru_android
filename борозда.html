борозда́ (12012)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *borzda. Doublet of бразда́ (brazdá), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

Noun[edit]

борозда́ • (borozdá) | f | inan (genitive борозды, nominative plural борозды, genitive plural боро́зд, diminutive бороздка)

  1. furrow

Related terms[edit]

  • борозди́ть (borozdítʹ)
