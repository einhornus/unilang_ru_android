поглоще́ние (23063)


Noun

поглоще́ние • (pogloščénije) n inan (genitive поглоще́ния, nominative plural поглоще́ния, genitive plural поглоще́ний)

  1. absorption (act or process of absorbing or sucking in anything)
  2. intake, uptake, takeover, acquisition
