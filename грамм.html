грамм (4242)


Noun

грамм • (gramm) m inan (genitive гра́мма, nominative plural гра́ммы, genitive plural гра́ммов or грамм*) (* Colloquial.)

  1. gram

Usage notes

  • The irregular zero genitive plural form is used in technical and scientific contexts and colloquial speech, the regular form is normal in literary styles.
