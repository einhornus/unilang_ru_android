интерне́т (5845)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English Internet.

Noun[edit]

интерне́т • (intɛrnɛt) | m | inan (genitive интерне́та, nominative plural интерне́ты, genitive plural интерне́тов)

  1. the Internet
    Ско́лько вы вчера́ сиде́ли в интерне́те?
    Skólʹko vy včerá sidéli v intɛrnɛte?
    How long did you hang out on the internet yesterday?

Usage notes[edit]

The plural form интерне́ты (intɛrnɛty) is used in a humorous way, like internets (from G. W. Bush).
