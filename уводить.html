уводи́ть (7389)


Etymology[edit]

у- (u-) + | води́ть (vodítʹ)

Verb[edit]

уводи́ть • (uvodítʹ) | impf (perfective увести́)

  1. to take away, to lead away, to withdraw (troops)
  2. to carry off, to lift
