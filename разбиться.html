разби́ться (6058)


Etymology[edit]

разби́ть (razbítʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

разби́ться • (razbítʹsja) | pf (imperfective разбива́ться)

  1. (intransitive) to break, to crash, to shatter
  2. (intransitive) to break up, to split, to divide
  3. to collapse, to die (e.g. of dreams)
  4. to get seriously hurt
  5. passive of разби́ть (razbítʹ)
