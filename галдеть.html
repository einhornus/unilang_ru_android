галде́ть (16668)


Etymology[edit]

From галда (gáldá), from Proto-Slavic *galъda.

Verb[edit]

галде́ть • (galdétʹ) | impf (perfective загалде́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to make a racket (a lot of noise)
