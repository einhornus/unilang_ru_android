фиа́лка (16035)


Etymology[edit]

Old Polish fiałek, variant of fiołek, from Middle High German vîol, from Latin viola. Influenced by the common suffix -ка (-ka).

Noun[edit]

фиа́лка • (fiálka) | f | inan (genitive фиа́лки, nominative plural фиа́лки, genitive plural фиа́лок)

  1. (botany) violet

Related terms[edit]
