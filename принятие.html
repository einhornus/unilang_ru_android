приня́тие (6200)


Etymology[edit]

приня́ть (prinjátʹ) + | -тие (-tije)

Noun[edit]

приня́тие • (prinjátije) | n | inan (genitive приня́тия, nominative plural приня́тия, genitive plural приня́тий)

  1. acceptance, adoption (an agreeing to terms or proposals)
  2. reception (act of receiving something)
