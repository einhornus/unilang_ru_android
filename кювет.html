кюве́т (25846)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French cuvette.

Noun[edit]

кюве́т • (kjuvét) | m | inan (genitive кюве́та, nominative plural кюве́ты, genitive plural кюве́тов)

  1. ditch (along a road)
  2. (military) cunette, cuvette
  3. (dated) moat

Related terms[edit]

  • кюве́та (kjuvéta)
