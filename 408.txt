<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">holly</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">hollies</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>Any of various shrubs or (mostly) small trees, of the genus <i>Ilex</i>, either evergreen or deciduous, used as decoration especially at Christmas.</li>
<li>The wood from this tree.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">with a qualifier</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> Any of several unrelated plant species likened to <i>Ilex</i> because of their prickly, evergreen foliage and/or round, bright-red berries</li></ol>
