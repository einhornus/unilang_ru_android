жужжа́ть (11547)


Etymology[edit]

Onomatopoeic.

Verb[edit]

жужжа́ть • (žužžátʹ) | impf (perfective зажужжать or прожужжать)

  1. to hum, to buzz, to drone, to whirr
  2. (figuratively, | often | derogatory) to say something
  3. (figuratively | | derogatory) to fatigue someone by saying too much or by repetition of the same thing (прожужжать все у́ши, все у́ши прожужжал)
