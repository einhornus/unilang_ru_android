напада́ть (5175)


Etymology 1

Verb

напада́ть • (napadátʹ) impf (perfective напа́сть)

  1. to attack, to fall on, to assault, to descend (on)
  2. to come acrosś, to come upoń, to hit on
    я напа́л на мысль ― ja napál na myslʹ ― the thought occurred me
  3. (feeling, condition) to come (over), to grip, to seize, to overcome
Related terms
  • нападе́ние (napadénije)

Etymology 2

Verb

напа́дать • (napádatʹ) pf

  1. to fall (some quantity)
