ло́же (6747)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Old East Slavic ло́же (lože). Cognate to лежа́ть. Compare Polish łoże.

Noun[edit]

ло́же • (lóže) | n | inan (genitive ло́жа, nominative plural ло́жа, genitive plural лож)

  1. (archaic) bed, couch
  2. riverbed; channel
  3. gunstock
Synonyms[edit]
  • (bed): посте́ль (postélʹ), крова́ть (krovátʹ)
  • (riverbed): ру́сло (rúslo)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

ло́же • (lóže) | f | inan

  1. dative singular of ло́жа (lóža)
  2. prepositional singular of ло́жа (lóža)
