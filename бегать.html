бегать (1326)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *běgati, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰegʷ-, cognate with Lithuanian bėgti and Ancient Greek φέβομαι (phébomai, “to flee”).

Verb[edit]

бегать • (bégatʹ) | impf (perfective побе́гать)

  1. to run

Usage notes[edit]

  • Бегать (Bégatʹ) and побе́гать (pobégatʹ) are abstract verbs. Their counterparts, бежа́ть (bežátʹ) and побежа́ть (pobežátʹ), are concrete verbs.
  • The compounds of бегать are formed from -бегать (-begátʹ).
