sonny (18673)


Etymology

son + | -y

Noun

sonny (plural sonnies)

  1. a familiar form of address for a boy
  2. (colloquial, derogatory) form of address to a boy or man, to express contempt, warning etc.
    Synonyms: friend, buster, buddy, pal
