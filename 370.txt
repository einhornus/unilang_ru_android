<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">gold</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">golds</b>)

<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A heavy yellow elemental metal of great value, with atomic number 79 and symbol Au.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable  or  uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A coin or coinage made of this material, or supposedly so.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A deep yellow colour, resembling the metal gold.

<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A gold medal.
