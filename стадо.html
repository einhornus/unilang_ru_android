ста́до (3585)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic ста́до (stado), from Proto-Slavic *stado (“herd, flock”).

Noun[edit]

ста́до • (stádo) | n | inan (genitive стада, nominative plural стада, genitive plural ста́д)

  1. herd, flock

Related terms[edit]

  • стадный (stadnyj)
