муть (10285)


Noun

муть • (mutʹ) f inan (genitive му́ти, nominative plural му́ти, genitive plural му́тей)

  1. dregs, mud, blur; slime sediment
  2. (colloquial) nonsense, froth, foam

Related terms

  • му́тный (mútnyj)
  • мути́ть impf (mutítʹ)
