треск (3348)


Noun[edit]

треск • (tresk) | m | inan (genitive треска, nominative plural трески, genitive plural тре́сков)

  1. crack, crash, snap (sharp sound made when solid material breaks)

Synonyms[edit]

  • хруст (xrust)
