восстана́вливать (8788)


Etymology[edit]

восстанови́ть (vosstanovítʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

восстана́вливать • (vosstanávlivatʹ) | impf (perfective восстанови́ть)

  1. to restore, to reestablish, to reinstate
  2. to recover, to recuperate
  3. to regain, to retrieve
  4. to regenerate, to reclaim
  5. to reconstruct, to rebuild
  6. to repair, to revive
  7. to reinstall
  8. to renew

Related terms[edit]

  • восстановле́ние | n (vosstanovlénije)
