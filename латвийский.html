латви́йский (16843)


Etymology

ла́твия (Látvija) + | -и́йский (-íjskij)

Adjective

латви́йский • (latvíjskij)

  1. Latvian (relating to the country Latvia as a state)

Usage notes

  • латви́йский is used to refer to the country Latvia as a state, its laws, constitution, flag, government, etc. To refer to ethnic Latvians and their language, culture, customs, traditions and so on, use латы́шский (latýšskij). Compare also росси́йский (rossíjskij) and ру́сский (rússkij).

Related terms

  • ла́твия (Látvija)
  • латвиец (latvijec), латвийка (latvijka)
  • латы́ш (latýš), латы́шка (latýška), латы́шский (latýšskij)
