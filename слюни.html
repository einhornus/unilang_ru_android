слю́ни (14544)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *slina, from Proto-Indo-European *H₂leyH- (“to smear”). Cognates include Old Church Slavonic слина (slina), Bulgarian слюна́ (sljúna), Czech slina, Polish ślina.

Noun[edit]

слю́ни • (sljúni) | m | inan | pl (genitive слюне́й, plural only, diminutive слю́нки)

  1. (colloquial) saliva, drool
