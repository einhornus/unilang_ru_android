императри́ца (14122)


Etymology

Originally borrowed from Latin imperātrīx via its oblique stem (compare the ablative singular imperātrīce), but the resulting form may also be interpreted as an in-house Russian Slavic derivation from импера́тор (imperator) with the suffix -ица (-ica).

Noun

императри́ца • (imperatríca) f anim (genitive императри́цы, nominative plural императри́цы, genitive plural императри́ц, masculine импера́тор)

  1. empress
