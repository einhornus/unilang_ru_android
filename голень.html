го́лень (16263)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic голѣнь (golěnĭ), го́лень (golenĭ), from Proto-Slavic *golěnь / *golenь (“shin”), from *golъ (“naked”).

Noun[edit]

го́лень • (gólenʹ) | f | inan (genitive го́лени, nominative plural го́лени, genitive plural го́леней, diminutive голяшка)

  1. (anatomy) shin, shank

Synonyms[edit]

  • лысто (lýsto)
