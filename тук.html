тук (11223)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic тукъ (tukŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tukъ

Noun[edit]

тук • (tuk) | m | inan (genitive ту́ка, nominative plural ту́ки, genitive plural ту́ков)

  1. (obsolete) fat, lard
  2. fertilizer

Related terms[edit]

  • ту́чный (túčnyj)
  • тучность (túčnostʹ)
  • тучнеть (tučnétʹ)
