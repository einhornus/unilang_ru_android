отстоя́ть (9142)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | стоя́ть (stojátʹ)

Verb[edit]

отстоя́ть • (otstojátʹ) | pf (imperfective отста́ивать) (imperative missing)

  1. to defend, to protect, to hold against enemy attacks
  2. (figuratively) to maintain, to assert
  3. to desilt

Verb[edit]

отстоя́ть • (otstojátʹ) | pf (imperfective отста́ивать) (imperative not missing)

  1. to stand (on one's feet) (somewhere) from beginning to end
  2. to tire out (one's legs) from standing too long

Verb[edit]

отстоя́ть • (otstojátʹ) | impf (imperative missing)

  1. to be away (from), to be distant (from), to be apart, to be located (a certain distance from)
