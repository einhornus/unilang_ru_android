берест (22229)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *berstъ.

Noun[edit]

берест • (bérest) | m | inan (genitive береста, nominative plural бересты, genitive plural бе́рестов)

  1. elm (tree or wood) (Ulmus minor)
