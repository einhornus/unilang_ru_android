прибы́тие (6022)


Noun[edit]

прибы́тие • (pribýtije) | n | inan (genitive прибы́тия, nominative plural прибы́тия, genitive plural прибы́тий)

  1. arrival (act of arriving or something that has arrived)

Related terms[edit]

  • быть (bytʹ)
  • при- (pri-)
