шля́гер (27272)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Schlager.

Noun

шля́гер • (šljáger) m inan (genitive шля́гера, nominative plural шля́геры, genitive plural шля́геров)

  1. hit (success, especially in the entertainment industry)

Synonyms

  • хит (xit)

Related terms

  • шлягерный (šljagernyj)
