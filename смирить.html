смири́ть (30546)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic съмѣрити (sŭměriti, “mitigate”). After regular assimilation ѣ…и gt; и…и reanalysed as a derivative мири́ть (mirítʹ).

Verb[edit]

смири́ть • (smirítʹ) | pf (imperfective смиря́ть)

  1. to humble, to tame
  2. to suppress, to restrain (an emotion)
