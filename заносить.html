заноси́ть (10110)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) + | носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Verb[edit]

заноси́ть • (zanosítʹ) | impf (perfective занести́)

  1. to bring, to carry (somewhere) (in passing)
  2. to note down, to enter (data, records)
  3. (impersonal) to cause to skid
    маши́ну зано́сит ― mašínu zanósit ― the car is skidding
  4. (impersonal) to cause to become covered (with) (e.g. snow, dust, etc.)
  5. to lift, to raise (arm, leg, etc.)

Related terms[edit]

  • зано́с | m (zanós)

Verb[edit]

заноси́ть • (zanosítʹ) | pf (imperfective занашивать)

  1. to dirty by wearing a long time
