зелень (3824)


Noun

зе́лень • (zélenʹ) f inan (genitive зе́лени, nominative plural зе́лени, genitive plural зе́леней)

  1. verdure (greenness, vegetation)
  2. greens; vegetables, edible grass (i. e. dill, parsley, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) dollars, greenbacks
