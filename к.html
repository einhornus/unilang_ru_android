Etymology 2[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kъ(n).

Preposition[edit]

к • (k) (+ dative case)

  1. to, toward
    к до́му ― k dómu ― to the house
    к бра́ту ― k brátu ― to (my) brother
    ко мне ― ko mne ― to me
  2. by, for (of time)
    ко вто́рнику ― ko vtórniku ― by Tuesday, for Tuesday
Usage notes[edit]

ко (ko) is used before words beginning with certain awkward consonant clusters such as мн- (mn-), вс- (vs-), вт- (vt-), and сн- (sn-).

Etymology 3[edit]
