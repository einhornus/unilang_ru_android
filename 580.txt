<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">night</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">nights</b>)
</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The period of darkness beginning at the end of evening astronomical twilight when the sun is 18 degrees below the <a href = vnd.unilang:горизонт.html>horizon</a>, and ending at the beginning of morning astronomical twilight.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">How do you sleep at <b>night</b> when you attack your kids like that!?</i></div></dd></dl>

<dl><dd>The Bat—they called him the Bat. Like a bat he chose the <b>night</b> hours for his work of rapine; like a bat he struck and vanished, pouncingly, noiselessly; like a bat he never showed himself to the face of the day.</dd></dl></li>
<li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>2013</b> July 19,  <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.theguardian.com/profile/iansample">Ian Sample</a>,  “<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2013/jul/08/irregular-bedtimes-affect-childrens-brains">Irregular bedtimes may affect children's brains</a>”, in  <cite><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Guardian_Weekly" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Guardian Weekly">The Guardian Weekly</a></cite>, volume 189, number 6, page 34:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">Irregular bedtimes may disrupt healthy brain development in young children, according to a study of intelligence and sleeping habits.   |  Going to bed at a different time each <b>night</b> affected girls more than boys, but both fared worse on mental tasks than children who had a set bedtime, researchers found.</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> An evening or night spent at a particular activity.
