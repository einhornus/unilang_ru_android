вегетати́вный (20354)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from German vegetativ + | -ный (-nyj).

Adjective[edit]

вегетати́вный • (vegetatívnyj)

  1. vegetative, autonomic

Related terms[edit]

  • вегетация (vegetácija)
