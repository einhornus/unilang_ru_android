конек (7064)


Etymology[edit]

конь (konʹ) + | -ёк (-jók)

Noun[edit]

конёк • (konjók) | m | anim or m | inan (genitive конька́, nominative plural коньки́, genitive plural конько́в)

  1. (animate) diminutive of конь (konʹ): (little) horse, horsy
    морско́й конёк ― morskój konjók ― seahorse
    Конёк-Горбунок ― Konjók-Gorbunók ― Konyok-Gorbunok ("Little Humpbacked Horse" in the Slavic mythology)
  2. (inanimate) skate (ice skate or roller skate)
    однорядный роликовый конёк ― odnorjádnyj rólikovyj konjók ― in-line skate
  3. (animate) hobby, fad, soapbox, favourite subject
    сесть на своего́ конька́ ― sestʹ na svojevó konʹká ― to pursue one's favorite subject, to mount one's hobby-horse
  4. (animate) pipit (bird)
  5. (inanimate) (roof) ridge, finial
