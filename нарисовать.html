нарисова́ть (3448)


Etymology[edit]

на- (na-) + | рисова́ть (risovátʹ)

Verb[edit]

нарисова́ть • (narisovátʹ) | pf (imperfective рисова́ть)

  1. to draw, to paint, to design, to crayon
  2. to picture (imagine)
  3. to depict, to paint, to portray
