гаси́ть (8361)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *gasìti.

Verb

гаси́ть • (gasítʹ) impf (perfective погаси́ть)

  1. to put out, to extinguish
  2. to turn off (light)
  3. to cancel (debt); to make invalid
  4. to quench
  5. to slake (lime)
  6. to kill (a ball)
  7. (slang) to kill, to murder, to waste; to beat
