трос (7512)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch tros.

Noun

трос • (tros) m inan (genitive тро́са, nominative plural тро́сы, genitive plural тро́сов)

  1. hawser, line, rope (thick)

Synonyms

  • кана́т (kanát)
