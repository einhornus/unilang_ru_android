мимохо́дом (11369)


Etymology

From ми́мо (mímo) + | -о- (-o-) + | -ход (-xod) + | -ом (-om, instrumental suffix), based on a combining form of ходи́ть (xodítʹ, “to walk, to go”).

Adverb

мимохо́дом • (mimoxódom)

  1. (colloquial) in passing
