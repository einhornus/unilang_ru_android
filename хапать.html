ха́пать (29495)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *xāpàti.

Verb[edit]

ха́пать • (xápatʹ) | impf (perfective схапать or хапнуть)

  1. (colloquial, | rude) to grab (quickly); to steal; to take (from someone unlawfully)
  2. (colloquial, | rude) to take bribes
