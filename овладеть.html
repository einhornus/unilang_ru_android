овладе́ть (5161)


Etymology[edit]

о- (o-) + | владе́ть (vladétʹ)

Verb[edit]

овладе́ть • (ovladétʹ) | pf (imperfective овладева́ть) (+ instrumental case)

  1. to seize, to capture, to become the owner
  2. to overcome (of feelings)
  3. to get control over, to dominate
  4. to master, to become proficient
