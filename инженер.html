инжене́р (1111)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Ingenieur, from French ingénieur.

Noun

инжене́р • (inženér) m anim (genitive инжене́ра, nominative plural инжене́ры, genitive plural инжене́ров)

  1. engineer (person qualified or professionally engaged in engineering)

Derived terms

  • инжене́рный (inženérnyj)
  • инжене́рия (inženérija)
