туша (27661)


Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

туша • (túša) | f | inan (genitive туши, nominative plural туши, genitive plural туш)

  1. carcass
  2. (colloquial, | derogatory) hulk; fat man's body, bulk

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

туша • (túša) | m | inan

  1. genitive singular of туш (tuš)

Etymology 3[edit]

Participle[edit]

туша • (tušá)

  1. present adverbial imperfective participle of туши́ть (tušítʹ)
