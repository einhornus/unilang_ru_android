сажа́ть (4805)


Verb[edit]

сажа́ть • (sažátʹ) | impf (perfective посади́ть)

  1. to seat, to set
  2. to plant
  3. to put in jail

Synonyms[edit]

  • сади́ть (sadítʹ) (colloquial)
