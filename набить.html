наби́ть (6344)


Etymology

на- (na-) + | бить (bitʹ)

Verb

наби́ть • (nabítʹ) pf (imperfective набива́ть)

  1. to stuff, to pack, to fill
  2. to fix (ontó)
  3. (colloquial) to type (some text)
  4. to break, to smash (a lot of something)

на-би́ть
