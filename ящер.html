я́щер (21177)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *aščerъ.

Noun[edit]

я́щер • (jáščer) | m | anim (genitive я́щера, nominative plural я́щеры, genitive plural я́щеров)

  1. (a large) lizard (reptile)
  2. pangolin (mammal)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (pangolin): панголин (pangolín)

Related terms[edit]

  • я́щерица (jáščerica)
  • я́щерка (jáščerka)
