обмы́ть (18062)


Etymology[edit]

об- (ob-) + | мыть (mytʹ)

Verb[edit]

обмы́ть • (obmýtʹ) | pf (imperfective обмыва́ть)

  1. to wash, to bathe
  2. (colloquial) to celebrate, to drink (to a purchase or deal)

Related terms[edit]

  • обмывание (obmyvanije)
