а́нгел (2472)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ἄγγελος (ángelos).

Noun[edit]

а́нгел • (ángel) | m | anim (genitive а́нгела, nominative plural а́нгелы, genitive plural а́нгелов)

  1. angel
  2. an innocent
  3. dear, darling
