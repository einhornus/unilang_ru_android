усиде́ть (20983)


Etymology

у- (u-) + | сиде́ть (sidétʹ)

Verb

усиде́ть • (usidétʹ) pf (imperfective уси́живать)

  1. (perfective only) to remain sitting (after being pushed)
  2. (perfective only) to sit still (despite one's desires or intentions)
  3. (perfective only, colloquial) to hold on (to a position or job), to remain (in a position or job)
  4. (perfective only, usually in the negative) to stay in place, to refrain from leaving
  5. (low colloquial) to sit at the table and eat or drink (something) (fully or in large quantities)

Related terms

  • сиде́ть (sidétʹ)
  • сиде́ние (sidénije), сиде́нье (sidénʹje)
  • сидя́чий (sidjáčij)
  • си́день (sídenʹ)
