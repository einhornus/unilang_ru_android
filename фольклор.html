фолькло́р (14655)


Noun

фолькло́р • (folʹklór) m inan (genitive фолькло́ра, nominative plural фолькло́ры, genitive plural фолькло́ров)

  1. folklore

Usage notes

  • Some speakers and scholars insist on the palatalised pronunciation of the 2nd л, hence the spelling "фольклёр".
