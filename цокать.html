цо́кать (17149)


Verb

цо́кать • (cókatʹ) impf

  1. to click, to clatter, to clank
  2. to utter the sound [t͡s], to click with the tongue to make a similar sound
  3. (linguistics) to pronounce the letter ч (č) ([t͡ɕ]) as ц (c) ([t͡s]) in some dialects
