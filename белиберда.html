белиберда́ (31623)


Etymology[edit]

The variants билиберда (biliberdá), кериберда (keriberdá), кулиберда (kuliberdá) are also found. Per Vasmer, possibly borrowed from a Turkic language.

Noun[edit]

белиберда́ • (beliberdá) | f | inan (genitive белиберды́, uncountable)

  1. (colloquial, | derogatory) rubbish, nonsense, balderdash
