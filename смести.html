смести́ (24626)


Etymology 1

Verb

смести́ • (smestí) pf (imperfective смета́ть)

  1. to brush off, to sweep off, to wipe off
  2. to carry away (by wind, etc.)
  3. (figuratively) to destroy, to dispel, to eliminate
  4. to collect into one place (by raking or sweeping)
Related terms
  • мести́ impf (mestí)
