находи́ть (1303)


Etymology

на- (na-) + | ходи́ть (xodítʹ)

Verb

находи́ть • (naxodítʹ) impf (perfective найти́)

  1. to find
  2. (figuratively) to think, to consider
  3. to come acrosś
  4. to happen to have

Related terms

  • ходи́ть (xodítʹ)
