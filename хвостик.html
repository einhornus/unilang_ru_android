хво́стик (8628)


Etymology

хвост (xvost) + | -ик (-ik)

Noun

хво́стик • (xvóstik) m inan (genitive хво́стика, nominative plural хво́стики, genitive plural хво́стиков)

  1. Diminutive of хвост (xvost): (small) tail
  2. (colloquial) an additional amount
    ему́ пятьдеся́т лет с хво́стиком ― jemú pjatʹdesját let s xvóstikom ― he is on the shady side of fifty
    сто рубле́й с хво́стиком ― sto rubléj s xvóstikom ― one hundred roubles odd
  3. squiggle (in writing), tail, end, hook (e.g. of the comma)
  4. pigtail, ponytail
