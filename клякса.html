кля́кса (13314)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Klecks.

Noun[edit]

кля́кса • (kljáksa) | f | inan (genitive кля́ксы, nominative plural кля́ксы, genitive plural кля́кс)

  1. blot (a blemish, spot or stain made by a coloured substance)
