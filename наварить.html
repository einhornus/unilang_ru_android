навари́ть (24798)


Etymology[edit]

на- (na-) + | вари́ть (varítʹ)

Verb[edit]

навари́ть • (navarítʹ) | pf (imperfective наваривать)

  1. to cook (some amount of food)
  2. (metallurgy) to boil (a quantity of steel) in an open-hearth furnace
  3. to lengthen by welding
  4. to weld on
  5. to profit
