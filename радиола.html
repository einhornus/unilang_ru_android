радио́ла (18295)


Etymology 1

Originally a trademark of the RCA Corporation.

Noun

радио́ла • (radióla) f inan (genitive радио́лы, nominative plural радио́лы, genitive plural радио́л)

  1. radiogram (combination radio and record player)

Etymology 2

Noun

радио́ла • (radióla) f inan (genitive радио́лы, nominative plural радио́лы, genitive plural радио́л)

  1. allseed flax (Radiola linoides, syn. Linum radiola)
