записа́ть (1845)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) + | писа́ть (pisátʹ)

Verb[edit]

записа́ть • (zapisátʹ) | pf (imperfective запи́сывать)

  1. to write down, to enter (to set something down in writing)
  2. to record (to make a record of information; to make an audio or video recording of)
  3. to enroll, to register (to enlist (someone) or make (someone) a member of)
  4. (computing, | rare) to save (to write a file to a disk)
  5. (computing) to burn (to write a file to an optical disc)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (register): зарегистри́ровать (zaregistrírovatʹ)
  • (save): сохрани́ть (soxranítʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • за́пись (zápisʹ)
  • запи́ска (zapíska)
  • записа́ться (zapisátʹsja), запи́сываться (zapísyvatʹsja)
