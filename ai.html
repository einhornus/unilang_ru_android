ai (3698)


Etymology

Compare Russian ой́ (oj, “ow!”).

Interjection

ái! or aĩ!

  1. ow!, ah!, (expresses pain, fear or surprise)
  2. oi!, hey! (used to attract someone's attention)

Synonyms

  • oi!, ui!
  • oi!, a!
