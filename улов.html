уло́в (15231)


Etymology[edit]

From у- (u-) +‎ -лов (-lov).

Noun[edit]

уло́в • (ulóv) | m | inan (genitive уло́ва, nominative plural уло́вы, genitive plural уло́вов)

  1. catch, take (fish)

Related terms[edit]
