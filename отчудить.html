отчуди́ть (18662)


Etymology[edit]

Synchronically, from от- (ot-) + | чуд- (čud-) + | -ить (-ítʹ). The middle part is cognate with чужо́й (čužój, “another's, alien”), but lacking the Early Proto-Slavic *-jь suffix, suggesting that the term originated in Early Proto Slavic or before.

Verb[edit]

отчуди́ть • (otčudítʹ) | pf (imperfective отчуждать)

  1. to alienate, to expropriate
  2. to estrange
  3. (colloquial, | perfective only) to do something very strange
