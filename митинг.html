ми́тинг (4818)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English meeting.

Noun[edit]

ми́тинг • (míting) | m | inan (genitive ми́тинга, nominative plural ми́тинги, genitive plural ми́тингов)

  1. (politics) rally, mass-meeting (a demonstration; an event where people gather together to protest against a given cause or express solidarity)
  2. (neologism) meeting (gathering among business people to discuss their business)
