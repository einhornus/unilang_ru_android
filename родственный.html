ро́дственный (7858)


Etymology[edit]

родство́ (rodstvó) + | -енный (-ennyj)

Adjective[edit]

ро́дственный • (ródstvennyj)

  1. related
  2. kindred
  3. akin, cognate, kin, consanguineous (of the same kin; related by blood)
  4. consanguine
  5. sister
  6. allied
  7. relational
  8. congenerous
  9. agnate
  10. parental
