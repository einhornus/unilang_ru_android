библиоте́карша (23590)


Etymology[edit]

From библиоте́карь (bibliotékarʹ) + | -ша (-ša).

Noun[edit]

библиоте́карша • (bibliotékarša) | f | anim (genitive библиоте́карши, nominative plural библиоте́карши, genitive plural библиоте́карш, masculine библиоте́карь)

  1. (colloquial) female librarian

Usage notes[edit]

  • Some occupation names work for both males and females. The male form of the term may refer to a male or a female. This is a more colloquial term.
