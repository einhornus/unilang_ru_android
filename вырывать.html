вырыва́ть (9450)


Etymology 1[edit]

вы́рыть (výrytʹ) + | -вать (-vátʹ)

Verb[edit]

вырыва́ть • (vyryvátʹ) | impf (perfective вы́рыть)

  1. to dig out, to dig up, to unearth, to exhume

Etymology 2[edit]

вы- (vý-) + | -рывать (-ryvátʹ)

Verb[edit]

вырыва́ть • (vyryvátʹ) | impf (perfective вы́рвать)

  1. to pull out
  2. to tear out
  3. to snatch out (from hands)
  4. to pull up (a plant)
  5. to eradicate, to extirpate, to root out
  6. (colloquial) to extort, to wring, to wrest
