мота́ться (8778)


Etymology[edit]

мота́ть (motátʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

мота́ться • (motátʹsja) | impf (perfective мотнуться)

  1. (colloquial) to dangle
  2. (colloquial)to fuss/rush about
  3. idiomatic:
    мота́ться как бобик ― motátʹsja kak bóbik ― to run in circles; run around like a chicken with its head cut off
  4. (colloquial) to go frequently, to make frequent trips/visits (to a place)
  5. passive of мота́ть (motátʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • мотальный (motálʹnyj)
