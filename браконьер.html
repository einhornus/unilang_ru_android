браконье́р (26450)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French braconnier.

Noun[edit]

браконье́р • (brakonʹjér) | m | anim (genitive браконье́ра, nominative plural браконье́ры, genitive plural браконье́ров)

  1. poacher (a person who trespasses in order to take game illegally)

Related terms[edit]

  • браконьерство (brakonʹjerstvo)
  • браконьерствовать (brakonʹjerstvovatʹ)
