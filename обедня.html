обе́дня (31392)


Etymology[edit]

From обед (obéd, “lunch”) + | -ня (-nja), presumably due to its occurrence around lunchtime.

Noun[edit]

обе́дня • (obédnja) | f | inan (genitive обе́дни, nominative plural обе́дни, genitive plural обе́ден)

  1. (religion) mass, liturgy
