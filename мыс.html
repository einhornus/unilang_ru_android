мыс (9113)


Etymology[edit]

Attested first from the year 1651, of unknown provenance. Also found as Belarusian мыс (mys), Ukrainian мис (mys), Czech mys, Slovak mys.

Noun[edit]

мыс • (mys) | m | inan (genitive мы́са, nominative plural мы́сы or мы́сы, genitive plural мы́сов or мы́сов)

  1. cape, promontory
    мыс До́брой Наде́жды
    mys Dobroj Nadeždy
    Cape of Good Hope

Related terms[edit]

  • мысик | m (mýsik)
  • мысок | m (mysók)
