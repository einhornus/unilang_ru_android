нока́ут (25314)


Etymology

Borrowed from English knockout.

Noun

нока́ут • (nokáut) m inan (genitive нока́ута, nominative plural нока́уты, genitive plural нока́утов)

  1. (sports) knockout

Related terms

  • нокаутировать (nokautirovatʹ)
