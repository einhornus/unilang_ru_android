отбро́сить (4050)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | бро́сить (brósitʹ)

Verb[edit]

отбро́сить • (otbrósitʹ) | pf (imperfective отбра́сывать)

  1. to throw away, to throw aside
  2. to make retreat (army)
  3. to move back, to move to the previous position
  4. to cast (a shadow)
