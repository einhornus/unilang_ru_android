чре́сла (32416)


Etymology

From Old Church Slavonic чрѣсла (črěsla), from Proto-Slavic *čerslo. Displaced the native East Slavic form че́ресла (čéresla).

Noun

чре́сла • (črésla) n inan pl (genitive чре́сл, plural only)

  1. (archaic, anatomy) reins, small of the back

Synonyms

  • поясни́ца (pojasníca), бёдра (bjódra)
  • че́ресла (čéresla, dialectal)
