ве́ять (7525)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic вѣꙗти (vějati), from Proto-Slavic *vějati, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *wḗˀtei, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂weh₁-.

Verb[edit]

ве́ять • (véjatʹ) | impf

  1. (intransitive) to blow gently
  2. (intransitive) to wave, to flutter
  3. (transitive) to winnow (grain)

Related terms[edit]

  • суховей (suxovéj)
  • тиховейный (tixovéjnyj)
  • ветер (véter)
