внучка (16820)


Etymology 1[edit]

внук (vnuk) + | -ка (-ka), from Old East Slavic вънукъ (vŭnukŭ), from Proto-Slavic *vъnukъ

Noun[edit]

внучка • (vnúčka) | f | anim (genitive внучки, nominative plural внучки, genitive plural вну́чек, masculine внук)

  1. granddaughter
Related terms[edit]
  • внук | m (vnuk)

Noun[edit]

внучка • (vnučká) | m | anim

  2. accusative singular of внучо́к (vnučók)
