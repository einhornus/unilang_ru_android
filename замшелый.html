замше́лый (16822)


Etymology[edit]

замшеть (zamšétʹ, “to become mossy”) + | -лый (-lyj)

Adjective[edit]

замше́лый • (zamšélyj)

  1. mossy, moss-grown
  2. (figuratively) moss-grown, old-fashioned; set in one's ways

Related terms[edit]

  • мох (mox)
