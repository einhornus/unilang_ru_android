дуэ́ль (8032)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French duel.

Noun[edit]

дуэ́ль • (duélʹ) | f | inan (genitive дуэ́ли, nominative plural дуэ́ли, genitive plural дуэ́лей)

  1. duel (combat between two persons, often over a matter of honor)

Related terms[edit]

  • дуэльный (duelʹnyj)
  • дуэлянт (dueljant)
  • дуэлист (duelist)
