негде (6458)


Etymology[edit]

не- (ne-) + | где (gde)

Adverb[edit]

негде • (négde)

  1. there is no place to (negative of где)
    Негде сесть (or: Мне негде сесть) ― Négde sestʹ (or: Mne négde sestʹ) ― There is nowhere for me to sit.
  2. (archaic, | stilted) somewhere
    Synonym: где-то (gdé-to)

Usage notes[edit]

  • Put the noun or pronoun in the dative case and use the infinitive of a verb.
