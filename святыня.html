святы́ня (15395)


Etymology[edit]

свято́й (svjatój) + | -ыня (-ýnja)

Noun[edit]

святы́ня • (svjatýnja) | f | inan (genitive святы́ни, nominative plural святы́ни, genitive plural святы́нь)

  1. shrine
  2. sacred object, something considered sacred, a sanctity

Related terms[edit]

  • свято́й (svjatój), свяще́нный (svjaščénnyj)
