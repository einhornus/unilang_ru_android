телеса́ (32403)


Etymology[edit]

From the Old Church Slavonic plural of тѣло (tělo).

Noun[edit]

телеса́ • (telesá) | n | inan | pl (genitive теле́с, plural only)

  1. (colloquial, | humorous, | ironic) the body of a fat person
