гул (3623)


Etymology

Compare semantically analogous Svan გულ (gul).

Noun

гул • (gul) m inan (genitive гу́ла, nominative plural гу́лы, genitive plural гу́лов)

  1. boom, rumble, rumbling, hum, buzz, din
