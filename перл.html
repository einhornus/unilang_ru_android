перл (20445)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps borrowed from German Perl.

Noun[edit]

перл • (perl) | m | inan (genitive перла, nominative plural пе́рлы, genitive plural пе́рлов)

  1. (dated) pearl
  2. (printing) pearl
  3. (ironical) malapropism, howler
    пе́рлы прему́дрости ― pérly premúdrosti ― "pearls of wisdom" (only ironic)
    бюрократи́ческие пе́рлы ― bjurokratíčeskije pérly ― bureaucratic gobbledygook
  4. (computing) Perl
