покоро́бить (22189)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) + | коро́бить (koróbitʹ)

Verb[edit]

покоро́бить • (pokoróbitʹ) | pf (imperfective коро́бить)

  1. (also | | impersonal) to warp
    доску покоро́било от сы́рости ― dósku pokoróbilo ot sýrosti ― the board was warped from the dampness
  2. (figuratively) to ruin, to disfigure
  3. to grate, to jar
  4. (figuratively, | also | | impersonal) to shock, to disgust

Related terms[edit]

  • коробление (koróblenije)
