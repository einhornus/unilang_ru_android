настоя́ть (9943)


Etymology 1[edit]

Verb[edit]

настоя́ть • (nastojátʹ) | pf (imperfective наста́ивать)

  1. to insist, to persist, to press on

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

настоя́ть • (nastojátʹ) | pf (imperfective наста́ивать)

  1. to draw, to extract, to infuse
