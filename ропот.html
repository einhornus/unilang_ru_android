ро́пот (18702)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic ръпътъ (rŭpŭtŭ), from Proto-Slavic *rъpъtъ, onomatopoeic.

Noun[edit]

ро́пот • (rópot) | m | inan (genitive ро́пота, nominative plural ро́поты, genitive plural ро́потов)

  1. murmur, grumble, growl

Related terms[edit]

  • ропта́ть (roptátʹ)
