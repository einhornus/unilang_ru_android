зава́ривать (19192)


Etymology[edit]

завари́ть (zavarítʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

зава́ривать • (zavárivatʹ) | impf (perfective завари́ть)

  1. to brew, to infuse, to steep, (tee, coffee etc) to make
  2. to pour boiling water (over), to scald
  3. to weld up, to close (up)
  4. (colloquial) to start
    зава́ривать ка́шу ― zavárivatʹ kášu ― to stir up trouble; make a mess
