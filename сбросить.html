сбро́сить (2787)


Etymology[edit]

с- (s-) + | бро́сить (brósitʹ)

Verb[edit]

сбро́сить • (sbrósitʹ) | pf (imperfective сбра́сывать)

  1. to throw down, to drop
  2. (a government) to throw off
  3. (skin, leaves) to shed
  4. (clothes, shoes) to throw off

Related terms[edit]

  • броса́ть (brosátʹ)
  • сброс (sbros)
