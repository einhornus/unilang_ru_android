при́сказка (23751)


Noun

при́сказка • (prískazka) f inan (genitive при́сказки, nominative plural при́сказки, genitive plural при́сказок)

  1. (story-teller's) prelude, introduction
  2. (interesting or funny) saying, expression; proverb, adage
