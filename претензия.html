прете́нзия (4964)


Noun

прете́нзия • (pretɛ́nzija, preténzija) f inan (genitive прете́нзии, nominative plural прете́нзии, genitive plural прете́нзий)

  1. claim, pretension, title (demand for one's rights, or for someone/something one thinks they have the right to).
  2. complaint, dissatisfaction.
  3. (law) formal complaint of a creditor/purchaser/customer expressing protest to a debtor/supplier/contractor on account of a contract violation.
  4. pretense (an attempt to impress others by showing off some positive attributes).

Synonyms

  • реклама́ция (reklamácija)
  • жа́лоба (žáloba)
  • амби́ция (ambícija)
