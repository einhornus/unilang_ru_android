льнуть (26660)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *lьnǫti.

Verb[edit]

льнуть • (lʹnutʹ) | impf (perfective прильну́ть)

  1. to cling (to), to stick (to)
  2. (imperfective only, | figuratively) to cultivate (someone), to fawn on
  3. (imperfective only, | figuratively) to feel drawn towards
