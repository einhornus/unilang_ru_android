чудно (13724)


Etymology 1

Adverb

чу́дно • (čúdno)

  1. wonderfully, beautifully

Predicative

чу́дно • (čúdno)

  1. it is marvellous, it is wonderful, it is lovely

Adjective

чу́дно • (čúdno)

  1. short neuter singular of чу́дный (čúdnyj)

Etymology 2

Adjective

чудно́ • (čudnó)
