спя́тить (14106)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from с- (s-) + | пятить (pjátitʹ)

Verb[edit]

спя́тить • (spjátitʹ) | pf

  1. (colloquial) to go balmy, to go off one's rocker
    ты спя́тил! ― ty spjátil! ― you must be mad/crazy!; you're out of your mind!

Synonyms[edit]

  • сойти́ с ума́ (sojtí s umá), свихну́ться (svixnútʹsja)
