наверну́ться (24249)


Etymology[edit]

наверну́ть (navernútʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

наверну́ться • (navernútʹsja) | pf (imperfective навёртываться)

  1. (intransitive) to wind (на (na) + accusative, around something)
  2. (intransitive) to screw (на (na) + accusative, onto something)
  3. to appear (of tears in the eyes)
  4. (colloquial) to turn up, to appear unexpectedly (somewhere)
  5. passive of наверну́ть (navernútʹ)
