маринова́ть (12345)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from French marin(er) + | -овать (-ovátʹ).

Verb[edit]

маринова́ть • (marinovátʹ) | impf (perfective замариновать)

  1. to marinate, to pickle
  2. (colloquial) to delay, to shelve, to put off, to keep waiting, to keep, to make languish

Related terms[edit]

  • маринованный (marinóvannyj)
  • марина́д | m (marinád)
