лине́йка (8356)


Noun[edit]

лине́йка • (linéjka) | f | inan (genitive лине́йки, nominative plural лине́йки, genitive plural лине́ек)

  1. ruler (for measuring or drawing straight lines)
  2. line (for writing)
    писа́ть по лине́йкам ― pisátʹ po linéjkam ― to write on the lines
  3. (polygraphy) rule
    наборная лине́йка ― nabórnaja linéjka ― setting rule
  4. line (formation)
