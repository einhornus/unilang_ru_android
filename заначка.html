Noun[edit]

зана́чка • (zanáčka) | f | inan (genitive зана́чки, nominative plural зана́чки, genitive plural зана́чек)

  1. (low | | colloquial) stash (criminal or otherwise)
  2. (colloquial) nest egg
  3. (criminal | | slang) spike shoved into a scale to reduce weight
