шо́рох (4936)


Etymology 1

Onomatopoeic.

Noun

шо́рох • (šórox) m inan (genitive шо́роха, nominative plural шо́рохи, genitive plural шо́рохов)

  1. rustle (soft, crackling sound)

Etymology 2

May be the same as 1. Cognate to Ukrainian ше́рех (šérex, “grease ice”).

Noun

шо́рох • (šórox) m inan (genitive шо́роха, nominative plural шо́рохи, genitive plural шо́рохов)

  1. (dialectal) rough surface
  2. (dialectal) thin broken ice; grease ice, pancake ice
Synonyms
  • (grease ice): са́ло (sálo)
