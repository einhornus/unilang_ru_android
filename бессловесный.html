бесслове́сный (19503)


Etymology[edit]

From бес- (bes-) + | словеса́ (slovesá) + | -ный (-nyj). Synchronically, based on словеса́ (slovesá, “words, verbiage”). Diachronically, словес- (sloves-) is the original stem of сло́во (slóvo, “word”), which was originally an s-stem. Compare слове́сный (slovésnyj).

Adjective[edit]

бесслове́сный • (besslovésnyj)

  1. mute (unable to speak)
    бесслове́сное живо́тное ― besslovésnoje živótnoje ― mute animal
  2. silent
    бесслове́сная роль ― besslovésnaja rolʹ ― silent role
  3. wordless
  4. meek, humble, lowly (especially in being unwilling to express one's opinion)
