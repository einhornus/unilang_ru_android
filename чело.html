чело́ (13433)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *čelo, from Proto-Indo-European *kelh₃os.

Noun[edit]

чело́ • (čeló) | n | inan (genitive чела, nominative plural чела, genitive plural чёл)

  1. (archaic, | anatomy) forehead, brow
    Synonym: лоб (lob) (regular term)
