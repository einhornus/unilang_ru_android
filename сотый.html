со́тый (11040)


Etymology[edit]

  • Vasmer: from Proto-Slavic *sъtъ.
  • Černyx: adjective со́тый and derivatives are later, in dictionaries since 1704.

Adjective[edit]

со́тый • (sótyj)

  1. hundredth (ordinal)

Related terms[edit]

  • сто (sto)
  • со́тня (sótnja)
