страж (7302)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic стражь (stražĭ), from Proto-Slavic *storžь (“guard”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *storʔg-. Doublet of сторож (stórož).

Noun[edit]

страж • (straž) | m | anim (genitive стра́жа, nominative plural стра́жи, genitive plural стра́жей)

  1. guardian
  2. sentinel (a sentry or guard)
  3. custodian

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

страж • (straž) | f | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of стра́жа (stráža)
