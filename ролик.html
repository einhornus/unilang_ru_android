ро́лик (12454)


Etymology[edit]

рол (rol) + | -ик (-ik)

Noun[edit]

ро́лик • (rólik) | m | inan (genitive ро́лика, nominative plural ро́лики, genitive plural ро́ликов, related adjective роликовый)

  1. roller, roll (that which rolls)
  2. bowl
  3. pulley, block
  4. sheave
  5. trundle
  6. caster
  7. (plural only) (colloquial) in-line skates
  8. video clip
