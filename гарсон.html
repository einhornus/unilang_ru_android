гарсо́н (25677)


Etymology

From French garçon.

Noun

гарсо́н • (garsón) m anim (genitive гарсо́на, nominative plural гарсо́ны, genitive plural гарсо́нов)

  1. (chiefly French context) waiter, garçon, boy
