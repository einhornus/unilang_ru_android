шевелю́ра (12593)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French chevelure.

Noun[edit]

шевелю́ра • (ševeljúra) | f | inan (genitive шевелю́ры, nominative plural шевелю́ры, genitive plural шевелю́р)

  1. the hair on one's head
