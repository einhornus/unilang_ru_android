арти́ст (1294)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French artiste. First attested in 1775 (Can this(+) etymology be sourced?).

Noun[edit]

арти́ст • (artíst) | m | anim (genitive арти́ста, nominative plural арти́сты, genitive plural арти́стов, feminine арти́стка)

  1. artist, actor, performer

Related terms[edit]

  • артисти́ческий (artistíčeskij)
  • артисти́чный (artističnyj)
