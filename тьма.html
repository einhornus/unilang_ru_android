тьма (2088)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tьma.

Noun[edit]

тьма • (tʹma) | f | inan (genitive тьмы, nominative plural тьмы, genitive plural те́м)

  1. dark, darkness
Synonyms[edit]
  • темнота́ | f (temnotá), те́мень | f (témenʹ)
Related terms[edit]
  • тёмный (tjómnyj)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed in Old Russian from an ancestor of Mongolian түм (tüm).

Noun[edit]

тьма • (tʹma) | f | inan (genitive тьмы, nominative plural тьмы, genitive plural те́м)

  1. thousands, a multitude, a host, lots of
