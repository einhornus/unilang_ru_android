принести́ (641)


Etymology

при- (pri-) + | нести́ (nestí).

Verb

принести́ • (prinestí) pf (imperfective приноси́ть)

  1. to bring (by foot), to fetch ((transitive) to transport toward somebody/somewhere)
  2. to yield, to bear
  3. to offer (apologies, thanks, etc.)

Related terms

  • прино́с m (prinós)
