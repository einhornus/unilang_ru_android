лесни́чий (29459)


Etymology 1

лесни́к (lesník) + | -ий (-ij, adjectival suffix)

Noun

лесни́чий • (lesníčij) m anim (genitive лесни́чего, nominative plural лесни́чие, genitive plural лесни́чих)

  1. woodsman, forest ranger, chief forester
Usage notes
  • This noun does not decline the same way as the relational adjective, and is derived from an etymologically different suffix.

Etymology 2

лесни́к (lesník) + | -ий (-ij, possessive suffix)

Adjective

лесни́чий • (lesníčij)

  1. (relational) woodsman's, forest ranger's

Related terms

  • лесни́к (lesník)
  • лес (les), лесно́й (lesnój), лесо́к (lesók)
  • леси́стый (lesístyj)
  • лесни́на (lesnína)
