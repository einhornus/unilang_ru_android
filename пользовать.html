по́льзовать (25440)


Etymology[edit]

по́льза (pólʹza) + | -овать (-ovatʹ)

Verb[edit]

по́льзовать • (pólʹzovatʹ) | impf (perfective попользовать)

  1. (literary | or | dated) to benefit
  2. to use, to exploit
  3. (literary | or | dated) to treat (of a doctor and patient)
