кото́рый (31)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *kotorъ, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷóteros.

Pronoun

кото́рый • (kotóryj) m (interrogative)

  1. (interrogative) which?
    • кото́рую кни́гу вы хоти́те? ― Kotóruju knígu vy xotíte? ― Which book do you want?
    • кото́рый час? ― Kotóryj čas? ― What time is it?
  2. (relative pronoun) which, that, who
    • Я хочу́ кни́гу, кото́рая лежи́т на столе́. ― Ja xočú knígu, kotóraja ležít na stolé. ― I want the book that is on the table.

Usage notes

  • The instrumental кото́рою (kotóroju) is significantly less common than its shortened form кото́рой (kotóroj), and overuse of кото́рою (kotóroju) is likely to be felt archaic or dated.
