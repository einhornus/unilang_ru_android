ко́рмчий (31284)


Etymology[edit]

From корма (kormá) + | -чий (-čij); the helm or rudder was usually in the stern of the ship. Compare корми́ло (kormílo, “helm, rudder”).

Noun[edit]

ко́рмчий • (kórmčij) | m | anim (genitive ко́рмчего, nominative plural ко́рмчие, genitive plural ко́рмчих, feminine кормчая)

  1. (dated, | nautical) helmsman (he who is responsible for steering a ship)
  2. (stilted, | figuratively) helmsman, leader (of a country)
