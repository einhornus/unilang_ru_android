dirty

Прилага́тельное.

Значе́ние

  1. гря́зный, запачканый
    Despite a walk in the rain, my shoes weren't too dirty.
  2. неприли́чный, непристо́йный, са́льный
    At the reception, Uncle Nick got drunk and told dirty jokes to the bridesmaids.
  3. бесче́стный, посты́дный
    He might have scored, but it was a dirty trick that won him the penalty.
  4. незако́нный, нелега́льный
    I won't accept your dirty money!

Сино́нимы

  1. filthy, soiled, sordid, unclean, unwashed
  2. indecent, lewd, obscene, raunchy, salacious
  3. cheating, foul, unsporting, unsportsmanlike
