панихи́да (17359)


Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek παννυχίδα (pannukhída), from Ancient Greek παννυχίς (pannukhís, “night-festival, vigil”).

Noun[edit]

панихи́да • (panixída) | f | inan (genitive панихи́ды, nominative plural панихи́ды, genitive plural панихи́д)

  1. funeral service

Related terms[edit]

  • панихидный (panixídnyj)
