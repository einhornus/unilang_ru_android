чувя́к (29111)


Noun

чувя́к • (čuvják) m inan (genitive чувя́ка, nominative plural чувя́ки, genitive plural чувя́ков or чувя́к)

  1. (regional) soft shoe without heels (in the Crimea and the Caucasus)
