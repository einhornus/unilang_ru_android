чувя́к (29109)


Noun[edit]

чувя́к • (čuvják) | m | inan (genitive чувя́ка, nominative plural чувя́ки, genitive plural чувяков or чувя́к)

  1. (regional) soft shoe without heels (in the Crimea and the Caucasus)
