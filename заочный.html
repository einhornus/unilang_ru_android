зао́чный (16151)


Adjective

зао́чный • (zaóčnyj)

  1. (done or said) in somebody's absence; (relational) absentee
  2. by correspondence, remote (e.g. of education), extramural

Related terms

  • зао́чник m (zaóčnik)
