administrator (2434)


Etymology

From administrō (“attend upoń, assist”), from ad- (“to”) + | ministrō (“attend, manage”).

Noun

administrātor m (genitive administrātōris); third declension

  1. manager, conductor, administrator

Related terms

  • administer
  • administrātiō
  • administrātīvus
  • administrō
