пурпурный (23658)


Etymology[edit]

пурпур (púrpur, “Tyrian purple, crimson”) + | -ный (-nyj); cognate with English purple.

Adjective[edit]

пурпурный or пурпурный • (púrpurnyj or purpúrnyj)

  1. (relational, | historical) Tyrian purple (relating to the dye)
    пурпурная мастерска́я ― púrpurnaja masterskája ― Tyrian purple dyer's workshop
  2. crimson, purple
  3. magenta (e.g. in the CMYK color model)
  4. (chemistry) purpuric
