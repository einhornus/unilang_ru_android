усво́ить (7876)


Etymology[edit]

у- (u-) + | свой (svoj) + | -ить (-itʹ)

Verb[edit]

усво́ить • (usvóitʹ) | pf (imperfective усва́ивать)

  1. to master, to learn
    Мы с ма́лых лет усво́или э́ти назва́ния.
    My s mályx let usvóili éti nazvánija.
    We since [our] earliest years have been extracting ownership of these [words'] meanings.
  2. to adopt (a habit or custom)
  3. to digest, to assimilate
