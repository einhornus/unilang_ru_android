impute


_v.
1 вменять (обыкн. в вину, редк. в заслу́гу) (to) His enemies tried to impute some unfortunate remarks to the politician, who denied having said them.
2 припи́сывать кому-л., относи́ть на чей-л. счет (to) Some politicians impute the rise in crime to the greater freedom being enjoyed by young people. _Syn: attribute
