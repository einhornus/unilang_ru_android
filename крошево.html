кро́шево (21482)


Noun

кро́шево • (króševo) n inan (genitive кро́шева, nominative plural кро́шева, genitive plural кро́шев)

  1. hash (chopped food, especially meat and potatoes)
  2. (colloquial) medley (mixture of miscellaneous things)
