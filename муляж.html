муля́ж (24136)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French moulage.

Noun[edit]

муля́ж • (muljáž) | m | inan (genitive муляжа́, nominative plural муляжи́, genitive plural муляже́й)

  1. dummy, full-scale model, decoy, waxwork
  2. anatomical dummy
