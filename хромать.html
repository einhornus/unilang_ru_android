хрома́ть (14185)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from хромо́й (xromój) + | -ать (-átʹ).

Verb[edit]

хрома́ть • (xromátʹ) | impf

  1. to limp, to be lame
    хрома́ть на пра́вую но́гу ― xromátʹ na právuju nógu ― to be lame in the right leg
  2. to be poor, to leave much to be desired, to be far from perfect, to not be up to standard
    у него́ хрома́ет орфография ― u nevó xromájet orfográfija ― his spelling is poor; his spelling leaves much to be desired

Related terms[edit]

  • хромо́й (xromój)
  • хромота (xromotá)
  • хромоногий (xromonógij)
