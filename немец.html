немец (453)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *němьcь (“foreigner, German”), from *němъ (“mute”) (Russian немой (nemój)).

Noun[edit]

немец • (némec) | m | anim (genitive не́мца, nominative plural не́мцы, genitive plural не́мцев, feminine не́мка)

  1. German, German man
  2. (obsolete) foreigner

Related terms[edit]

  • неме́цкий (neméckij)
