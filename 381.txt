<hr><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Verb">Verb</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">guess</strong> (<i>third-person singular simple present</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en 3 | s | pres-form-of" lang="en">guesses</b>, <i>present participle</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en pres | ptcp-form-of" lang="en">guessing</b>, <i>simple past and past participle</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en past | and | past | ptcp-form-of" lang="en">guessed</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>To reach a partly (or totally) unqualified conclusion.</li>
<li>To solve by a correct conjecture; to conjecture rightly.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">He who <b>guesses</b> the riddle shall have the ring.</i></div></dd></dl></li>

<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">That album is quite hard to find, but I <b>guess</b> you could try ordering it online.</i></div></dd></dl>

<hr><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">guess</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">guesses</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>A prediction about the outcome of something, typically made without factual evidence or support.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">If you don't know the answer, take a <b>guess</b>.</i></div></dd></dl>
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>1907</b>,  L. Frank Baum,  <cite><a href="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Ozma_of_Oz/Chapter_12" class="extiw" title="s:Ozma of Oz/Chapter 12">Ozma of Oz</a></cite>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">"But I shall have eleven <b>guesses</b>," answered Ozma. "Surely I ought to guess one object in eleven correctly; and, if I do, I shall rescue one of the royal family and be safe myself. Then the rest of you may attempt it, and soon we shall free all those who are enslaved."</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul>
<dl><dd><span class="nyms synonym"><span class="defdate">Synonyms:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">estimate</span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en">hypothesis</span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en">prediction</span></span></dd></dl></li></ol>
