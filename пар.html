пар (25919)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *parъ, *para.

Noun[edit]

пар • (par) | m | inan (genitive па́ра, nominative plural пары, genitive plural паро́в)

  1. steam, vapor
    конденсация па́ра ― kondɛnsácija pára ― condensation of steam
  2. fallow (fallow land)
