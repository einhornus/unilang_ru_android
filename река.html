река (510)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic рѣка (rěka), from Proto-Slavic *rěka, from Proto-Indo-European *H₃royHkeh₂, from *H₃reyH-, whence English runlet and rivulet and Spanish río.

Noun[edit]

река • (reká) | f | inan (genitive реки, nominative plural реки, genitive plural рек)

  1. river, stream
