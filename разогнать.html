разогна́ть (9298)


Etymology[edit]

разо- (razo-) + | гнать (gnatʹ)

Verb[edit]

разогна́ть • (razognátʹ) | pf (imperfective разгоня́ть)

  1. to disperse, to scatter
  2. (colloquial) to banish, to dismiss (many people)
  3. to disband (an organization)
  4. to cause (a feeling) to pass
  5. to speed up (something)
  6. to race (a vehicle)

Related terms[edit]

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
