отби́ть (5398)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | бить (bitʹ)

Verb[edit]

отби́ть • (otbítʹ) | pf (imperfective отбива́ть)

  1. to beat off (an attack; enemies), to repulse
  2. to knock off, to break off
    отби́ть охо́ту ― otbítʹ oxótu ― to discourage
  3. to parry, to return (a ball)
  4. to take, to snatch away (by force)
  5. to liberate (by force)
  6. to beat, to drum, to strike (a rhythm)
