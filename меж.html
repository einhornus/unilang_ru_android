меж (2546)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Old East Slavic *межу́ (*mežu), from Proto-Slavic *meďu, from Proto-Indo-European *médʰyos. Doublet of ме́жду (méždu), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

Preposition[edit]

меж • (mež) (+ instrumental case, genitive case)

  1. between, among
Synonyms[edit]
  • ме́жду (méždu)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

меж • (mež) | f | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of межа́ (mežá)
