кино (1539)


Noun[edit]

кино • (kinó) | n | inan (indeclinable)

  1. movie, motion picture, film
    звуково́е кино ― zvukovóje kinó ― sound film (as opposed to "silent film")
  2. movies, cinema, the pictures
