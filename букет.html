буке́т (5037)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French bouquet.

Noun[edit]

буке́т • (bukét) | m | inan (genitive буке́та, nominative plural буке́ты, genitive plural буке́тов)

  1. bouquet, bunch of flowers, posy
  2. bouquet, aroma
