ога́рок (8659)


Etymology[edit]

о- (o-) + | го́р(еть) (gor(étʹ)) + | -ок (-ok), with unclear replacement of о with а.

Noun[edit]

ога́рок • (ogárok) | m | inan or m | anim (genitive ога́рка, nominative plural ога́рки, genitive plural ога́рков)

  1. stub, remnant, cinder (of a mostly-burned candle, electrode, etc.)
  2. (low | | colloquial, | animate) weakling, wimp (especially of a teenager)
  3. (low | | colloquial, | derogatory, | animate) rowdy, hooligan, rascal (especially of a teenager)
