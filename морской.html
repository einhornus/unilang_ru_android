морско́й (1308)


Etymology[edit]

мо́ре (móre) + | -ской (-skój), from Proto-Slavic *moře, from Proto-Indo-European *móri, whence English marine and maritime.

Adjective[edit]

морско́й • (morskój)

  1. (relational) sea
    морско́й волк ― morskój volk ― sea dog, old salt
  2. nautical
  3. maritime
  4. naval
    морско́й флот ― morskój flot ― navy
  5. seaside

Related terms[edit]

  • мо́ре (móre)
  • моря́к (morják)
