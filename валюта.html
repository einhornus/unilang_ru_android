валю́та (4657)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Italian valuta (“currency”), from Latin valeō (“to be worth”).

Noun[edit]

валю́та • (valjúta) | f | inan (genitive валю́ты, nominative plural валю́ты, genitive plural валю́т)

  1. currency (money or other item used to facilitate transactions)
    курс валю́т ― kurs valjút ― exchange rate
  2. (specifically) foreign currency

Synonyms[edit]

  • (currency): де́нежная едини́ца (dénežnaja jediníca)

Related terms[edit]

  • валютчик (valjutčik), валютирование (valjutirovanije)
  • валю́тный (valjútnyj)
