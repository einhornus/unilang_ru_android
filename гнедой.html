гнедо́й (19046)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *gnědъ. Cognates include Czech hnědý and Slovak hnedý.

Adjective[edit]

гнедо́й • (gnedój)

  1. chestnut (of a horse)

Noun[edit]

гнедо́й • (gnedój) | m | anim (genitive гнедо́го, nominative plural гнеды́е, genitive plural гнеды́х)

  1. chestnut-colored horse
