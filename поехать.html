пое́хать (492)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) + | е́хать (jéxatʹ)

Verb[edit]

пое́хать • (pojéxatʹ) | pf (imperfective е́хать) (concrete)

  1. to go (in a conveyance or on horseback)
  2. to ride, to drive
    пое́хать за го́род
    pojéxatʹ za górod
    to go to the suburbs of a city by driving (suburbs are described as something "behind" a city)
  3. to travel
    • Я пое́ду на почту.
      Ja pojédu na póčtu.
      I'm going to go to the post office. (concrete)

Usage notes[edit]

Пое́хать and е́хать are concrete verbs. Their counterpart, е́здить, is an abstract verb.
