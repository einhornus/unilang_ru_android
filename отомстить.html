отомсти́ть (6889)


Etymology[edit]

ото- (oto-) + | мстить (mstitʹ)

Verb[edit]

отомсти́ть • (otomstítʹ) | pf (imperfective мстить)

  1. to revenge oneself, to avenge (to take vengeance for)

Usage notes[edit]

  • This verb is intransitive but possesses a past passive participle.
