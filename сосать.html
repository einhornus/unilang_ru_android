соса́ть (7152)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic съсати (sŭsati), from Proto-Slavic *sъsàti.

Verb[edit]

соса́ть • (sosátʹ) | impf (perfective пососать or отсосать)

  1. to suck
  2. (slang, | vulgar) to perform fellatio
    Perfective: отсосать (otsosátʹ)
