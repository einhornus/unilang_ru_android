ребя́та (350)


Noun[edit]

ребя́та • (rebjáta) | m | anim | pl (genitive ребя́т, plural only)

  1. young men, boys, friends, comrades

Noun[edit]

ребя́та • (rebjáta) | m | anim | pl

  1. nominative plural of ребёнок (rebjónok): children, kids, babies

Synonyms[edit]

  • (child, kid, baby): де́ти (déti) (more common)
