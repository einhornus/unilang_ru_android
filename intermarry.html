intermarry


_v.
1 породниться; смеша́ться путём бра́ка (о ра́сах, племена́х) (with) The shipwrecked sailors intermarried with the natives living on the island, forming a healthy mixed group which is still strong today.
2 вступи́ть в брак (о ро́дственниках) (with) Members of some ancient races intermarried with their own sisters.
