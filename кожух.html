кожу́х (10723)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kožuxъ.

Noun[edit]

кожу́х or кожу́х* • (kožúx or kóžux*) | m | inan (genitive кожуха or кожуха*, nominative plural кожухи́ or кожуха*, genitive plural кожухо́в) (* Professional usage.)

  1. sheepskin coat
  2. casing, housing, jacket of a gun, paddle-box, bonnet, boot, box, cabinet, cage, case, cover, encasement, hood, mantle, sheath, shell, shroud, shrouding
