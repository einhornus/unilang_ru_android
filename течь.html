течь (25747)


Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic течи (teči), from Proto-Slavic *teťi, Proto-Indo-European *tekʷ- (“to run, flow”).

Verb[edit]

течь • (téčʹ) | impf (perfective вы́течь or стечь)

  1. to flow, to run, to stream, to move
  2. to leak
Synonyms[edit]
