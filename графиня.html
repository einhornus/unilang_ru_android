графи́ня (7929)


Etymology

From German Grafin, with the ending influenced by the feminine suffix -ы́ня (-ýnja).

Noun

графи́ня • (grafínja) f anim (genitive графи́ни, nominative plural графи́ни, genitive plural графи́нь, masculine граф)

  1. countess

Related terms

  • граф (graf)
  • гра́фство (gráfstvo)
