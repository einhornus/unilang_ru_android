совра́ть (6106)


Etymology[edit]

со- (so-) + | врать (vratʹ)

Verb[edit]

совра́ть • (sovrátʹ) | pf (imperfective врать)

  1. to lie
  2. to make a mistake
  3. to be inaccurate
