увы́ (2430)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic [Term?], ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wai. Cognate with English woe, Latin vae, Lithuanian vaĩ, Sanskrit उवे (uvé), Hittite [script needed] (uwai), Persian وای | (vây).

Interjection[edit]

увы́ • (uvý)

  1. alas!

Междоме́тие; неизменяемое.
