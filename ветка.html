ветка (1921)


Noun[edit]

ветка • (vétka) | f | inan (genitive ве́тки, nominative plural ве́тки, genitive plural ве́ток)

  1. small branch, twig
  2. line, branch line, sideline
  3. thread (in a discussion forum)
