переверну́ть (3783)


Etymology[edit]

пере- (pere-) + | верну́ть (vernútʹ)

Verb[edit]

переверну́ть • (perevernútʹ) | pf (imperfective перевёртывать or перевора́чивать)

  1. to turn around
  2. to overturn, to turn over
  3. to turn (a page)
  4. to turn inside out
  5. to turn upside down
  6. (colloquial) to turn over (while searching, making a mess)
  7. (colloquial) to transform, to radically change
  8. (colloquial) to stun

Related terms[edit]

  • перевертеть (perevertétʹ)
