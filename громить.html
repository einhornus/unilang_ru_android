громи́ть (18295)


Verb[edit]

громи́ть • (gromítʹ) | impf (perfective погромить or разгроми́ть)

  1. to raid, to sack, to loot
  2. to smash up
  3. to inveigh (against), to fulminate (against)
  4. (an enemy) to smash, to rout, to crush, to pound

Related terms[edit]

  • громи́ла (gromíla)
  • погро́м (pogróm)
  • разгро́м (razgróm)
