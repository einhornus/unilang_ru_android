зары́ть (9073)


Etymology

за- (za-) + | рыть (rytʹ)

Verb

зары́ть • (zarýtʹ) pf (imperfective зарыва́ть)

  1. to bury
    зары́ть тала́нт в зе́млю ― zarýtʹ talánt v zémlju ― to let one's talent go to waste (literally, “to bury (one's) talent in the earth”)
    вот где соба́ка зары́та ― vot gde sobáka zarýta ― so that's where the problem lies!; that's where the shoe pinches (literally, “here's where the dog is buried”)
  2. to fill up (with earth), to make (a ditch, pit, etc.) level with the ground
    зары́ть кана́ву ― zarýtʹ kanávu ― to fill up/in a ditch

Synonyms

  • закопа́ть (zakopátʹ)
