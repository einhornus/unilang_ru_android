томи́ть (11625)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic томити (tomiti), from Proto-Slavic *tomiti, from Proto-Indo-European *TēmH-.

Verb[edit]

томи́ть • (tomítʹ) | impf

  1. to weary (with a rather negative intention), to torment, to torture, to languish
  2. (cooking) to stew

Related terms[edit]

  • томле́ние | n (tomlénije)
