ступе́нь (3981)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *stǫpenь.

Noun[edit]

ступе́нь • (stupénʹ) | f | inan (genitive ступе́ни, nominative plural ступе́ни, genitive plural ступе́ней) (musical step, footstep)
ступе́нь • (stupénʹ) | f | inan (genitive ступе́ни, nominative plural ступе́ни, genitive plural ступе́ней) (degree, grade)

  1. (music) step (interval between two contiguous degrees of the scale)
  2. step, footstep, rung
  3. degree, grade, level, phase, stage
