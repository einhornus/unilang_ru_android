сиро́п (16653)


Etymology

Borrowed from French sirop.

Noun

сиро́п • (siróp) m inan (genitive сиро́па, nominative plural сиро́пы, genitive plural сиро́пов)

  1. syrup

Related terms

  • сиропчик (siropčik)
