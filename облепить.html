облепи́ть (9420)


Etymology

об- (ob-) + | лепи́ть (lepítʹ)

Verb

облепи́ть • (oblepítʹ) pf (imperfective облепля́ть)

  1. to stick all over (with), to paste all over (with); to cover (with), to plaster (with)
  2. (colloquial) to besiege, to cling (to); to swarm all over (of flies)

Related terms

  • облепи́ха f (oblepíxa)

об-ле-пи́ть
