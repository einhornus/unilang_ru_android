хе́рес (30259)


Etymology

Borrowed from Spanish jerez.

Noun

хе́рес • (xéres) m inan (genitive хе́реса, nominative plural хе́ресы, genitive plural хе́ресов, related adjective хе́ресный)

  1. sherry (fortified wine)
