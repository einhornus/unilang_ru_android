тяга́ть (27757)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tędzati, *tęgati.

Verb[edit]

тяга́ть • (tjagátʹ) | impf (perfective потягать)

  1. (low | | colloquial) to pull, to drag
  2. (low | | colloquial) to carry around (especially something heavy)
