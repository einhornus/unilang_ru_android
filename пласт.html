пласт (8307)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic пластъ (plastŭ, “thin layer”), from Proto-Slavic *plastъ.

Noun[edit]

пласт • (plast) | m | inan (genitive пласта́, nominative plural пласты́, genitive plural пласто́в)

  1. layer, sheet, stratum
  2. bed (a deposit of ore, coal etc.)

Synonyms[edit]

  • слой (sloj), ряд (rjad)
