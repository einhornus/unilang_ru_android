сосу́д (3530)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic съсудъ (sŭsudŭ), from Proto-Slavic *sъ- + *sǫdъ.

Noun[edit]

сосу́д • (sosúd) | m | inan (genitive сосу́да, nominative plural сосу́ды, genitive plural сосу́дов)

  1. vessel (container)
  2. (anatomy) vessel

Related terms[edit]

  • сосудик | m (sosúdik)
  • сосу́дистый (sosúdistyj)
