сма́хивать (10106)


Etymology[edit]

смахну́ть (smaxnútʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

сма́хивать • (smáxivatʹ) | impf (perfective смахну́ть)

  1. to whisk (away, off), to flap (away, off), to flick away, to brush (off)
    сма́хивать пыль ― smáxivatʹ pylʹ ― to dust; brush the dust off

Related terms[edit]

  • маха́ть | impf (maxátʹ), махну́ть | pf (maxnútʹ)

Verb[edit]

сма́хивать • (smáxivatʹ) | impf

  1. to look like, to resemble, to smack (of)
