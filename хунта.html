ху́нта (16283)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Spanish junta, from Latin iunctus, perfect passive participle of iungō.

Noun[edit]

ху́нта • (xúnta) | f | inan (genitive ху́нты, nominative plural ху́нты, genitive plural ху́нт)

  1. junta
