грана́т (6557)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from German Granat, from Latin pomum granatum (“grainy apple”)

грана́т

Noun[edit]

грана́т • (granát) | m | inan (genitive грана́та, nominative plural грана́ты, genitive plural грана́тов)

  1. pomegranate
  2. garnet
Related terms[edit]
  • гранатовый (granatovyj)
