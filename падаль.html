па́даль (4456)


Noun[edit]

па́даль • (pádalʹ) | f | anim or f | inan (genitive па́дали, nominative plural па́дали, genitive plural па́далей)

  1. (animate) carrion, offal (dead flesh; carcasses)
  2. (colloquial, | derogatory, | animate) an objectionable person

Synonyms[edit]

  • мертвечи́на (mertvečína)
