туши́ть (11100)


Etymology 1

Verb

туши́ть • (tušítʹ) impf (perfective потуши́ть or затуши́ть)

  1. to extinguish, to put out
  2. to turn off (light)
  3. to quench
  4. to suppress

Etymology 2

Verb

туши́ть • (tušítʹ) impf (perfective потуши́ть)

  1. to stew, to braise (in cooking)
