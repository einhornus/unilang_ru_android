близлежа́щий (15237)


Etymology[edit]

First attested in 1766. From близ (bliz) + | лежа́щий (ležáščij), calque of German nahegelegen or nächstliegend.

Adjective[edit]

близлежа́щий • (blizležáščij)

  1. nearby, close by
  2. contiguous, adjacent, neighboring
