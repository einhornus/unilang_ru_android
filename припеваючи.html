припева́ючи (31228)


Verb[edit]

припева́ючи • (pripevájuči)

  1. (dated) present adverbial imperfective participle of припевать (pripevátʹ)

Adverb[edit]

припева́ючи • (pripevájuči)

  1. (colloquial) happily; with clover; as a prince (literally: singing along)
