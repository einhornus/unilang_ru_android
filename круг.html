круг (901)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *krǫgъ.

Noun

круг • (krug) m inan (genitive кру́га, nominative plural круги́, genitive plural круго́в)

  1. circle, ring
  2. sphere, range
  3. orbit
  4. average
  5. slice
  6. (racing) lap
