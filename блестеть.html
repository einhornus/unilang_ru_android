блесте́ть (2513)


Verb[edit]

блесте́ть • (blestétʹ) | impf (perfective блесну́ть)

  1. to shine, to glitter, to sparkle, to flash
  2. (figuratively) to be gifted with, to shine with, to sparkle with (+ instrumental)
    он не бле́щет умо́м ― on ne bléščet umóm ― he is not gifted with intelligence, he is not a genius

Usage notes[edit]

  • блиста́ть (blistátʹ) means the same thing as блесте́ть (blestétʹ) but the latter is also used figuratively. Present tense forms can coincide.

Synonyms[edit]

  • блиста́ть (blistátʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • блеск (blesk)
  • блесна́ (blesná)
  • блёстка (bljóstka)
  • блестя́щий (blestjáščij)
