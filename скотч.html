скотч (22890)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from English Scotch tape, but the meaning has expanded beyond the corresponding meaning in English.

Noun

скотч • (skotč) m inan (genitive ско́тча, nominative plural ско́тчи, genitive plural ско́тчей)

  1. adhesive tape

Etymology 2

Borrowed from English Scotch.

Noun

скотч • (skotč) m inan (genitive ско́тча, nominative plural ско́тчи, genitive plural ско́тчей)
