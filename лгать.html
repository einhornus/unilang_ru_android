лгать (5964)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *lъgati (compare Serbo-Croatian ла̀гати/làgati, Bulgarian лъжа (lǎža)), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lewgʰ-, whence also English lie, German lügen.

Verb[edit]

лгать • (lgatʹ) | impf (perfective солга́ть or налгать)

  1. to lie, to tell lies
    лгать кому-либо в глаза
    lgatʹ komú-libo v glazá
    to lie to someone's face

Synonyms[edit]

  • врать (vratʹ)
