вира́ж (16448)


Etymology

Borrowed from French virage.

Noun

вира́ж • (viráž) m inan (genitive виража́, nominative plural виражи́, genitive plural вираже́й) (bend, turn)
вира́ж • (viráž) m inan (genitive вира́жа, nominative plural вира́жи, genitive plural вира́жей) (chemical solution)

  1. bend, curve
  2. (aviation) banking, turn, horizontal turn
  3. (photography) toning solution
