фы́ркать (11140)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Verb

фы́ркать • (fýrkatʹ) impf (perfective фы́ркнуть)

  1. to snort, to sniff
  2. to sniff scornfully, to scoff, to grouse, to grumble
  3. (colloquial) to chuckle, to chortle, to snicker
