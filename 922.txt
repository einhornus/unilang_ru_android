<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">figuratively</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A mazelike place of dark alleys etc in which it's easy to lose oneself; especially one that may be overcrowded.

<li>A heart-shaped hoe.</li></ol>
