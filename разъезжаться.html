разъезжа́ться (18323)


Etymology[edit]

раз- (raz-) + | -езжать (-jezžátʹ, “to go (by vehicle)”) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

разъезжа́ться • (razʺjezžátʹsja) | impf (perfective разъе́хаться)

  1. to depart, to leave
  2. to disperse
  3. to pass by one another (on a narrow road, etc.)
  4. to pass by and miss one another, to fail to meet
  5. to separate, to break up (of a couple)
  6. (colloquial) to fall apart
