wat (18365)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Thai วัด (wát).

Noun

wat (plural wats)

  1. A Buddhist temple in Southeast Asia.
    There are two wats neaŕ this village.

Etymology 2

Borrowing from Amharic ወጥ (wäṭ).

Noun

wat

  1. A kind of stew or curry eaten in Ethiopia and Eritrea.
