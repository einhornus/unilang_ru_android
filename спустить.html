спусти́ть (3803)


Etymology

с- (s-) + | пусти́ть (pustítʹ)

Verb

спусти́ть • (spustítʹ) pf (imperfective спуска́ть)

  1. to let down, to lower, to sink (to allow to descend)
    Synonym: опусти́ть (opustítʹ)
  2. to launch, to lower (a boat)
  3. to unchain, to unleash, to let loose
  4. (colloquial) to ejaculate (sperm)

Synonyms

  • опусти́ть (opustítʹ)
