каза́ть (20476)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kāzàti.

Verb[edit]

каза́ть • (kazátʹ) | impf (perfective вы́казать)

  1. (dated) to show
  2. (dated) to tell
  3. (dated) to indicate
