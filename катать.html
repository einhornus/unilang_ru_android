ката́ть (11266)


Verb[edit]

ката́ть • (katátʹ) | impf (perfective поката́ть)

  1. to drive (a trolley), to take (somebody) for a drive
  2. to ride (on a bicycle, on a swing, etc.)
  3. to roll (to cause to revolve)

Usage notes[edit]

ката́ть (katátʹ) is in the class of Russian abstract verbs. Its counterparts, кати́ть (katítʹ)/катнуть (katnútʹ) and покати́ть (pokatítʹ), are concrete verbs.
