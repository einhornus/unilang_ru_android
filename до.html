до (50)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *do, from Proto-Indo-European *de, whence English to.

Preposition[edit]

до • (do) (+ genitive case)

  1. before
  2. up to, till, until
  3. with respect to, concerning, regarding
Related terms[edit]
  • до- (do-)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

до • (do) | n | inan (indeclinable)

  1. (musical note) C
    до диез ― dó dijéz/diéz ― C sharp
    бемоль ― dó bemólʹ ― C flat
