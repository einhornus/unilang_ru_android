стыть (14114)


Verb[edit]

стыть • (stytʹ) | impf (perfective осты́ть)

  1. to be getting cool / cold; to be cooling down
  2. to freeze, to be frozen, to be cold
    кровь сты́нет в жи́лах ― krovʹ stýnet v žílax ― one's blood freezes, one's blood runs cold, it makes one's blood curdle

Synonyms[edit]

  • стынуть (stýnutʹ)
  • (to cool down): остыва́ть (ostyvátʹ)
