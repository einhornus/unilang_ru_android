спина́ (301)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Balto-Slavic [Term?], from Proto-Indo-European *spey- (“sharp point”), whence English spine and spike.

Noun[edit]

спина́ • (spiná) | f | inan (genitive спины, nominative plural спины, genitive plural спи́н)

  1. (anatomy) back
