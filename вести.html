вести (304)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *vesti.

Verb[edit]

вести • (vestí) | impf (perfective повести)

  1. to lead, to preside over, to chair
    вести заседа́ние ― vestí zasedánije ― to preside over a meeting
    вести перегово́ры ― vestí peregovóry ― to negotiate, to conduct negotiations
    вести себя́ ― vestí sebjá ― to behave like
    пло́хо себя́ вести ― plóxo sebjá vestí ― to behave poorly
    вести себя́ хорошо́ ― vestí sebjá xorošó ― to acquit oneself well
  2. to drive
Usage notes[edit]

Вести is a concrete verb. Its counterpart, води́ть, is an abstract verb.
