отрыва́ться (5235)


Etymology[edit]

отрыва́ть (otryvátʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

отрыва́ться • (otryvátʹsja) | impf (perfective оторва́ться)

  1. to come off; to tear off; to be torn off
  2. (of an aircraft) to take off
  3. to turn away, to tear oneself away (from work, a book. etc.)
    не отрыва́ясь ― ne otryvájasʹ ― without stopping work; without rest; nonstop
  4. to separate, to disengage (from pursuit)
  5. to lose contact (with someone)
  6. (colloquial) to have a break, to rest from daily routine
  7. passive of отрыва́ть (otryvátʹ)
