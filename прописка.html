пропи́ска (8083)


Etymology[edit]

прописа́ть (propisátʹ, “to register”) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

пропи́ска • (propíska) | f | inan (genitive пропи́ски, nominative plural пропи́ски, genitive plural пропи́сок)

  1. propiska, residence permit, (mandatory) registration (USSR)
  2. the stamp in the Soviet internal passport (serving as a personal ID) with the registered address.
  3. (by extension), place where one lives (as stated in the stamp)

Related terms[edit]

  • прописа́ть (propisátʹ), пропи́сывать (propísyvatʹ)
