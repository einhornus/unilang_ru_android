карто́шка (2012)


Etymology[edit]

From картоха (kartóxa) + | -ка (-ka), a dialectal form of карто́фель (kartófelʹ). | 1 | Recorded since the middle of the 19th century. Synchronically analyzable as карто(фель) (kartó(felʹ)) + | -шка (-ška).

Noun[edit]

карто́шка • (kartóška) | f | inan (genitive карто́шки, nominative plural карто́шки, genitive plural карто́шек)

  1. (colloquial) potato
