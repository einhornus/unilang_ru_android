по́ле (852)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *poľe, from Proto-Indo-European *pleh₂-.

Noun[edit]

по́ле • (póle) | n | inan (genitive поля, nominative plural поля, genitive plural поле́й)

  1. field, ground
  2. (plural) margins
  3. (plural) brim of the hat

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

по́ле • (póle) | m | inan

  1. prepositional singular of пол (pol)

Etymology 3[edit]

Noun[edit]

по́ле • (polé) | f | inan

  1. dative/prepositional singular of по́ла (polá)
