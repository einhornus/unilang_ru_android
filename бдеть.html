бдеть (25437)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic бъдѣти (bŭděti, “to be awake”), from Proto-Slavic *bъděti (“to be awake”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *bud-, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰudʰ.

Verb

бдеть • (bdetʹ) impf

  1. (dated) to be awake, not sleep
    Synonym: бо́дрствовать (bódrstvovatʹ)
  2. (literary, dated) to keep watch, to keep vigil

Related terms

  • блюсти́ (bljustí)
  • бо́дрый (bódryj)
  • буди́ть (budítʹ)
