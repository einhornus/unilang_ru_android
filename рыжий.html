ры́жий (10352)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic рꙑжии (ryžii), from Proto-Slavic *ryďь, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rūdʰ-yo-.

Adjective[edit]

ры́жий • (rýžij) (comparative (по)ры́жее or (по)ры́жей)

  1. red, sorrel
  2. (of hair) red, ginger
  3. (of a person or other thing that has hair) red-haired, red-headed, ginger

Related terms[edit]

► Russian terms derived from the PIE root *h₁rewdʰ-‎ (0 c, 8 e)

Noun[edit]

ры́жий • (rýžij) | m | anim (genitive ры́жего, nominative plural ры́жие, genitive plural ры́жих, feminine ры́жая)

  1. circus clown
