кали́бр (8833)


Noun

кали́бр • (kalíbr) m inan (genitive кали́бра, nominative plural кали́бры, genitive plural кали́бров)

  1. calibre (diameter of the bore of a firearm)
  2. go/no go gauge, plug gauge (tool)
