уре́зать (21439)


Etymology 1[edit]

у- (u-) + | ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Verb[edit]

уре́зать • (urézatʹ) | pf (imperfective уре́зать or урезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Etymology 2[edit]

уре́зать (urézatʹ) + | -ать (-átʹ)

Verb[edit]

уре́зать • (urezátʹ) | impf (perfective уре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Related terms[edit]

  • урез (urez), урезание (urezánije), урезка (urezka), урезывание (urezyvanije)
