тари́ф (10648)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian tariffa, ultimately from Arabic تَعْرِفَة | (taʿrifa).

Noun

тари́ф • (taríf) m inan (genitive тари́фа, nominative plural тари́фы, genitive plural тари́фов, related adjective тари́фный)

  1. tariff, rate, table of rates
