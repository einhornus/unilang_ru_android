ажиота́ж (19845)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French agiotage.

Noun[edit]

ажиота́ж • (ažiotáž) | m | inan (genitive ажиота́жа, nominative plural ажиота́жи, genitive plural ажиота́жей)

  1. stockjobbing, agiotage, rush; stir, hullabaloo
