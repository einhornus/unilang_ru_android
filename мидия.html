ми́дия (18193)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Greek μύδια (mýdia), plural of μύδι (mýdi).

Noun[edit]

ми́дия • (mídija) | f | anim (genitive ми́дии, nominative plural ми́дии, genitive plural ми́дий)

  1. mussel
