благослови́ть (14115)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic благословити (blagosloviti), probably a loan translation of Ancient Greek ἐυλογεῖν (eulogeîn). Synchronically analyzable as бла́го (blágo, “good”) + | -о- (-o-) + | сло́во (slóvo, “word”) + | -ить (-ítʹ).

Verb[edit]

благослови́ть • (blagoslovítʹ) | pf (imperfective благословля́ть)

  1. (religion) to bless
  2. (colloquial) to give one's blessing, to give permission

Related terms[edit]

  • благослове́ние (blagoslovénije)
  • благослове́нный (blagoslovénnyj)
