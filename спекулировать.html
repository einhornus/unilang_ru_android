спекули́ровать (20579)


Etymology

Perhaps from German spekulieren + | -овать (-ovatʹ); see also -и́ровать (-írovatʹ).

Verb

спекули́ровать • (spekulírovatʹ) impf

  1. (financial) to speculate, to profiteer, to gamble (in)
  2. (figuratively) to exploit, to take advantage of
  3. to speculate (on)

Related terms

  • спекуляти́вный (spekuljatívnyj)
  • спекуля́нт (spekuljánt)

спе-ку-ли́-ро-вать
