спа́йка (24568)


Noun[edit]

спа́йка • (spájka) | f | inan (genitive спа́йки, nominative plural спа́йки, genitive plural спа́ек)

  1. adhesion (abnormal union of surface)
  2. joint, seal
  3. (medicine) commissure
