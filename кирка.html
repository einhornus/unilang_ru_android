ки́рка (13372)


Etymology 1[edit]

Unclear. Possibly, from Greek κερκίς (kerkís) or Turkish küräk.

Noun[edit]

ки́рка • (kirká) | f | inan (genitive ки́рки, nominative plural ки́рки or ки́рки, genitive plural ки́рок, related adjective кирковый)

  1. pickaxe

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from German Kirche. Doublet of це́рковь (cerkovʹ).

Noun[edit]

ки́рка • (kírka) | f | inan (genitive ки́рки, nominative plural ки́рки, genitive plural ки́рок)

  1. Lutheran church
