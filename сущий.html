су́щий (8614)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic сꙑ (sy), present active participle of бꙑти (byti).

Participle[edit]

су́щий • (súščij)

  1. present active imperfective participle of быть (bytʹ)

Adjective[edit]

су́щий • (súščij)

  1. mere
  2. sheer, rank
  3. arrant (utter)
  4. very
  5. regular
  6. great
