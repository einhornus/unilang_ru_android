ребяти́шки (4055)


Etymology

ребя́та (rebjáta) + | -и́шки (-íški)

Noun

ребяти́шки • (rebjatíški) n anim pl (genitive ребяти́шек, plural only)

  1. (colloquial) Diminutive of ребя́та (rebjáta): (small) children, kids
  2. (colloquial) endearing form of ребя́та (rebjáta)
  3. (colloquial) group of children, kids

Synonyms

  • дети́шки (detíški)
