тужу́рка (14912)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French toujours + -ка (-ka).

Noun[edit]

тужу́рка • (tužúrka) | f | inan (genitive тужу́рки, nominative plural тужу́рки, genitive plural тужу́рок)

  1. pea jacket, mess jacket
