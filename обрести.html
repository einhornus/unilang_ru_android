обрести́ (3814)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *obrěsti.

Verb

обрести́ • (obrestí) pf (imperfective обрета́ть)

  1. (literary) to gain, to acquire, to be blessed with
    обрести́ поко́й ― obrestí pokój ― to find peace
    обрести́ ве́рных друзе́й ― obrestí vérnyx druzéj ― to find faithful friends

Derived terms
