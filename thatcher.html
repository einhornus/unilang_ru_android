thatcher (10860)


Etymology

thatch + | -er

Noun

thatcher (plural thatchers)

  1. A person who installs thatch as a roofing material.
  2. A device which removes dead grass clippings from a lawn. (Technically a dethatcher, often a lawnmower attachment.)
