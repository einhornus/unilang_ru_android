auditorium (9901)


Etymology

From audiō (“I hear”) + | -tōrium.

Noun

audītōrium n (genitive audītōriī or audītōrī); second declension

  1. (law) A hearing.
  2. A hall or lecture room.
  3. A school.
  4. An audience; an assembled group of listeners.
