тремби́та (27985)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ukrainian трембіта (trembita), from Hungarian trombita, from Italian trombetta, diminutive of tromba.

Noun

тремби́та • (trembíta) f inan (genitive тремби́ты, nominative plural тремби́ты, genitive plural тремби́т)

  1. (music) trembita (an alpine horn made of wood)
