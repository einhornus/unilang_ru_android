навора́чивать (28696)


Etymology[edit]

навороти́ть (navorotítʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

навора́чивать • (navoráčivatʹ) | impf (perfective навороти́ть)

  1. (imperfective only) to wind (something) up, to wrap (something) up circularly
  2. (low | | colloquial) to heap up (a large quantity of something, in a disorderly way)
  3. (colloquial) to overcomplicate (something), to make (something) overly convoluted
  4. (low | | colloquial) to make a mess of (something), to do (something undesirable or erroneous)

Related terms[edit]

  • наворот (navorót), наворотный (navorótnyj)
  • навёртывать | impf (navjórtyvatʹ), наверну́ть | pf (navernútʹ)
  • навёртка (navjórtka)
  • навёртывание (navjórtyvanije)
  • наверчивать | impf (navérčivatʹ), навертеть | pf (navertétʹ)
  • наверчивание (navérčivanije)

Verb[edit]

навора́чивать • (navoráčivatʹ) | impf (perfective наверну́ть)

  1. (low | | colloquial) to eat a lot of (something)
