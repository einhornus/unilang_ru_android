портя́нка (7347)


Noun[edit]

портя́нки (1)

портя́нка • (portjánka) | f | inan or f | anim (genitive портя́нки, nominative plural портя́нки, genitive plural портя́нок)

  1. (inanimate) footwrap, portyanki (a strip of cloth worn around the feet)
  2. (animate, | derogatory, | Russia or Russians) a person trusting the government, believing any propaganda, following leaders

Synonyms[edit]

  • (an over-patriotic Russian/Soviet citizen): сово́к (sovók), ва́тник (vátnik)
