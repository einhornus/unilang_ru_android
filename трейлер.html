тре́йлер (18573)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English trailer.

Noun[edit]

тре́йлер • (trɛjler) | m | inan (genitive тре́йлера, nominative plural тре́йлеры, genitive plural тре́йлеров)

  1. trailer (vehicle towed behind another, used for carrying equipment)
  2. trailer (preview of a film)
