шезло́нг (16092)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French chaise longue.

Noun[edit]

шезло́нг • (šezlóng) | m | inan (genitive шезло́нга, nominative plural шезло́нги, genitive plural шезло́нгов)

  1. chaise longue
  2. deck chair, beach chair (a folding chair with a wooden frame)
