<head> <meta charset='utf-8'> </head> <body><h1> tempo (10450)</h1><br><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p>Borrowed from <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_language" class="extiw" title="w:Italian language">Italian</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="it">tempo</i>, from <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin" class="extiw" title="w:Latin">Latin</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="la">tempus</i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">time</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>. Doublet of <i class="Latn mention" lang="en">tense</i>.
</p>
<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">tempo</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">tempos</b> <i>or</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">tempi</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>A frequency or rate.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">chess</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A move which is part of one's own plan or strategy and forces, e.g. by means of a check or attacking a piece, the opponent to make a move which is not bad but of no use for him (the player gains a tempo, the opponent loses a tempo), or equivalently a player achieves the same result in fewer moves by one approach rather than another.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">bridge</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The timing advantage of being on lead, thus being first to initiate a strategy to develop tricks for one's side.</li>
<li>The timing of a particular event – earlier or later than in an alternative situation (as in chess example)</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">music</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The number of beats per minute in a piece of music; also, an indicative term denoting approximate rate of speed in written music (examples: allegro, andante)</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">cycling</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The steady pace set by the frontmost riders.</li>
<li>A small truck or cargo van with three or four wheels, commonly used for commercial transport and deliveries (particularly in Asian and African countries): a genericized trademark, originally associated with the manufacturer Vidal amp; Sohn Tempo-Werke GmbH.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">American football</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A rapid rate of play by the offense resulting from reducing the amount of time which elapses after one play ends and the next starts.</li></ol>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Usage_notes">Usage notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<p>The plural <i class="Latn mention" lang="en">tempi</i> is only used for the musical sense; all other meanings have the plural <i class="Latn mention" lang="en">tempos</i>.
</p>
