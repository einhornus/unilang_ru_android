ж (291)


Etymology 1[edit]

Usage notes[edit]

Its name is же (že) and it sounds like the s in pleasure.

Etymology 2[edit]

Particle[edit]

ж • (ž)

  1. Apocopic form of же (že)

Etymology 3[edit]

Noun[edit]

ж • (ž) | m | inan (indeclinable)

  1. (grammar) Abbreviation of же́нский род (žénskij rod, “feminine gender”).
Synonyms[edit]
  • ж. (ž.)

Etymology 4[edit]

Noun[edit]

ж • (ž) | f | inan (indeclinable)

  1. Abbreviation of же́нская уборная (žénskaja ubórnaja, “ladies' room, women's toilet”).
Synonyms[edit]
