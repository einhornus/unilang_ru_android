запа́дать (27825)


Etymology 1[edit]

запад- (zapad-) + | -ать (-átʹ)

Verb[edit]

запа́дать • (zapadátʹ) | impf (perfective запа́сть)

  1. to fall back, to fall behind, to sink down
    клавиша запа́дает ― kláviša zapadájet ― the key is stuck; the key won't come up
  2. to sink deeply
    слова его́ запали мне в душу ― slová jevó zapáli mne v dúšu ― his words imprinted themselves in my mind [on my soul]; his words sank home
  3. (slang) to take (to), to fall (for)
Related terms[edit]
  • за́пад (západ)
