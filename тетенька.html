тетенька (18652)


Etymology

тётя (tjótja) + | -енька (-enʹka)

Noun

тётенька • (tjótenʹka) f anim (genitive тётеньки, nominative plural тётеньки, genitive plural тётенек)

  1. endearing form of тётя (tjótja): auntie
  2. (colloquial) granny, auntie (referring to an older or elderly woman); an adult woman (from a child's perspective or as a form of address like "madam" but much less formal)

Synonyms

  • (aunt): тётка (tjótka), тётя (tjótja)
  • (woman): же́нщина (žénščina), ба́ба (bába)

те-тень-ка
