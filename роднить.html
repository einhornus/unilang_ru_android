родни́ть (24642)


Etymology

Perhaps from родня́ (rodnjá) + | -и́ть (-ítʹ).

Verb

родни́ть • (rodnítʹ) impf (perfective сродни́ть or породни́ть)

  1. to make related
    Perfective: породни́ть (porodnítʹ)
  2. to bring closer together
    Perfective: сродни́ть (srodnítʹ)
  3. (imperfective only) to be in common (between), to create an affinity (between)
    подру́г родни́ла любо́вь к му́зыке ― podrúg rodníla ljubóvʹ k múzyke ― their love of music created an affinity between the friends
