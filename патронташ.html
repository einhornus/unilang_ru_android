патронта́ш (25570)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Patrontasche.

Noun

патронта́ш • (patrontáš) m inan (genitive патронта́ша, nominative plural патронта́ши, genitive plural патронта́шей)

  1. (military) bandolier, cartridge/ammunition belt
