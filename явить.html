яви́ть (14234)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic ꙗвити (javiti), from Proto-Slavic *aviti.

Verb

яви́ть • (javítʹ) pf (imperfective явля́ть)

  1. (literary, poetic) to show (to have somebody see something)
