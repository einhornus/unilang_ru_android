помаленьку (12383)


Etymology[edit]

From по (po) + | маленьку (malénʹku), perhaps an obsolete short dative form of маленький (málenʹkij), with stress shift.

Adverb[edit]

помаленьку • (pomalénʹku)

  1. (colloquial) little by little, gradually
  2. (colloquial) so-so, tolerable

Related terms[edit]

  • маленький (málenʹkij)
