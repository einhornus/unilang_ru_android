попи́сать (21515)


Etymology 1[edit]

по- (po-) + | писа́ть (pisátʹ)

Verb[edit]

попи́сать • (popisátʹ) | pf

  1. to write (for some time, for a while), to do some writing
    ничего́ не попишешь ― ničevó ne popíšešʹ ― nothing can be done about it; you can't help it
  2. to paint, to spend some time painting

  • письмо́ (pisʹmó)

Etymology 2[edit]

по- (po-) + | писа́ть (písatʹ)

Verb[edit]

попи́сать • (popísatʹ) | pf (imperfective писа́ть)

  1. to pee, to pee-pee, to tinkle, to wee
