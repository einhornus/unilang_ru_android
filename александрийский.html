александри́йский (32344)


Etymology[edit]

From Александрия (Aleksandríja) + | -ийский (-íjskij) when referring to Alexandria; from Александр (Aleksándr) + | -ийский (-íjskij) when referring to Alexander the Great.

Adjective[edit]

александри́йский • (aleksandríjskij)

  1. Alexandrian (referring to the city of Alexandria in Egypt or to Alexander the Great)
  2. alexandrine (verse, etc.)

Related terms[edit]

  • Александр (Aleksándr), александровский (aleksándrovskij)
  • Александрия (Aleksandríja)
