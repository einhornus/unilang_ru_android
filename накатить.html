накати́ть (18390)


Etymology

на- (na-) + | кати́ть (katítʹ)

Verb

накати́ть • (nakatítʹ) pf (imperfective нака́тывать)

  1. to roll (on, ontó)
  2. (waves) to roll in, to coast in
  3. (weather) to set in, (events) to begin, to break out
  4. (feelings, mood) to come (over), to be seized (with), to have an attack
  5. (alcohol) to drink strong liquor, to especially vodka
  6. (computer) to apply patches, including installation of updates and applying cracks
