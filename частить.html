части́ть (28633)


Etymology

ча́стый (částyj) + | -и́ть (-ítʹ)

Verb

части́ть • (častítʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to do something rapidly/hurriedly
    не части, говори́ ясне́й ― ne časti, govorí jasnéj ― don't gabble/jabber, speak more distinctly
  2. (colloquial, dated) to visit someone frequently, to see much of someone
