дробь (9903)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *drobь. Compare Gothic 𐌲𐌰𐌳𐍂𐌰𐌱𐌰𐌽 (gadraban, “to chisel, to hew out”).

Noun[edit]

дробь • (drobʹ) | f | inan (genitive дроби, nominative plural дроби, genitive plural дробе́й)

  1. (mathematics) fraction, broken number
  2. (weaponry) small-shot, pellets, shot
  3. (music) drum roll
  4. (symbol) slash
