изразе́ц (31933)


Etymology[edit]

Per Vasmer, from из- (iz-) + | ре́зать (rézatʹ, “to cut”) + | -ец (-éc), with unexplained vowel shift in the root. Per Preobrazhensky (quoted by Trubachev), the root is rather рази́ть (razítʹ, “to strike”).

Noun[edit]

изразе́ц • (izrazéc) | m | inan (genitive изразца́, nominative plural изразцы́, genitive plural изразцо́в)

  1. ceramic tile
