Noun

кат • (kat) m anim or m inan (genitive ка́та, nominative plural ка́ты, genitive plural ка́тов)

  1. (historical) executioner
  2. (nautical) cat
    кат-ба́лка ― kat-bálka ― cathead
  3. (botany) khat
  4. (computing) cut (livejournal cut, lt;lj-cutgt;, which allows you to hide all or part of your entry behind a link)
