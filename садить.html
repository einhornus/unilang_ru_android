сади́ть (21634)


Etymology[edit]

Verb[edit]

сади́ть • (sadítʹ) | impf (perfective посади́ть)

  1. to make or let someone sit down, to seat
  2. to plant (e.g. trees or flowers)
  3. (colloquial) to imprison, to put to jail

Synonyms[edit]

  • сажа́ть (sažátʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • сесть (sestʹ)
  • сиде́ть (sidétʹ)
