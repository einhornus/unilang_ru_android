влезть (3539)


Etymology[edit]

в- (v-) + | лезть (leztʹ)

Verb[edit]

влезть • (vleztʹ) | pf (imperfective влеза́ть)

  1. to get into (something, also figurative), to climb into, to scramble into
    ско́лько вле́зет ― skólʹko vlézet ― as much as you like
    влезть в долги ― vleztʹ v dolgí ― get into debts
  2. to fit in, to go in
  3. to meddle
    влезть не в своё дело ― vleztʹ ne v svojó délo ― to poke one's nose into other people's affairs; to pry
    влезть в душу ― vleztʹ v dúšu ― to worm oneself into smb.'s confidence (to get into somebody's soul)
