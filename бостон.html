босто́н (22006)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English Boston.

Noun[edit]

босто́н • (bostón) | m | inan (genitive босто́на, nominative plural босто́ны, genitive plural босто́нов)

  1. high-grade woolen cloth
  2. Boston (old card game)
  3. Boston (type of dance)

Related terms[edit]

  • Босто́н (Bóston)
