старина (5870)


Noun

старина́ • (stariná) f inan (genitive старины́, nominative plural стари́ны, genitive plural стари́н)

  1. olden time, olden days, antiquity
    в старину́ ― v starinú ― in olden times, formerly, in the old days
    тряхну́ть старино́й ― trjaxnútʹ starinój ― to revive a bit of the past, to go back to the good old days
  2. antiquities, antique

Related terms

  • стари́нный (starínnyj)
  • ста́рый (stáryj)

Noun

старина́ • (stariná) m anim (genitive старины́, nominative plural стари́ны, genitive plural стари́н)

  1. (colloquial, address) old man, old boy, old chap, old fellow

Synonyms

  • стари́к (starík)
