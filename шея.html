ше́я (745)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *šija, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *syuh₁-

Noun[edit]

ше́я • (šéja) | f | inan (genitive ше́и, nominative plural ше́и, genitive plural шей)

  1. (anatomy) neck
    сиде́ть на ше́е ― sidétʹ na šéje ― to burden someone
  2. (rare) neck (place, reducted in size) | 1 |
    переше́ек ― perešéjek ― isthmus

Related terms[edit]

  • шейка (šéjka)
  • ше́йный (šéjnyj)
