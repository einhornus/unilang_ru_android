окре́ст (21863)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic окрьстъ (okrĭstŭ), from о- (o-) + | крест (krest), cognate with Old Church Slavonic окрьсть (okrĭstĭ), Bulgarian окръст (ókrǎst), Polabian vuokárst (“circle”).

Adverb[edit]

окре́ст • (okrést)

  1. around, nearby, in the neighborhood

Preposition[edit]

окре́ст • (okrést)

  1. around, near

Synonyms[edit]

  • вокру́г (vokrúg), ря́дом (rjádom), кру́гом (krugom), о́коло (ókolo)
