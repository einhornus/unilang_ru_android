tu (12610)


Etymology

Borrowed from English thou, French tu, German du, Italian tu, Spanish tú, Russian ты (ty), all ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *túh₂ with + | -u.

Pronoun

tu (second person singular)

  1. (informal, familiar) you (singular), thou
    Synonym: vu (formal)

  • tuo
