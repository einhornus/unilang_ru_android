увози́ть (10093)


Etymology

у- (u-) + | вози́ть (vozítʹ)

Verb

увози́ть • (uvozítʹ) impf (perfective увезти́)

  1. to take away, to carry away (by vehicle)
  2. to abduct, to kidnap

Related terms

  • уво́з (uvóz)
