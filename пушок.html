пушо́к (2407)


Etymology[edit]

пух (pux) + | -ок (-ók)

Noun[edit]

пушо́к • (pušók) | m | inan (genitive пушка, nominative plural пушки, genitive plural пушко́в)

  1. down
  2. fluff
  3. bloom (on fruit)

Related terms[edit]

  • пух (pux)
