пушо́к (2409)


Etymology

пух (pux) + | -о́к (-ók)

Noun

пушо́к • (pušók) m inan (genitive пушка́, nominative plural пушки́, genitive plural пушко́в)

  1. down
  2. fluff
  3. bloom (on fruit)

Related terms

  • пух (pux)
