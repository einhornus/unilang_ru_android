фуга́с (25831)


Etymology

Borrowed from French fougasse.

Noun

фуга́с • (fugás) m inan (genitive фуга́са, nominative plural фуга́сы, genitive plural фуга́сов)

  1. field explosive, landmine, fougasse mine

Related terms

  • фуга́сный (fugásnyj)
