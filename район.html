район (785)


Etymology[edit]

First attested in 1865. Borrowed from French rayon, from rai + -on, from Latin radius.

Noun[edit]

район • (rajón) | m | inan (genitive райо́на, nominative plural райо́ны, genitive plural райо́нов)

  1. district
  2. region, area

рай-он | (дореформ. раіонъ)
