се́рвис (10556)


Etymology

Borrowed from English service.

Noun

се́рвис • (sɛ́rvis, sérvis) m inan (genitive се́рвиса, nominative plural се́рвисы, genitive plural се́рвисов, related adjective се́рвисный)

  1. service, hospitality
  2. quality service
  3. (colloquial) service center
  4. (computing, informatics) service, tools
