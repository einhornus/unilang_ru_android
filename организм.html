органи́зм (1378)


Etymology

Borrowed from French organisme.

Noun

органи́зм • (organízm) m inan (genitive органи́зма, nominative plural органи́змы, genitive plural органи́змов)

  1. system of organs making up the body of a living thing
    перееда́ть вре́дно для на́шего органи́зма.
    Perejedátʹ vrédno dlja náševo organízma.
    Overeating is bad for our bodies.
  2. organism (living thing, usually a small and simplistic one)
  3. (figuratively) a complex structure or mechanism
