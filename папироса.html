папиро́са (2906)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Polish papieros, from papier (“paper”) fused with Spanish cigarros.

Noun[edit]

папиро́сы «Беломорканал»

папиро́са • (papirósa) | f | inan (genitive папиро́сы, nominative plural папиро́сы, genitive plural папиро́с)

  1. papirosa (strong Russian cigarette made with dark tobacco, no filter)

Synonyms[edit]

  • сигаре́та (sigaréta) (a usual cigarette)
