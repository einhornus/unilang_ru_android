трюмо́ (22650)


Etymology

Borrowed from French trumeau.

Noun

трюмо́ • (trjumó) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. trumeau (the pillar or center post supporting the lintel in the middle of a doorway)
  2. pier glass, trumeau mirror
