бульо́н (11344)


Etymology

Borrowed from French bouillon.

Noun

бульо́н • (bulʹón) m inan (genitive бульо́на, nominative plural бульо́ны, genitive plural бульо́нов)

  1. broth, bouillon (water in which food (meat or vegetable etc) has been boiled)
  2. clear soup
