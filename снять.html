снять (559)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *sъnęti, from *sъ(n)- (see *sъ(n)) + *ęti. Reanalyzed as с- (s-) + -нять (-njatʹ), from which other words in -нять (-njatʹ) were created, such as наня́ть (nanjátʹ), обня́ть (obnjátʹ).

Verb[edit]

снять • (snjatʹ) | pf (imperfective снима́ть)

  1. to take off, to take away, to take down
  2. to take, to make
  3. to photograph, to film
  4. to rent (e.g. an apartment)
