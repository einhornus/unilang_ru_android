brigadier (15588)


Etymology

Borrowed from French brigadier.

Noun

brigadier (plural brigadiers)

  1. (military) An army rank; an officer commanding a brigade.
  2. (military, Britain) the highest field officer grade, below general officers, NATO grade O7

Synonyms

  • brigadier general
  • senior colonel
