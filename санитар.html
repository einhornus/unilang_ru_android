санита́р (4633)


Noun[edit]

санита́р • (sanitár) | m | anim (genitive санита́ра, nominative plural санита́ры, genitive plural санита́ров, feminine санита́рка)

  1. orderly, hospital attendant, male nurse, corpsman, paramedic, aidman
