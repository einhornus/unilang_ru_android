ора́льный (30114)


Etymology[edit]

Probably from German oral + | -ный (-nyj) or oral (oral) + | French -ный.

Adjective[edit]

ора́льный • (orálʹnyj)

  1. (relational) mouth; oral

Usage notes[edit]

  • The term should not be used for "spoken", e.g. oral task, у́стный (ústnyj) should be used instead.

Synonyms[edit]

  • ротово́й (rotovój)
