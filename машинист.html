машини́ст (7295)


Noun

машини́ст • (mašiníst) m anim (genitive машини́ста, nominative plural машини́сты, genitive plural машини́стов)

  1. machinist, engine driver; engineman

Usage notes

The term машини́стка (mašinístka) doesn't mean "female machinist" but "(female) typist".
