ра́мпа (31675)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French rampe.

Noun[edit]

ра́мпа • (rámpa) | f | inan (genitive ра́мпы, nominative plural ра́мпы, genitive plural ра́мп)

  1. (theater) footlights
  2. (theater) low barrier along the proscenium, hiding the lights
  3. (theater, | dated) low barrier separating a box from the auditorium
  4. (dated) railing
  5. (military, | historical) embrasure, crenel
  6. (technical | or | skateboarding) ramp
  7. apparatus for centralized distribution to consumers of compressed gas cylinders
