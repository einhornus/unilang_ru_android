корпе́ть (25220)


Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from Old Church Slavonic кръпа (krŭpa).

Verb[edit]

корпе́ть • (korpétʹ) | impf

  1. (colloquial) to work hard/meticulously/thoroughly (on something), to pore off, to plug away
    Сего́дня я до́лго корпе́л над но́вой програ́ммой.
    Sevódnja ja dólgo korpél nad nóvoj prográmmoj.
    Today I have plugged away on the new program for a long time.
