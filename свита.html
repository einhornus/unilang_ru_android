сви́та (7099)


Etymology[edit]

Via Polish świta or German Suite from French suite.

Noun[edit]

сви́та • (svíta) | f | inan (genitive сви́ты, nominative plural сви́ты, genitive plural сви́т)

  1. suite, escort, retinue, entourage
  2. (geology) suite
