то́поль (5909)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *topolь.

Noun[edit]

то́поль • (tópolʹ) | m | inan (genitive тополя, nominative plural тополя or тополи*, genitive plural тополе́й or тополе́й*) (* Poetic.)

  1. poplar (any of various deciduous trees of the genus Populus)
  2. cottonwood

Related terms[edit]

  • тополёк (topoljók)
  • тополевый (topolevyj), тополиный (topolinyj)
