мольбе́рт (20619)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Malbrett

Noun

мольбе́рт • (molʹbért) m inan (genitive мольбе́рта, nominative plural мольбе́рты, genitive plural мольбе́ртов)

  1. easel (upright frame for displaying or supporting something)
