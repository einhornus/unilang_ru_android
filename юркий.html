ю́ркий (13118)


Etymology[edit]

From юр (jur, “busy place, market square; whirlpool, strong current”) + | -кий (-kij).

Adjective[edit]

ю́ркий • (júrkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)юрче)

  1. agile, nimble
  2. quick, brisk (of a vehicle)

Related terms[edit]
