о́ный (12340)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *onъ

Determiner[edit]

о́ный • (ónyj)

  1. (high register) that, that very, the aforementioned
  2. (obsolete) that other (as opposed to this)
    во вре́мя оно ― vo vrémja óno ― of yore; in olden days; once a very long time ago
    во времена́ о́ны ― vo vremená óny ― once upon a time; in the old days; once
    во дни о́ны ― vo dní óny ― once upon a time; in olden days; once
  3. (obsolete) otherworldly, unearthly

Synonyms[edit]

  • тот (tot)
