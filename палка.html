па́лка (1909)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *palъka.

Noun[edit]

па́лка • (pálka) | f | inan (genitive па́лки, nominative plural па́лки, genitive plural па́лок)

  1. stick, cane, club
  2. (vulgar) instance of sex, a fuck
