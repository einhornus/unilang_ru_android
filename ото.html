ото (9511)


Preposition[edit]

ото • (oto)

  1. from
  2. since

Usage notes[edit]

This is a variant of от (ot) before words beginning with certain awkward consonant clusters such as мн- (mn-), вс- (vs-), and сн- (sn-).
