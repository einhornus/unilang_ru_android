натаска́ть (17773)


Etymology[edit]

на- (na-) + | таска́ть (taskátʹ)

Verb[edit]

натаска́ть • (nataskátʹ) | pf (imperfective ната́скивать)

  1. to carry or drag (an amount of something, in several batches)
  2. (colloquial) to steal (an amount of something)
  3. to store (an amount of something)
  4. to train (an animal)
  5. (figuratively, | colloquial) to teach superficially, to coach superficially
  6. (figuratively, | colloquial) to gather or extract from several sources

Synonyms[edit]

  • (carry, drag, steal, store): натащить (nataščítʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • тащи́ть | impf (taščítʹ), потащи́ть | pf (potaščítʹ), вы́тащить | pf (výtaščitʹ)
  • таска́ть (taskátʹ), таска́ться (taskátʹsja)
