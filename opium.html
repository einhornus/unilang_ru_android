opium (12420)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ὄπιον (ópion), from ὀπός (opós, “juice of a plant”), from Proto-Indo-European *swokʷos (“juice, resin”).

Noun

opium n (genitive opiī or opī); second declension

  1. opium, poppy-juice
