<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">ale</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">ales</b>)
</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">dated</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A beer made without hops.</li>
<li>A beer produced by so-called <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/warm_fermentation" class="extiw" title="w:warm fermentation">warm fermentation</a> and not pressurized.</li>
<li>A festival in English country places, so called from the liquor drunk.</li></ol>
