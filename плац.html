плац (9158)


Etymology

First attested in 1705 as пляц (pljac). From German Platz via Polish plac, from French place, from Latin platea.

Noun

плац • (plac) m inan (genitive пла́ца, nominative plural пла́цы, genitive plural пла́цев)

  1. parade ground
