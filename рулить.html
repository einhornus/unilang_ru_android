рули́ть (23905)


Etymology[edit]

руль (rulʹ) + | -ить (-ítʹ)

Verb[edit]

рули́ть • (rulítʹ) | impf

  1. (aviation) to taxi
  2. (vernacular) to steer the wheel
  3. (ironic) to be in charge, to be in the driver's seat

Related terms[edit]

  • руль (rulʹ), рулево́й (rulevój)
  • руло́н (rulón)
  • рулет (rulét)
  • руле́тка (rulétka)
