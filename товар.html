това́р (1666)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tovarъ, of Turkic origin; compare Bashkir тауар (tawar, “merchandise, goods”), Turkish davar (“livestock”).

Noun[edit]

това́р • (továr) | m | inan (genitive това́ра, nominative plural това́ры, genitive plural това́ров)

  1. commodity, product, article
  2. (plural) goods, wares

Related terms[edit]

  • това́рный (továrnyj)
  • товарооборо́т | m (tovarooborót)
  • товарообмен (tovaroobmén)
