ржать (11368)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited Old East Slavic ръзати (rŭzati), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *rewǵ-

Verb[edit]

ржать • (ržatʹ) | impf (perfective заржа́ть)

  1. to neigh
    ло́шадь ржёт ― lóšadʹ ržót ― a horse neighs
  2. (figuratively, | low | | colloquial) to laugh
    ржёт! ― ržot! ― lol!

Related terms[edit]

  • ржа́ние (ržánije)
  • ржачка (ržačka)
