отбира́ть (7113)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | -бирать (-birátʹ)

Verb[edit]

отбира́ть • (otbirátʹ) | impf (perfective отобра́ть)

  1. to choose, to select, to single out
  2. to take away

Related terms[edit]

  • отбо́р | m (otbór)
