хво́рост (12725)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic хворостъ (xvorostŭ), from Proto-Slavic *xvorstъ.

Noun[edit]

хво́рост • (xvórost) | m | inan (genitive хво́роста, nominative plural хво́росты, genitive plural хво́ростов)

  1. brushwood
  2. (cooking) straws, twiglets (pastry)

Related terms[edit]

  • хворостина (xvorostína)
  • хворостяной (xvorostjanój)
