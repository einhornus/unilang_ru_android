уны́ние (11267)


Noun

уны́ние • (unýnije) n inan (genitive уны́ния, nominative plural уны́ния, genitive plural уны́ний)

  1. gloom, melancholy, dismay
    • (Can we date this quote?), Alexander Pushkin, “На холмах Грузии | On the Hills of Georgia | ”, in (Please provide the book title or journal name) | [1]:

      Unýnʹja mojevó, ništó ne múčit, ne trevóžit.
      My melancholy, remains untouched and undisturbed.
