пе́сенка (4763)


Etymology[edit]

пе́сня (pésnja) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

пе́сенка • (pésenka) | f | inan (genitive пе́сенки, nominative plural пе́сенки, genitive plural пе́сенок)

  1. Diminutive of пе́сня (pésnja): (little) song
    твоя́ пе́сенка спета ― tvojá pésenka spéta ― you're finished; you're history; you're dead (literally, “your little song is sung”)
