мано́к (22087)


Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

мано́к • (manók) | m | inan (genitive манка́, nominative plural манки́, genitive plural манко́в)

  1. hunter's whistle, call
  2. (figuratively) bait
Related terms[edit]
  • мани́ть (manítʹ)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

мано́к • (mánok) | f | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of манка́ (mánka)
