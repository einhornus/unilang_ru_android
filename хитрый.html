хи́трый (3076)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic хытръ (xytrŭ), from Proto-Slavic *xytrъ. Cognate with Bulgarian хитър (hitǎr, “sly”), Serbo-Croatian хитар/hitar (“hasty”), Slovak and Czech chytrý (“clever”).

Adjective[edit]

хи́трый • (xítryj) (comparative (по)хитрее or (по)хитрей)

  1. sly, cunning, artful
  2. intricate, involved
    Э́то не хитро/хитро. ― Éto ne xítro/xitró. ― That's simple.
    Э́то дело не хи́трое. ― Éto délo ne xítroje. ― It is very simple; A child could do it.
  3. skilful, resourceful
