поплы́ть (4476)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) + | плыть (plytʹ)

Verb[edit]

поплы́ть • (poplýtʹ) | pf (imperfective плыть)

  1. to start swimming, to start floating
    Он поплы́л в сто́рону маяка́.
    On poplýl v stóronu majaká.
    He started swimming towards the lighthouse.
  2. to start sailing

Usage notes[edit]

  • This is a concrete verb. Its abstract counterpart is попла́вать (poplávatʹ).
