поде́ржанный (7086)


Participle

поде́ржанный • (podéržannyj)

  1. past passive perfective participle of подержа́ть (poderžátʹ)

Adjective

поде́ржанный • (podéržannyj)

  1. used, secondhand (that has or have previously been owned by someone else)

Synonyms

  • б/у (b/u)
