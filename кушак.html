куша́к (27239)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic кушакъ (kušakŭ), from Crimean Tatar куша́к or Turkish kuşak

Noun[edit]

куша́к • (kušák) | m | inan (genitive кушака́, nominative plural кушаки́, genitive plural кушако́в)

  1. belt, girdle, sash
