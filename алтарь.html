алта́рь (11256)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin altāre (often attested in plural form altāria).

Noun[edit]

алта́рь • (altárʹ) | m | inan (genitive алтаря́, nominative plural алтари́, genitive plural алтаре́й)

  1. altar
    Synonym: жертвенник (žértvennik)
