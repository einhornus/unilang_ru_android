футбо́л (5657)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English football.

Noun[edit]

футбо́л • (futból) | m | inan (genitive футбо́ла, nominative plural футбо́лы, genitive plural футбо́лов)

  1. football (the game, UK English), soccer (US English)
