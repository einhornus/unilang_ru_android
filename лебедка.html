лебедка (16037)


Etymology[edit]

ле́бедь (lébedʹ) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

лебёдка • (lebjódka) | f | anim or f | inan (genitive лебёдки, nominative plural лебёдки, genitive plural лебёдок)

  1. (animate) pen (female swan)
  2. (inanimate) winch
