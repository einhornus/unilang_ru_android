<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">music</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A musical form, <a href = vnd.unilang:поп.html>popular</a> before the time of the sonata, consisting of a string or series of pieces all in the same key, mostly in various dance rhythms, with sometimes an elaborate prelude.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">music</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> An excerpt of instrumental music from a larger work that contains other elements besides the music; for example, the <i>Nutcracker Suite</i> is the music (but not the dancing) from the ballet <i>The Nutcracker</i>, and the <i>Carmen Suite</i> is the instrumental music (but not the singing and dancing) from the opera <i>Carmen</i>.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">computing</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A group of related computer programs distributed together.</li></ol>
