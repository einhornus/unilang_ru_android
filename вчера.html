вчера́ (536)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *vьčera, related to Russian вечер (véčer, “evening”).

Adverb[edit]

вчера́ • (včerá)

  1. yesterday

Noun[edit]

вчера́ • (včerá) | n | inan (indeclinable)

  1. yesterday

Related terms[edit]

  • вчера́шний (včerášnij)
  • позавчера́ (pozavčerá)
