вчера́ (538)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *vьčera, related to Russian ве́чер (véčer, “evening”).

Adverb

вчера́ • (včerá)

  1. yesterday

Noun

вчера́ • (včerá) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. yesterday

Related terms

  • вчера́шний (včerášnij)
  • позавчера́ (pozavčerá)
