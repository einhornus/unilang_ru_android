врата́ (17222)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic врата́ (vrata), from Proto-Slavic *vorta. Doublet of воро́та (voróta), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun

врата́ • (vratá) n inan pl (genitive врат, plural only)

  1. (archaic or poetic) gate, gates

Synonyms

  • воро́та n pl (voróta)

Derived terms

  • врата́рь (vratárʹ)

вра-та́
