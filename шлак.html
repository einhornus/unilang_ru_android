шлак (19762)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Schlacke.

Noun

шлак • (šlak) m inan (genitive шла́ка, nominative plural шла́ки, genitive plural шла́ков)

  1. slag, dross, cinder, scoria
  2. (usually plural) bad stuff accumulated in the body
  3. (colloquial) something unnecessary or useless
