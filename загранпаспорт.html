загранпаспорт (32010)


Etymology[edit]

Acronym of | заграни́чный па́спорт (zagraníčnyj pásport), literally "foreign passport" or "passport for abroad".

Noun[edit]

загранпаспорт • (zagranpásport) | m | inan (genitive загранпаспорта, nominative plural загранпаспорта, genitive plural загранпаспортов)

  1. passport (for traveling)

Usage notes[edit]

  • In Russia, there are two distinct types of passports: заграни́чный па́спорт (zagraníčnyj pásport) (i.e. foreign passport) and вну́тренний па́спорт (vnútrennij pásport) (i.e. internal or domestic passport). The former is used for traveling abroad and the latter is a standard ID.
