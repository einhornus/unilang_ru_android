мохна́тый (6123)


Etymology[edit]

From obsolete мохна (moxná, “bundle of hair or feathers”) + -атый (-átyj).

Adjective[edit]

мохна́тый • (moxnátyj)

  1. hairy, furry, wooly
  2. shaggy, unkempt

Related terms[edit]

  • мох (mox)
