тра́хнуть (14856)


Etymology

трах (trax) + | -нуть (-nutʹ)

Verb

тра́хнуть • (tráxnutʹ) pf

  1. (vernacular) to bang, to boom
    тра́хнули пушки ― tráxnuli puški ― the guns banged/boomed
  2. (vernacular) to crash, to bang
    тра́хнуть кого́-либо по голове́ ― tráxnutʹ kovó-libo po golové ― to bang smb on the head
    тра́хнуть кулако́м по столу́ ― tráxnutʹ kulakóm po stolú ― to bang one's fist on the table; bring one's fist down on the table
  3. (vulgar) to lay, to bang, to screw

Related terms

  • трах (trax)
