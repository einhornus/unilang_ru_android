appendix (3220)


Etymology

From appendō (“hang upoń”).

Noun

appendix f (genitive appendicis); third declension

  1. supplement, addition
  2. appendage
  3. barberry (shrub)

Related terms

  • appendicium
  • addendum
  • appendō
  • appensor
  • appensus
