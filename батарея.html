батаре́я (1907)


Etymology[edit]

From French batterie, possibly via German Batterie.

Noun[edit]

батаре́я • (bataréja) | f | inan (genitive батаре́и, nominative plural батаре́и, genitive plural батаре́й)

  1. (electrical) battery
  2. (artillery) battery
  3. (heating) radiator
