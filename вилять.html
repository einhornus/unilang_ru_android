виля́ть (10980)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *viljati, imperfective of *viliti.

Verb[edit]

виля́ть • (viljátʹ) | impf (perfective вильну́ть)

  1. to wag
  2. to twist, to zigzag, to make sharp turns, (river) to meander
  3. (colloquial) to prevaricate, to be evasive
