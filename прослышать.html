прослы́шать (20132)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + | слы́шать (slýšatʹ)

Verb

прослы́шать • (proslýšatʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to learn about, to hear about (something) from rumors (+ accusative or о (o) + prepositional or про (pro) + accusative)
