волшба́ (31904)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic вълшьба (vŭlšĭba), from Proto-Slavic *vъlšьba, from *vъlšiti (compare Old Church Slavonic влъснѫти (vlŭsnǫti)).

Noun

волшба́ • (volšbá) f inan (genitive волшбы́, uncountable)

  1. (obsolete) magic, sorcery

Derived terms

  • волше́бный (volšébnyj)
  • волшебство́ (volšebstvó)

Related terms

  • волхв (volxv)
