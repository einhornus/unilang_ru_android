подстегну́ть (30439)


Etymology[edit]

под- (pod-) + | стегну́ть (stegnútʹ)

Verb[edit]

подстегну́ть • (podstegnútʹ) | pf (imperfective подстёгивать)

  1. (colloquial) to fasten, to clip (something to something, usually from below or from the side)
  2. to whip on, to drive forward by whipping
  3. (colloquial, | figuratively) to spur, to motivate to action
  4. (colloquial) to intensify; to whet (one's appetite)

Related terms[edit]

  • стега́ть | impf (stegátʹ), стегну́ть | pf (stegnútʹ), стегануть | pf (steganútʹ)
  • стегание (stegánije)
  • стежок (stežók)
