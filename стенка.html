сте́нка (1754)


Etymology

стена́ (stená) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun

сте́нка • (sténka) f inan (genitive сте́нки, nominative plural сте́нки, genitive plural сте́нок)

  1. Diminutive of стена́ (stená, “wall”)
  2. side (of a container)
  3. (anatomy) wall
  4. (furniture) wall unit
  5. (soccer) defending players who form a line to block the attacking team's free kick

Related terms

  • поста́вить к сте́нке | (colloquial) to execute, especially by shooting
  • стена́ (stená)
  • шкаф (škaf)
