лететь (1019)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic летѣти (letěti), from Proto-Slavic *letěti

Verb[edit]

лететь • (letétʹ) | impf (perfective полете́ть)

  1. to fly, to be flying

Usage notes[edit]

лететь (letétʹ) and полете́ть (poletétʹ) are Russian concrete verbs. Their counterparts, лета́ть (letátʹ) and полета́ть (poletátʹ), are abstract verbs.
