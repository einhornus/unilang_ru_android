ток (3255)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tokъ.

Noun[edit]

ток • (tok) | m | inan (genitive то́ка, nominative plural то́ки, genitive plural то́ков)

  1. (water, | electricity) current
Synonyms[edit]
  • пото́к (potók)
  • тече́ние (tečénije)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

ток • (tok) | m | inan (genitive то́ка, nominative plural то́ка, genitive plural то́ков)

  1. (agriculture) threshing floor

Etymology 3[edit]

Noun[edit]

ток • (tok) | m | inan (genitive то́ка, nominative plural то́ка, genitive plural то́ков)

  1. mating place (of birds)
Related terms[edit]
  • токовать | impf (tokovátʹ)
  • токовище | n (tokovíšče)

Etymology 4[edit]

Borrowed from French toque.

Noun[edit]

ток • (tok) | m | inan (genitive то́ка, nominative plural то́ки, genitive plural то́ков)

  1. toque
