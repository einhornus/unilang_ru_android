сверчо́к (14196)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic свьрчькъ (svĭrčĭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *svьrčь.

Noun[edit]

сверчо́к • (sverčók) | m | anim (genitive сверчка́, nominative plural сверчки́, genitive plural сверчко́в)

  1. cricket (insect)
  2. grass warbler (Locustella) (bird)
