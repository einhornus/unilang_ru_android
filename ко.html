ко (326)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kъ(n).

Preposition[edit]

ко • (ko) (+ dative case)

  1. to, towards
    ко мне ― ko mné ― to me
  2. (time): by, for
    ко вто́рнику ― ko vtórniku ― by Tuesday

Usage notes[edit]

This is a variant form of к (k) used before words beginning with awkward consonant clusters such as вс- (vs-), вт- (vt-), мн- (mn-), сн- (sn-).
