продува́ть (19972)


Etymology

проду́ть (prodútʹ) + | -ва́ть (-vátʹ)

Verb

продува́ть • (produvátʹ) impf (perfective проду́ть)

  1. to clean by blowing, to blow clean
  2. (impersonal) to cause to catch a cold from the wind
    его́ продува́ет сквозняко́м ― jevó produvájet skvoznjakóm ― he is catching a cold from the draft
  3. (figuratively, colloquial) to lose (in sports or gambling)
  4. (also impersonal) to blow through
    ветеро́к продува́л ко́мнату ― veterók produvál kómnatu ― the breeze was blowing through the room
    ко́мнату продува́ло ветерко́м ― kómnatu produválo veterkóm ― the breeze was blowing through the room

Related terms

  • дуть impf (dutʹ), поду́ть pf (podútʹ)
  • ду́тый (dútyj)
  • дутьё (dutʹjó)
