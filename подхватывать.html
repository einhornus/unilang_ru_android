подхва́тывать (9560)


Etymology[edit]

подхвати́ть (podxvatítʹ) + | -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Verb[edit]

подхва́тывать • (podxvátyvatʹ) | impf (perfective подхвати́ть)

  1. to catch (up), to pick up, to grasp, to snatch
  2. (colloquial) to catch, to pick up (a disease)
  3. to pick up (an expression, a song)
  4. to join in, to take up, to catch up
