<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">historical</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The holder of a secular office, formally subordinate to, but <i>de facto</i> the power behind the throne of, the caliph.</li>

<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">card games</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A variant of solitaire, played with two decks of cards.</li>
