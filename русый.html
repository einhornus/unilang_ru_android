ру́сый (7407)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic русъ (rusŭ), from Proto-Slavic *rusъ, from earlier *rudsъ, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rowdʰ-s-o-.

Adjective[edit]

ру́сый • (rúsyj)

  1. fair(-haired), blond(e), light brown (of hair), dirty blond
