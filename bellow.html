Глаго́л

Значе́ние

  1. (о живо́тных) мыча́ть, реве́ть
    • Countrymen, butchers, drovers, hawkers, boys, thieves, idlers, and vagabonds of every low grade, were mingled together in a mass; the whistling of drovers, the barking dogs, the bellowing and plunging of the oxen, the bleating of sheep, the grunting and squeaking of pigs, the cries of hawkers, the shouts, oaths, and quarrelling on all sides.
  2. (о челове́ке) ора́ть; вопи́ть, рыча́ть
    • On every piece of waste or common ground, some small gambler drove his noisy trade, and bellowed to the idle passersby to stop and try their chance.
  3. (о буре) бушева́ть, реве́ть, шуме́ть
