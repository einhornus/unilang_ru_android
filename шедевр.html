шеде́вр (7909)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chef-d'œuvre.

Noun

шеде́вр • (šedɛ́vr) m inan (genitive шеде́вра, nominative plural шеде́вры, genitive plural шеде́вров)

  1. masterpiece (piece of work that has been given much critical praise)

Related terms

  • шедевра́льный (šedɛvrálʹnyj)
