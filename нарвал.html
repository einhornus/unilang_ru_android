нарва́л (29597)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from English narwhal.

Noun[edit]

нарва́л • (narvál) | m | anim (genitive нарва́ла, nominative plural нарва́лы, genitive plural нарва́лов)

  1. narwhal

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

нарва́л • (narvál)
