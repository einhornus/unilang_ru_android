сейча́с (96)


Etymology[edit]

Univerbation of сей (sej) + час (čas), ”this hour”, ”this time” (note: “сей” is dated, bookish or stilted (unlike “этот”), “час” in the sense of “time” is dated or poetic).

Adverb[edit]

сейча́с • (sejčás)

  1. now, at present
  2. presently, immediately, at once
  3. just now

Synonyms[edit]

  • тепе́рь (tepérʹ)
