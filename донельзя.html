доне́льзя (17123)


Etymology[edit]

From до- (do-) + | нельзя́ (nelʹzjá), with stress shift.

Adverb[edit]

доне́льзя • (donélʹzja)

  1. (colloquial) totally, utterly, completely

Related terms[edit]

  • нельзя́ (nelʹzjá)
