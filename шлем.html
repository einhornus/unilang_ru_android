шлем (4160)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic шлѣмъ (šlěmŭ), from Proto-Slavic *šelmъ. Doublet of шелом (šelóm), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun[edit]

шлем • (šlem) | m | inan (genitive шле́ма, nominative plural шле́мы, genitive plural шле́мов)

  1. helmet
    Synonyms: шишак (šišák), ка́ска (káska)
  2. headpiece (as in a horse's)

Related terms[edit]

  • подшлемник (podšlemnik)
  • шлемофон (šlemofon)
