ча́йка (7720)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *čajьka, from onomatopoeia of cry *kē.

Noun[edit]

ча́йка • (čájka) | f | anim (genitive ча́йки, nominative plural ча́йки, genitive plural чаек)

  1. seagull

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

ча́йка • (čajká) | m | inan

  1. genitive singular of чаёк (čajók)
