жиро́к (25453)


Etymology[edit]

жир (žir) + | -ок (-ók)

Noun[edit]

жиро́к • (žirók) | m | inan (genitive жирка́, nominative plural жирки́, genitive plural жирко́в)

  1. (colloquial) Diminutive of жир (žir): small fat deposit in a tissue
  2. (colloquial) reserve, stock, surplus

  • жировик (žirovík)
