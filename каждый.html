ка́ждый (116)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic къжьдо (kŭžĭdo), from Proto-Slavic *kъžьdo

Adjective

ка́ждый • (káždyj)

  1. every, each
  2. either (of two)
  3. everybody, everyone

Related terms

► Russian terms derived from the PIE root *kʷe-‎ (0 c, 11 e)

ка́ж-дый
