нала́дить (5726)


Etymology

на- (na-) + | ла́дить (láditʹ)

Verb

нала́дить • (naláditʹ) pf (imperfective нала́живать)

  1. to adjust, to repair, to set right
  2. (colloquial) to organize, to arrange, to establish
    нала́дить дела́ ― naláditʹ delá ― to set things going
  3. (colloquial) to normalize, to smooth (relations)
    нала́дить сотру́дничество ― naláditʹ sotrúdničestvo ― to enter into cooperation
    нала́дить хоро́шие отноше́ния ― naláditʹ xoróšije otnošénija ― to establish good relations; to get along well
  4. (colloquial) to tune, to adjust to a certain harmony (of a musical instrument, song, etc.)
  5. (low colloquial) to drive away
  6. (low colloquial) to keep talking about, to harp on (perfective only)

Related terms

  • нала́дка (naládka), нала́дчик (naládčik)
