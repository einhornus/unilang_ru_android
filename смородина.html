сморо́дина (17461)


Etymology[edit]

From смород (smórod, “foul smell”) + | -ина (-ina), because of the smell of its leaves. For the semantics compare German Stinkbaum.

Noun[edit]

сморо́дина • (smoródina) | f | inan (genitive сморо́дины, nominative plural сморо́дины, genitive plural сморо́дин)

  1. currant (berry and plant)

Related terms[edit]

  • смерде́ть (smerdétʹ)
  • смрад (smrad)
