пристав (14121)


Etymology 1

Noun

при́став • (prístav) m anim (genitive при́става, nominative plural пристава́ or при́ставы, genitive plural приставо́в or при́ставов)

  1. (historical) the local police chief or constable in Pre-Revolutionary Russia
  2. (historical) bailiff, in Moscow in historical times

Etymology 2

Noun

приста́в • (pristáv) m inan (genitive приста́ва, nominative plural приста́вы, genitive plural приста́вов)

  1. (anatomy) in animals, the shape of the connection between the head or neck and the breast

Etymology 3

Participle

приста́в • (pristáv)

  1. short past adverbial perfective participle of приста́ть (pristátʹ)
