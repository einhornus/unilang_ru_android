тя́жко (7404)


Etymology[edit]

тя́жкий (tjážkij) + | -о (-o)

Adverb[edit]

тя́жко • (tjážko)

  1. sorely, gravely, heavily
  2. with difficulty

Adjective[edit]

тя́жко • (tjážko)

  1. short neuter singular of тя́жкий (tjážkij)
