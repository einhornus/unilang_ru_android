пи́кник (12210)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French pique-nique, English picnic

Noun[edit]

пи́кник • (pikník) | m | inan (genitive пи́кника, nominative plural пи́кники, genitive plural пи́кников)

  1. picnic (a meal eaten outdoors)
