бузина́ (21297)


Etymology[edit]

From earlier буз (buz), боз (boz), from Proto-Slavic *bъzъ.

Noun[edit]

бузина́ • (buziná) | f | inan (genitive бузины́, uncountable)

  1. elder (tree)
  2. elderberry
