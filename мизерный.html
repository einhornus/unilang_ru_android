мизерный (16923)


Etymology[edit]

French misère + | -ный (-nyj)

Usage notes[edit]

Sometimes pronounced as мизерный.

Adjective[edit]

мизерный • (mizérnyj)

  1. measly, scanty
  2. wretched, miserable
