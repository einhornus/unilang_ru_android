цари́ть (5540)


Etymology[edit]

From царь (carʹ).

Verb[edit]

цари́ть • (carítʹ) | impf (perfective воцарить)

  1. (dated) to be a tsar (monarch), to reign = ца́рствовать (cárstvovatʹ)
  2. to reign, to dominate, to prevail
