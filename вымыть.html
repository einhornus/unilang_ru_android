вы́мыть (5736)


Etymology[edit]

  • вы- (vý-) + | мыть (mytʹ)

Verb[edit]

вы́мыть • (výmytʹ) | pf (imperfective мыть or вымывать)

  1. (transitive) to wash, to wash out, to wash up, to wash away, to eluate

Related terms[edit]

  • See мыть (mytʹ) for related terms.
