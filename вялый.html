вялый (6277)


Adjective

вя́лый • (vjályj) (comparative (по)вя́лее or (по)вя́лей)

  1. withered, faded
  2. flabby, flaccid, limp
  3. sluggish, listless, languid, lethargic

Related terms

  • вя́нуть (vjánutʹ)
