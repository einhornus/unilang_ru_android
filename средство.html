сре́дство (496)


Etymology[edit]

среда́ (sredá) + | -ство (-stvo)

Noun[edit]

сре́дство • (srédstvo) | n | inan (genitive сре́дства, nominative plural сре́дства, genitive plural сре́дств)

  1. mean, facility
  2. tool, instrument
  3. remedy
  4. (pl.) means (money)
