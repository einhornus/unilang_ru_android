шах (5953)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Persian شاه | (šâh).

Noun[edit]

шах • (šax) | m | anim or m | inan (genitive ша́ха, nominative plural ша́хи, genitive plural ша́хов)

  1. shah
  2. (chess) check (Russian notation: +)
