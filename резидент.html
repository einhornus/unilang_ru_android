резиде́нт (11969)


Noun[edit]

резиде́нт • (rezidént) | m | anim (genitive резиде́нта, nominative plural резиде́нты, genitive plural резиде́нтов, feminine резидентка)

  1. (literary) resident
  2. (espionage) fixed-post spy; resident spy, resident, "rezident", chief of a country's intelligence operations in another country

Related terms[edit]
