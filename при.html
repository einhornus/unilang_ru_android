при (87)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *pri, from Proto-Indo-European *prey, whence English for and per.

Preposition[edit]

при • (pri) (+ locative case or prepositional case)

  1. in the presence of
  2. in the time of
  3. at, by

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

при • (pri)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of переть (perétʹ)

Морфологические и синтаксические свойства
