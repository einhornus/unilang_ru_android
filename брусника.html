брусни́ка (19579)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *brusьnica, from *brusьnъ + *-ica. Cognate with Czech brusnice, Slovak brusnica, Polish brusznica, Serbo-Croatian brùsnica, Slovene brusníca, Bulgarian брусница (brusníca), and more distantly with Lithuanian brùknė and Latvian brūklene.

Noun[edit]

брусни́ка • (brusníka) | f | inan (genitive брусни́ки, nominative plural брусни́ки, genitive plural брусни́к)

  1. cowberry, red whortleberry, lingonberry
