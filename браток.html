брато́к (10925)


Etymology[edit]

Noun[edit]

брато́к • (bratók) | m | anim (genitive братка́, nominative plural братки́, genitive plural братко́в)

  1. (colloquial) brother, man, bro
  2. (colloquial) mobster

Synonyms[edit]

Related terms[edit]
