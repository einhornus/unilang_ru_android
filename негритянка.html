негритя́нка (32113)


Noun[edit]

негритя́нка • (negritjánka) | f | anim (genitive негритя́нки, nominative plural негритя́нки, genitive plural негритя́нок, masculine негр)

  1. a black woman; Negress

Usage notes[edit]

  • The usage of the word "негр (negr)" or "негритя́нка (negritjánka)" in Russian is generally not considered pejorative. It is a neutral word in Russian, and is not used derogatively.
  • Due to Western influence, for political correctness some people avoid the term.

Related terms[edit]

  • негр (negr, noun) - black man
  • негритёнок (negritjónok) - little black boy
  • негритя́нский (negritjánskij, adjective)
  • негритос (negritós), негритоска (negritóska, noun) (offensive)
