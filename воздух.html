воздух (343)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic въздоухъ (vŭzduxŭ).

Noun

во́здух • (vózdux) m inan (genitive во́здуха, nominative plural *во́здухи, genitive plural *во́здухов)

  1. (usually uncountable) air
  2. (figuratively) air, atmosphere
  3. (criminal slang) money

Related terms

  • возду́шный (vozdúšnyj)
  • воздухопла́вание (vozduxoplávanije)
  • воздухопла́ватель (vozduxoplávatelʹ)

Noun

во́здух • (vózdux) m inan (genitive во́здуха, nominative plural во́здухи, genitive plural во́здухов)

  1. (religion) the veil for church communion vessels
