то́нкий (765)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic тънъкъ (tŭnŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tьnъkъ.

Adjective[edit]

то́нкий • (tónkij) (comparative (по)тоньше, superlative тонча́йший)

  1. thin
  2. slim, slender
  3. small, fine, delicate
  4. fine, high-quality (of food, wine, etc.)
  5. subtle
  6. keen
  7. high (voice)
