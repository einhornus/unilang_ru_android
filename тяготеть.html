тяготе́ть (19348)


Etymology

тя́гота (tjágota) + | -е́ть (-étʹ)

Verb

тяготе́ть • (tjagotétʹ) impf

  1. to be strongly attracted, to have a propensity/bent, to be drawn, to be strongly attached
  2. to hang (over), to weigh (upoń), to threaten
    над ним тяготе́ет стра́шное обвине́ние
    nad nim tjagotéjet strášnoje obvinénije
    he lies under a terrible accusation; a terrible accusation hangs over him

Related terms

  • тяготе́ние (tjagoténije), тя́га (tjága)
