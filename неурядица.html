неуря́дица (22023)


Noun[edit]

неуря́дица • (neurjádica) | f | inan (genitive неуря́дицы, nominative plural неуря́дицы, genitive plural неуря́диц)

  1. confusion, disorder, mess
  2. (usually plural) squabbling, arguing
