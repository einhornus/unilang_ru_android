dragon (4594)


Etymology

From Old French dragon, from Latin dracōnem, accusative of dracō, from Ancient Greek δράκων (drákōn). Doublet of drac.

Noun

dragon m (plural dragons, feminine dragonne)

  1. a dragon, creature or person
  2. a dragoon
