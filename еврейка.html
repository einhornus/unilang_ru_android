евре́йка (10975)


Etymology

евре́й (jevréj) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun

евре́йка • (jevréjka) f anim (genitive евре́йки, nominative plural евре́йки, genitive plural евре́ек, masculine евре́й)

  1. a female Jew, Jewess, Hebrew woman

Usage notes

  • The word евре́йка is used to refer to any female of Jewish ancestry, regardless of religious beliefs. When specifically referring to a follower of Judaism, the word иуде́йка (iudéjka) is used.

Related terms

  • евре́й (jevréj)
  • евре́йский (jevréjskij)
  • евре́йство (jevréjstvo)
  • евре́йчик (jevréjčik)
  • евре́ечка (jevréječka)
