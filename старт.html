старт (7531)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English start.

Noun[edit]

старт • (start) | m | inan (genitive ста́рта, nominative plural ста́рты, genitive plural ста́ртов)

  1. start (beginning point of a distance race or a process)
  2. launchpad

Synonyms[edit]

  • за́пуск (zápusk)
