мех (5988)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *měxъ. Cognates include Ukrainian міх (mix), Belarusian мех (mjex), Old Church Slavonic мѣхъ (měxŭ), Serbo-Croatian мијех/mijeh, Polish miech, Czech měch.

Noun[edit]

мех • (mex) | m | inan (genitive меха, nominative plural меха, genitive plural мехо́в)

  1. fur
Related terms[edit]
  • мехово́й (mexovój)
  • меховщик (mexovščík)
