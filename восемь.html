восемь (1165)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *osmь, from Proto-Indo-European *oḱtṓw.

Numeral[edit]

восемь • (vósemʹ)

  1. eight (8)

Usage notes[edit]

восемь (vosemʹ) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • восемь ма́льчиков ― vósemʹ málʹčikov ― eight boys
  • с восемью ма́льчиками ― s vosemʹjú málʹčikami ― with eight boys
  • ма́льчиков восемь ― málʹčikov vósemʹ ― approximately eight boys
  • пятьдеся́т восемь кни́г ― pjatʹdesját vósemʹ knig ― fifty-eight books
