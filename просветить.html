просветить (17280)


Etymology 1[edit]

про- (pro-) + | свети́ть (svetítʹ)

Verb[edit]

просветить • (prosvetítʹ) | pf (imperfective просве́чивать)

  1. to shine light through (something) (e.g. using an X-ray or backlight)
  2. to shine for an amount of time (e.g. of the sun)
Related terms[edit]
  • просве́т (prosvét)
  • просвечивание (prosvéčivanije)

Etymology 2[edit]

A borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

Verb[edit]

просветить • (prosvetítʹ) | pf (imperfective просвеща́ть)

  1. to enlighten, to educate, to illuminate
Related terms[edit]
  • просвети́тель (prosvetítelʹ)
  • просвещение (prosveščénije)
