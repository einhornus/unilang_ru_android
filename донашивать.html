дона́шивать (32353)


Etymology[edit]

доноси́ть (donosítʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

дона́шивать • (donášivatʹ) | impf (perfective доноси́ть)

  1. to finish carrying
  2. to wear out
  3. to carry (a child) to full term (in a pregnancy)

Related terms[edit]

  • недоно́сок (nedonósok)
