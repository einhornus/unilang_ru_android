отши́б (17788)


Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

отши́б • (otšíb) | m | inan (genitive отши́ба, nominative plural отши́бы, genitive plural отши́бов)

  1. (colloquial) remoteness, apartness, usually in combination with на (na):
    на отши́бе ― na otšíbe ― remotely, in the suburbs, far from the centre

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

отши́б • (otšíb)

  1. masculine singular past indicative perfective of отшибить (otšibítʹ)
