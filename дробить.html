дроби́ть (27156)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *drobiti.

Verb[edit]

дроби́ть • (drobítʹ) | impf (perfective раздроби́ть)

  1. to crush
  2. to smash, to shatter
  3. to fragment, to split up

Related terms[edit]

  • дробь (drobʹ), дро́бный (dróbnyj)
  • дробови́к (drobovík)
