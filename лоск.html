лоск (24780)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *lъskъ.

Noun

лоск • (losk) m inan (genitive ло́ска, nominative plural ло́ски, genitive plural ло́сков)

  1. gloss, lustre, polish
Related terms
  • лосни́ться (losnítʹsja)

Etymology 2

From Proto-Slavic *loskъ.

Noun

лоск • (losk) m inan (genitive ло́ска, nominative plural ло́ски, genitive plural ло́сков)

  1. (dialectal) dell, hollow, lowland
