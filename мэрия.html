мэ́рия (10683)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French mairie.

Noun[edit]

мэ́рия • (mɛrija) | f | inan (genitive мэ́рии, nominative plural мэ́рии, genitive plural мэ́рий)

  1. mayor's office
  2. city hall, town hall
