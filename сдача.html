сда́ча (5438)


Noun

сда́ча • (sdáča) f inan (genitive сда́чи, nominative plural сда́чи, genitive plural сда́ч)

  1. delivery
  2. (by extension) fighting back, hitting back the offender
  3. surrender
  4. change (money given back when a customer hands over more than the exact price of an item)
  5. deal (card game)
  6. letting out, leasing out (property; usually followed by внаём (vnajóm))

Related terms

  • сдава́ть impf (sdavátʹ), сдать pf (sdatʹ)
  • сдава́ться impf (sdavátʹsja), сда́ться pf (sdátʹsja)
