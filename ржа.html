ржа (17684)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic ръжа (rŭža), from Proto-Slavic *rъďa.

Noun

ржа • (rža) f inan (genitive ржи, uncountable)

  1. (archaic, colloquial) rust

Synonyms

  • (more common) ржа́вчина (ržávčina)

Related terms
