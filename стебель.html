сте́бель (7425)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *stьblь, from Proto-Indo-European *steib/*stib (“stem, cane”).

Noun[edit]

сте́бель • (stébelʹ) | m | inan (genitive сте́бля, nominative plural сте́бли, genitive plural стеблей or стеблей, diminutive стебелёк)

  1. (botany) stem, stalk (botany: above-ground stalk of a vascular plant)
