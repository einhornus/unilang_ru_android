я́блочник (30350)


Etymology

я́блоко (jábloko) + | -ник (-nik)

Noun

я́блочник • (jábločnik) m anim or m inan (genitive я́блочника, nominative plural я́блочники, genitive plural я́блочников)

  1. (animate) apple grower or seller
  2. (animate) member of an organization or political party using an apple as a symbol
  3. (animate) insect that feeds exclusively on apples
  4. (neologism, computing, slang, animate) avid user of Apple computers
  5. (cooking, inanimate) casserole or cake containing apples

Related terms

  • я́блоко (jábloko)
