сноби́зм (29749)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps borrowed from French snobisme.

Noun[edit]

сноби́зм • (snobízm) | m | inan (genitive сноби́зма, nominative plural сноби́змы, genitive plural сноби́змов)

  1. snobbishness, snobbery

Related terms[edit]

  • сноб (snob)
