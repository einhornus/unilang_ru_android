впери́ть (27426)


Verb

впери́ть • (vperítʹ) pf (imperfective вперя́ть)

  1. (dated, high register, transitive) to turn, to fix (especially взгля́д, взор, о́чи)
  2. (figuratively, informal) to get across (idea)
