впери́ть (27424)


Verb[edit]

впери́ть • (vperítʹ) | pf (imperfective вперять)

  1. (dated, | high register, | transitive) to turn, to fix (especially взгляд, взор, о́чи)
  2. (figuratively, | informal) to get across (idea)
