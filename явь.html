явь (15786)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *avь (“obvious, apparent, real”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ew- (“apparent”). Cognates include Polish jawa, Ukrainian ява (jáva), ява (javá) or яв (jav).

Noun[edit]

явь • (javʹ) | f | inan (genitive яви, nominative plural яви, genitive plural я́вей)

  1. reality

Related terms[edit]

  • наяву́ (najavú), я́вный (jávnyj)
