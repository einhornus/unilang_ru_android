затя́жка (14011)


Etymology[edit]

затяг- (zatjag-) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

затя́жка • (zatjážka) | f | inan (genitive затя́жки, nominative plural затя́жки, genitive plural затя́жек)

  1. puff, inhalation (cigarette)
  2. drawing tight, tightening
  3. tie-bar, baulk, straining beam, string piece
