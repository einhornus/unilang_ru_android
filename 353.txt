<p><span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sumerian_language" class="extiw" title="w:Sumerian language">Sumerian</a></span> <i class="Xsux mention" lang="sux">𒍑𒆪</i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="sux-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">gala</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, cognate to <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian_language" class="extiw" title="w:Akkadian language">Akkadian</a></span> <i class="Xsux mention" lang="akk">𒍑𒆪</i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="akk-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">kalû</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>.
