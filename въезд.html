въезд (8446)


Etymology[edit]

Deverbal of въезжа́ть (vʺjezžátʹ).

Noun[edit]

въезд • (vʺjezd) | m | inan (genitive въе́зда, nominative plural въе́зды, genitive plural въе́здов)

  1. entry, entrance (act of entering, especially when using any means of transportation)
  2. entry, entrance (doorway that provides a means of entering a building)

Related terms[edit]

  • въезжа́ть | impf (vʺjezžátʹ), въе́хать | pf (vʺjéxatʹ)
