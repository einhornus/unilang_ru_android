па́дший (20192)


Adjective

па́дший • (pádšij)

  1. (of a person) that lost good qualities; that underwent moral decline, or character decay
  2. (by extension) (of a woman) who became a prostitute

Related terms

  • па́вший (pávšij)
  • па́дать (pádatʹ), пасть (pastʹ)
  • паде́ние n (padénije)
