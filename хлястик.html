хля́стик (28875)


Noun[edit]

хля́стик • (xljástik) | m | inan (genitive хля́стика, nominative plural хля́стики, genitive plural хля́стиков)

  1. strap; long narrow strip of cloth or leather used to hold something like a piece of clothing or headwear, or a part of some equipment or furniture
