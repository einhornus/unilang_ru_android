нажа́ть (2799)


Etymology 1[edit]

на- (na-) + | жать (žatʹ, “to press, to squeeze”)

Verb[edit]

нажа́ть • (nažátʹ) | pf (imperfective нажима́ть)

  1. to press, to push
    Нажми́те любу́ю клавишу. ― Nažmíte ljubúju klávišu. ― Press any key. (in computer games)
  2. to urge, to impel, to influence, to put pressure on
  3. to press on, to press ahead
  4. to make by pressing
Related terms[edit]
  • жать (žatʹ)

Etymology 2[edit]

на- (na-) + | жать (žatʹ, “to reap, to harvest”)

Verb[edit]

нажа́ть • (nažátʹ) | pf (imperfective нажинать)

  1. to reap, to harvest (a specified quantity of something)
Related terms[edit]
  • жать | impf (žatʹ), сжать | pf (sžatʹ), пожа́ть | pf (požátʹ)
  • жа́тва (žátva)
  • жатка (žátka)
  • жнейка (žnéjka)
  • жнец (žnec)
