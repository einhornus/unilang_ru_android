больша́к (22078)


Noun[edit]

больша́к • (bolʹšák) | m | inan or m | anim (genitive большака́, nominative plural большаки́, genitive plural большако́в)

  1. (dated, | inanimate) a large road in rural areas
  2. (dated, | animate) male head of a family
