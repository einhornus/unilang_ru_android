гу́сто (3917)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *gǫstъ. Cognate to Serbo-Croatian gust, Bulgarian гъст (gǎst), Macedonian гу́ст (gust), Polish gęsty.

Adverb[edit]

гу́сто • (gústo)

  1. thickly, densely

Adjective[edit]

гу́сто • (gústo)

  1. short neuter singular of густо́й (gustój)
