нея́ркий (13739)


Etymology

не- (ne-) + | я́ркий (járkij)

Adjective

нея́ркий • (nejárkij) (no comparative)

  1. dim, faint (of light)
  2. (figuratively) pale, impure (of colors or sounds)
  3. (figuratively) imperceptible
  4. (figuratively) not producing a strong impression
  5. (figuratively) ordinary, nonunique

Related terms

  • я́рко (járko), я́ркость (járkostʹ)

не-я́р-кий
