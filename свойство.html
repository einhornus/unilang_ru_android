сво́йство (2670)


Etymology 1[edit]

свой (svoj) + | -ство (-stvo)

Noun[edit]

сво́йство • (svójstvo) | n | inan (genitive сво́йства, nominative plural сво́йства, genitive plural сво́йств)

  1. property, virtue, quality (attribute or abstract quality associated with an object)
  2. kind
  3. (in the plural) characteristics

Etymology 2[edit]

свой (svoj) + -ство (-stvo)

Noun[edit]

сво́йство • (svojstvó) | n | inan (genitive сво́йства, uncountable)

  1. relationship by marriage
  2. affinity
Related terms[edit]
  • свойственник (svojstvennik)
