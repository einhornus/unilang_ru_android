одеяло (1984)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic одѣꙝло (oděꙝlo, “garment, blanket”), from Old Church Slavonic одѣꙗло (odějalo, “outerwear, cloak”), from Old Church Slavonic дѣти (děti, “to set, to place”), from Proto-Slavic *děti, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁-, whence English face and do.

Noun[edit]

одеяло • (odejálo) | n | inan (genitive одея́ла, nominative plural одея́ла, genitive plural одея́л)

  1. blanket, counterpane
  2. quilt
