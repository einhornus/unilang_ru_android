па́хнуть (997)


Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *paxnǫti.

Verb[edit]

па́хнуть • (páxnutʹ) | impf

  1. to smell (of), to savour
    Деньги не па́хнут. ― Dénʹgi ne páxnut. ― Money has no smell.
  2. to smell, to reek (of) (+ instrumental case)
Related terms[edit]
  • запах (zapax)

Etymology 2[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *paxnǫti.

Verb[edit]

па́хнуть • (paxnútʹ) | pf

  1. to puff, to blow
    Пахнуло хо́лодом. ― Paxnúlo xólodom. ― There came a cold blast.
