ны́не (2503)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic нꙑнѣ (nyně), compare with Ukrainian нинi (nýni).

Adverb[edit]

ны́не • (nýne)

  1. now, nowadays, today, at present

Synonyms[edit]

  • днесь (dnesʹ)
  • ны́нче (nýnče)
  • сего́дня (sevódnja)
  • тепе́рь (tepérʹ)
