реставри́ровать (26184)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from German restaurieren + | -овать (-ovatʹ); see also -ировать (-irovatʹ).

Verb[edit]

реставри́ровать • (restavrírovatʹ) | impf or pf

  1. to restore, to refurbish (e.g. a building)
    Perfective: отреставри́ровать (otrestavrírovatʹ) (also)
  2. (literary) to restore, to bring back (e.g. an institution, custom, etc.)
