поно́с (9290)


Noun[edit]

поно́с • (ponós) | m | inan (genitive поно́са, nominative plural поно́сы, genitive plural поно́сов)

  1. diarrhea
  2. (colloquial, | figuratively) too much of something that is incapable to stop itself
    слове́сный поно́с ― slovésnyj ponós ― verbal diarrhea

Synonyms[edit]

  • диарея (diaréja)

Related terms[edit]

  • поноси́ть | impf (ponósitʹ)
