повора́чивать (4167)


Etymology[edit]

поворотить (povorotítʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

повора́чивать • (povoráčivatʹ) | impf (perfective поверну́ть or поворотить)

  1. (transitive) to turn, to swing
  2. to turn (to change direction of one's motion)

Usage notes[edit]

  • поверну́ть (povernútʹ) and поворотить (povorotítʹ) are both possible perfectives of повора́чивать (povoráčivatʹ), but the latter is specifically low-colloquial, while the former is normal register or colloquial.

Related terms[edit]

  • поворо́т (povorót)
