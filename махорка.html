махо́рка (10179)


Etymology[edit]

Modification of *амерфортский (*amerfórtskij, “Amersfoort (relational)”) + -ка (-ka), from the Dutch city of Amersfoort, known for its tobacco industries.

Noun[edit]

махо́рка • (maxórka) | f | inan (genitive махо́рки, nominative plural махо́рки, genitive plural махо́рок)

  1. A coarse, strong type of tobacco, makhorka (Nicotiana rustica)
