усыпи́ть (15407)


Etymology[edit]

From у- (u-) + | -сыпить (-sypítʹ), causative of спать (spatʹ), from Old East Slavic съпати (sŭpati), from Proto-Slavic *sъpati.

Verb[edit]

усыпи́ть • (usypítʹ) | pf (imperfective усыпля́ть)

  1. to put/lull to sleep
  2. to allay (suspicions)
  3. to euthanize (an animal)

Related terms[edit]

  • спать (spatʹ)
  • сон (son)
