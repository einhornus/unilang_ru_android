разду́мывать (5847)


Etymology 1[edit]

Verb[edit]

разду́мывать • (razdúmyvatʹ) | impf (perfective разду́мать)

  1. to think (about), to muse (over), to ponder (over), to consider, to ruminate (on), to brood (over), to deliberate
  2. to hesitate
    не разду́мывая ― ne razdúmyvaja ― without hesitation; without a moment's thought

Related terms[edit]

  • разду́мье (razdúmʹje)

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

разду́мывать • (razdúmyvatʹ) | impf (perfective разду́мать)

  1. to change one's mind
