<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">fox</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">foxes</b> <i>or</i> <span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content qualifier-content">nonstandard, dialectal</span><span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">)</span> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">foxen</b>)
