смо́кинг (18352)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French smoking, from English smoking jacket.

Noun[edit]

смо́кинг • (smóking) | m | inan (genitive смо́кинга, nominative plural смо́кинги, genitive plural смо́кингов)

  1. tuxedo
  2. dinner jacket
