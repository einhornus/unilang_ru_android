вы́кроить (22724)


Etymology[edit]

вы- (vý-) + | крои́ть (kroítʹ)

Verb[edit]

вы́кроить • (výkroitʹ) | pf (imperfective выкраивать)

  1. to cut out (patterns, shapes, etc. in tissue or skin)
  2. to shape by cutting
  3. (figuratively, | colloquial) to allocate (a part of an area or room)
  4. (figuratively, | colloquial) to find (a place for something)
  5. (figuratively) to have difficulty finding (the time or money to meet someone)

Related terms[edit]

  • крои́ть (kroítʹ)
  • кройка (krójka)
