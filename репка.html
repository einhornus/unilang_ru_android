ре́пка (26980)


Etymology 1[edit]

ре́па (répa) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

ре́пка • (répka) | f | inan (genitive ре́пки, nominative plural ре́пки, genitive plural ре́пок)

  1. (colloquial) endearing diminutive of ре́па (répa): (small) turnip
  2. (nautical) knot or sealing at the end of a rope cable to prevent it from unraveling

Etymology 2[edit]

ре́п(етитор) (rep(etítor)) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

ре́пка • (répka) | f | anim (genitive ре́пки, nominative plural ре́пки, genitive plural ре́пок)

  1. (colloquial, | humorous) female tutor
