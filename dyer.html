dyer (17199)


Etymology

dye + | -er

Noun

dyer (plural dyers)

  1. One who dyes, especially one who dyes cloth etc. as an occupation.

Synonyms

  • litster (archaic, UK, Scotland)

Related terms

  • dyer's broom
  • dyer's rocket
  • dyer's weed
