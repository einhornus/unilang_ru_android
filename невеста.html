невеста (2702)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *nevěsta. Probably from не (ne, “not”) + ведать (védatʹ, “to know”), indicating a strict hands-off relationship.

Noun[edit]

невеста • (nevésta) | f | anim (genitive неве́сты, nominative plural неве́сты, genitive plural неве́ст)

  1. fiancée, bride
  2. marriageable girl

Synonyms[edit]

  • молода́я (molodája)
  • новобра́чная (novobráčnaja)

Related terms[edit]

  • неве́стка (nevéstka)
