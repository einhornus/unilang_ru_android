астра́льный (11890)


Adjective

астра́льный • (astrálʹnyj)

  1. astral (relating to or resembling the stars)
  2. astral (relating to a supposed supersensible substance)

Synonyms

  • звёздный (zvjózdnyj)

Related terms

  • астра́л (astral)
