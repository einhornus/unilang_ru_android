соло́нка (30099)


Noun[edit]

соло́нка • (solónka) | f | inan (genitive соло́нки, nominative plural соло́нки, genitive plural соло́нок)

  1. salt shaker, salt cellar (a small container designed to hold salt and facilitate sprinkling)
