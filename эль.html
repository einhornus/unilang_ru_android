эль (10170)


Etymology

Borrowed from English ale.

Noun

эль • (elʹ) m inan (genitive э́ля, nominative plural э́ли, genitive plural э́лей)

  1. ale (English beer)

Noun

эль • (elʹ) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. The Cyrillic letter Л, л.
  2. The Roman letter L, l.

Synonyms

  • (letter): эл (el)
