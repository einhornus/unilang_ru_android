влия́ть (5571)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic въльꙗти (vŭlĭjati). In the modern senses, a semantic loan from French influer.

Verb

влия́ть • (vlijátʹ) impf (perfective повлия́ть)

  1. (obsolete) to pour in
  2. to influence, to have an influence
  3. to affect, to govern
  4. to have effect, to have action

Derived terms

  • влия́ние (vlijánije)
  • влия́тельный (vlijátelʹnyj)
