ресница (4009)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic рѣсьнъ (rěsĭnŭ) + -ица (-ica), from Proto-Slavic *ręs-ьn-

Noun

ресни́ца • (resníca) f inan (genitive ресни́цы, nominative plural ресни́цы, genitive plural ресни́ц)

  1. eyelash
