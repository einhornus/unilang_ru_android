вор (2196)


Etymology 1[edit]

Probably related to врать (vratʹ, “to lie”).

Noun[edit]

вор • (vor) | m | anim (genitive во́ра or во́ра*, nominative plural во́ры or во́ры*, genitive plural воро́в, feminine воро́вка) (* Nonstandard but common.)

  1. thief
  2. (obsolete) traitor, apostate, outlaw
  3. Clipping of вор в зако́не (vor v zakóne).
Synonyms[edit]
  • (thief): тать (tatʹ), похити́тель (poxitítelʹ)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from English vore.

Noun[edit]

вор • (vor) | m | inan (genitive во́ра, uncountable)

  1. vore (sexual fetish)
Synonyms[edit]
  • воор (voor), ворофилия (vorofilíja)
