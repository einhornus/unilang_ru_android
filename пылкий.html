пы́лкий (14770)


Etymology[edit]

пыл (pyl) + | -кий (-kij)

Adjective[edit]

пы́лкий • (pýlkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)пыльче or (по)пылче)

  1. ardent, fervent, fiery, impassioned, fervid
  2. torrid
  3. perfervid
  4. passionate
  5. spunky
  6. mettlesome
  7. impetuous
  8. ablaze, glowing
  9. flaming
  10. vivid
  11. hot
