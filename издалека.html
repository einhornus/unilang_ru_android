издалека́ (4464)


Etymology 1[edit]

Adverb[edit]

издалека́ • (izdaleká)

  1. from afar
  2. afar off
Usage notes[edit]
  • The form "издалека́" is more modern, standard and common.

Etymology 2[edit]

Adverb[edit]

издалека́ • (izdaljóka)

  1. Alternative spelling of издалёка (izdaljóka)
Usage notes[edit]
