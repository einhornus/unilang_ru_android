рее́стр (22995)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish rejestr, from Latin regestrum

Noun

рее́стр • (rejéstr) m inan (genitive рее́стра, nominative plural рее́стры, genitive plural рее́стров)

  1. register, registry
  2. roll (an official or public document)
