баба́хнуть (30922)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Verb

баба́хнуть • (babáxnutʹ) pf (imperfective баба́хать)

  1. (colloquial) to give a resounding slap, to go off with a bang, to pop, to explode (transitive and intransitive)
