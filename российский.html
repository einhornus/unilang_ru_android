российский (501)


Etymology[edit]

Россия (Rossíja) + | -ийский (-íjskij)

Adjective[edit]

российский • (rossíjskij)

  1. Russian (concerning the country, nationality)

Usage notes[edit]

The word российский emphasizes belonging to Russia, as a citizen or resident regardless of ethnicity, as opposed to русский (rússkij), which indicates ethnicity, etc. In some cases the words may be used interchangeably.

Related terms[edit]

  • всеросси́йский (vserossíjskij)
  • общеросси́йский (obščerossíjskij)
