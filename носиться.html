носи́ться (5276)


Etymology[edit]

носи́ть (nosítʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

носи́ться • (nosítʹsja) | impf

  1. to rush, to rush around, to run/drive around (on a high speed)
  2. to float around, to fly around
  3. (colloquial) to fuss over, to pay too much attention to, to be obsessed with (an idea)
  4. (of smells, sounds, etc.) to spread
  5. to wear, to be worn (of clothes)
    Э́то пла́тье хорошо́ носи́ться.
    Éto plátʹje xorošó nosítʹsja.
    This dress wears well.
  6. passive of носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Usage notes[edit]

носи́ться is an abstract verb. The counterpart, нести́сь (nestísʹ), is a concrete verb.
