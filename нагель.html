на́гель (30245)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Nagel (“nail”).

Noun

на́гель • (nágelʹ) m inan (genitive на́геля, nominative plural на́гели, genitive plural на́гелей) (normal usage)
на́гель • (nágelʹ) m inan (genitive на́геля, nominative plural нагеля́, genitive plural нагеле́й) (professional usage)

  1. (engineering) pin, peg, dowel
