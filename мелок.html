мелок (21482)


Etymology 1[edit]

мел (mel) + | -ок (-ók) | 1 |

Noun[edit]

мелок • (melók) | m | inan (genitive мелка́, nominative plural мелки, genitive plural мелко́в)

  1. chalk
  2. stick of pastel or similar
  3. (rare, | by extension) crayon

Adjective[edit]

мелок • (mélok)
