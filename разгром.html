разгро́м (7235)


Noun[edit]

разгро́м • (razgróm) | m | inan (genitive разгро́ма, nominative plural разгро́мы, genitive plural разгро́мов)

  1. destruction, defeat, downfall, rout
  2. discord, chaos, mayhem
