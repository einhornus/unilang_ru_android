отро́сток (19474)


Noun

отро́сток • (otróstok) m inan (genitive отро́стка, nominative plural отро́стки, genitive plural отро́стков)

  1. process, outgrowth
  2. appendage (an external body part that projects from the body)
  3. sprout, sucker
  4. slip
  5. rame
  6. ramification
  7. spur
