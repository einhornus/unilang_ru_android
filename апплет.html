аппле́т (30651)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English applet.

Noun[edit]

аппле́т • (applét) | m | inan (genitive аппле́та, nominative plural аппле́ты, genitive plural аппле́тов)

  1. (computing) applet (a small program module that runs under the control of a larger application)
