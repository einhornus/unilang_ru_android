ста́вка (3446)


Etymology[edit]

ста́вить (stávitʹ) + | -ка (-ka)

Noun[edit]

ста́вка • (stávka) | f | inan (genitive ста́вки, nominative plural ста́вки, genitive plural ста́вок)

  1. rate, wage, salary
    рабо́тать на по́лной ста́вке ― rabótatʹ na pólnoj stávke ― to have a full-time job
  2. stake
  3. (military) headquarters

Synonyms[edit]

  • ((military) headquarters) штаб | m (štab)
