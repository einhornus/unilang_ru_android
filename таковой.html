таково́й (3629)


Etymology[edit]

так (tak) + | -овой (-ovój)

Adjective[edit]

таково́й • (takovój) (no comparative)

  1. such
    был таков ― byl takóv ― disappeared, vanished; was such
    и был таков ― i byl takóv ― and off he went; and that was the last we saw of him
  2. alike, like
  3. same
    таково́е бы́ло получено 16-го с/м. ― takovóje býlo polúčeno 16-go s/m. ― the same was received on the 16th inst. (of this month)

Related terms[edit]

  • так (tak)
  • тако́й (takój)
