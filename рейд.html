рейд (9592)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from English raid.

Noun[edit]

рейд • (rejd) | m | inan (genitive ре́йда, nominative plural ре́йды, genitive plural ре́йдов)

  1. (military, | law enforcement) raid
    рейд в тыл проти́вника ― rejd v tyl protívnika ― a raid behind enemy lines

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from Dutch rede.

Noun[edit]

рейд • (rejd) | m | inan (genitive ре́йда, nominative plural ре́йды, genitive plural ре́йдов)

  1. (nautical) roads, roadstead, anchorage
    стоя́ть на ре́йде ― stojátʹ na réjde ― to be anchored in the roads
