сосна́ (3011)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic сосна́ (sosna), from Proto-Slavic *sosna, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱesn-.

Noun[edit]

сосна́ • (sosná) | f | inan (genitive сосны, nominative plural сосны, genitive plural со́сен)

  1. pine, pine tree
  2. pine wood

Related terms[edit]

  • сосновик | m (sosnovík)
  • сосно́вый (sosnóvyj)
