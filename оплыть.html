оплы́ть (27530)


Etymology

о- (o-) + | плыть (plytʹ)

Verb

оплы́ть • (oplýtʹ) pf (imperfective оплыва́ть)

  1. (transitive) to swim around
  2. (intransitive) to swell up; to become bloated; to gutter (of a candle)
