от (27)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *otъ.

Preposition[edit]

от • (ot) (+ genitive case)

  1. from
    от души ― ot duší ― from the heart
  2. since

Usage notes[edit]

Before words beginning with certain awkward consonant clusters such as мн- (mn-), вс- (vs-), and сн- (sn-), the variant form ото (oto) is used.
