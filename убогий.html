убо́гий (31694)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *ubogъ.

Adjective

убо́гий • (ubógij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)убо́же)

  1. wretched, poor, poverty-stricken, squalid, miserable, sordid, beggarly
  2. disabled, crippled
  3. small-minded, petty

Noun

убо́гий • (ubógij) m anim (genitive убо́гого, nominative plural убо́гие, genitive plural убо́гих, feminine убо́гая)

  1. pauper
  2. disabled person, cripple

Related terms

  • убо́жество (ubóžestvo)
