<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">mantel</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">mantels</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>The shelf above a fireplace which may be also a structural support for the masonry of the chimney.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">climbing</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A maneuver to surmount a ledge, involving pushing down on the ledge to bring up the body. Also called a mantelshelf.</li></ol>
