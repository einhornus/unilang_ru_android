целко́вый (28342)


Noun[edit]

целко́вый • (celkóvyj) | m | inan (genitive целко́вого, nominative plural целко́вые, genitive plural целко́вых)

  1. (historical) a silver coin worth one ruble
  2. (low | | colloquial, | somewhat | dated) ruble
    Synonym: рубль (rublʹ)
