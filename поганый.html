пога́ный (7262)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic поганъ (poganŭ, “pagan”), from Latin paganus (“pagan”).

Adjective[edit]

пога́ный • (pogányj)

  1. (archaic) heathen
  2. foul, unclean
  3. lousy, nasty, rotten
  4. (mushroom) poisonous

Related terms[edit]

  • поганить | impf (pogánitʹ), испоганить | pf (ispogánitʹ)
