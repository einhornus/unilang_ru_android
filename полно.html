полно (4009)


Etymology[edit]

From по́лный (pólnyj) + | -о (-o) (stem-stressed) or from по́лный (pólnyj) + | -о (-ó) (ending-stressed).

Adverb[edit]

полно • (pólno)

  1. fully, in full

полно • (polnó)

  1. brim-full, full to the brim

Interjection[edit]

полно • (pólno)

  1. okay, stop, that's it, enough (of this)

Adjective[edit]

полно • (polnó)

  1. short neuter singular of по́лный (pólnyj, “full, complete, perfect”)

полно • (pólno)

  1. short neuter singular of по́лный (pólnyj, “exhaustive, chubby”)
