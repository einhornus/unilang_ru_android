заря́ (4468)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *zorja.

Noun[edit]

заря́ • (zarjá) | f | inan (genitive зари, nominative plural зо́ри, genitive plural зорь)

  1. dawn, daybreak
    на заре́ ― na zaré ― at the earliest stage, at the beginning (literally, “at the dawn”)
    от зари до зари ― ot zarí do zarí ― from morning to night, from dawn to dusk (literally, “from dawn to dawn”)
  2. dusk, nightfall
  3. (military) reveille
  4. (military) tattoo, retreat
  5. outset, start

Related terms[edit]

  • зарни́ца (zarníca)
  • зарянка (zarjánka)
  • озаря́ть (ozarjátʹ), озари́ть (ozarítʹ)
