номеро́к (21732)


Etymology[edit]

но́мер (nómer) + | -ок (-ók)

Noun[edit]

номеро́к • (nomerók) | m | inan (genitive номерка́, nominative plural номерки́, genitive plural номерко́в)

  1. Diminutive of но́мер (nómer, “hotel room, license plate, etc.”)
  2. (colloquial) ticket, check (especially one issued in a coat check)
