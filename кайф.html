кайф (8576)


Etymology

Ultimately from Arabic كَيْف | (kayf, “pleasure”).

Noun

кайф • (kajf) m inan (genitive ка́йфа, uncountable)

  1. (colloquial) kif, euphoria caused by narcotic intoxication
  2. (colloquial) bliss, pleasant idleness

Derived terms

  • кайфова́ть (kajfovátʹ)
  • лови́ть кайф (lovítʹ kajf)
