набе́г (15331)


Etymology[edit]

Deverbal of набе́гать (nabegátʹ).

Noun[edit]

набе́г • (nabég) | m | inan (genitive набе́га, nominative plural набеги, genitive plural набе́гов)

  1. incursion, raid, foray (of barbarians, nomads, gangs, etc.)
  2. surge, rush, gust

Related terms[edit]

  • набеговый (nabégovyj)
