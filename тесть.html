тесть (6962)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tьstь.

Noun[edit]

тесть • (testʹ) | m | anim (genitive те́стя, nominative plural те́сти, genitive plural те́стей)

  1. a man’s father-in-law (wife’s father only)

Related terms[edit]

  • тёща (tjóšča)
