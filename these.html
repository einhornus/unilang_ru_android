these (66)


Etymology

From Middle English thes, from Old English þas, from Proto-West Germanic *þes-, a form of Proto-Germanic *sa (“that”), from Proto-Indo-European *só.

Determiner

these

    • Seinfeld, The Alternate Side
      These pretzels are making me thirsty.

Usage notes

Depending on the context, the word those may be used either in place of or interchangeably with these.
