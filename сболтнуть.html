сболтну́ть (21727)


Verb[edit]

сболтну́ть • (sboltnútʹ) | pf

  1. (colloquial) to just happen to say something, to say something silly (or wrong), to blurt out
    Я сболтну́л ли́шнее.
    Ja sboltnúl líšneje.
    I said something wrong.
