ко́ми (31858)


Noun

ко́ми • (kómi) m anim or m inan (indeclinable)

  1. Komi, a member of a Finno-Ugric people who live in the northwest Urals, Zyrians
  2. Komi, the Finno-Ugric language spoken by these people

Synonyms

  • (language): ко́ми-зырянский (kómi-zyrjanskij), зырянский (zyrjanskij)
