село (3826)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *selo, from Proto-Indo-European *sel-.

Noun[edit]

село • (seló) | n | inan (genitive села, nominative plural сёла, genitive plural сёл)

  1. village
Usage notes[edit]
  • Traditionally, село (seló) referred to a larger village with a church, while дере́вня (derévnja) referred to a smaller village without a church.
Related terms[edit]

  • селить (selítʹ)
  • сели́ться (selítʹsja)
  • се́льский (sélʹskij)
  • селяни́н (seljanín)
  • селянка (seljanka)
  • сельчанин | m (selʹčánin)
