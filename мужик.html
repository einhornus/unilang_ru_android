мужи́к (653)


Etymology[edit]

From муж (muž, “man (dated); husband”) + | -ик (-ik).

Noun[edit]

мужи́к • (mužík) | m | anim (genitive мужика́, nominative plural мужики́, genitive plural мужико́в)

  1. boor
  2. coarse, uncouth person
  3. (colloquial | or | derogatory) man (male person)
  4. (colloquial) husband
  5. (historical) peasant from tsarist Russia
  6. (slang) macho

Synonyms[edit]

  • (husband): муж (muž)
  • (man): мужчи́на (mužčína)
  • (peasant): крестья́нин (krestʹjánin)
  • (macho): мачо (máčo)
