пяток (4744)


Etymology 1[edit]

From пять (pjatʹ).

Noun[edit]

пяток • (pjatók) | m | inan (genitive пятка, nominative plural пятки, genitive plural пятко́в)

  1. (colloquial) (about) five

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

пяток • (pjátok) | f | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of пятка (pjátka)
