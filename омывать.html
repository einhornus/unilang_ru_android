омыва́ть (20415)


Etymology[edit]

омы́ть (omýtʹ) + | -вать (-vátʹ)

Verb[edit]

омыва́ть • (omyvátʹ) | impf (perfective омы́ть)

  1. (literary, | also | | impersonal) to wash
    дождь омы́л у́лицы ― doždʹ omýl úlicy ― the rain washed the streets
    у́лицы омы́ло дождём ― úlicy omýlo doždjóm ― the streets were washed by the rain
  2. (imperfective only) to pour over from all sides, to lap (of water)
  3. (imperfective only) to surround with its water (of the sea or ocean)
