тост (6037)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English toast.

Noun[edit]

тост • (tost) | m | inan (genitive то́ста, nominative plural то́сты, genitive plural то́стов)

  1. toast (salutation while drinking alcohol)
  2. toast (toasted bread)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (salutation while drinking alcohol): здра́вица (zdrávica)
  • (toasted bread): грено́к (grenók), гренка́ (grénka)
