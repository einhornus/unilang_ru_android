гид (12096)


Etymology

Borrowed from French guide.

Noun

гид • (gid) m anim (genitive ги́да, nominative plural ги́ды, genitive plural ги́дов)

  1. guide (someone who guides, especially someone hired to show people around a place)

Related terms

  • гиди́рование n (gidírovanije)

гид
