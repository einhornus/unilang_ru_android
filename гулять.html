гуля́ть (1697)


Etymology[edit]

Old East Slavic [Term?], cognate with Belarusian гуляць (huljácʹ), Ukrainian гуляти (huljaty).

Verb[edit]

гуля́ть • (guljátʹ) | impf (perfective погуля́ть)

  1. to walk, to go for a walk, to stroll
  2. to have time-off, to have free time
  3. to make merry, to enjoy oneself, to carouse
    1. to party
  4. to go with, to go out with someone
