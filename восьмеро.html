во́сьмеро (30753)


Numeral[edit]

во́сьмеро • (vósʹmero)

  1. eight (in a group together), eight of them
    во́сьмеро дете́й ― vósʹmero detéj ― eight children
    во́сьмеро часо́в ― vósʹmero časóv ― eight watches
    во́сьмеро очко́в ― vósʹmero očkóv ― eight pairs of glasses
    во́сьмеро из нас ― vósʹmero iz nas ― eight of us
    нас бы́ло во́сьмеро ― nas býlo vósʹmero ― there were eight of us

Usage notes[edit]

The nominative of a collective numeral governs the genitive plural of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. The other cases agree with that of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. Not used with feminine nouns. Used mostly when speaking of children and with a few nouns that are used in the plural only, such as часы́, очки́, or with pronouns.
