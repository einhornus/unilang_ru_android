штамп (8479)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Stampfe.

Noun[edit]

штамп • (štamp) | m | inan (genitive шта́мпа, nominative plural шта́мпы, genitive plural шта́мпов)

  1. stamp, rubber stamp, impress
  2. die (tool for cutting or shaping material using a press)
  3. (figuratively) stock phrase
