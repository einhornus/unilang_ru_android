нутро́ (8606)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *ǫtro.

Noun[edit]

нутро́ • (nutró) | n | inan (genitive нутра́, uncountable)

  1. the inside, the inward nature (especially of a person), gut feeling
    Я нутро́м чу́ю, не к добру́ всё э́то.
    Ja nutróm čúju, ne k dobrú vsjó éto.
    I have a gut feeling, this won't mean anything good.
    (literally, “I feel with [my] inside, not for the good is all this.”)
  2. inside
