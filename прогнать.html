прогна́ть (6346)


Etymology[edit]

про- (pro-) + | гнать (gnatʹ)

Verb[edit]

прогна́ть • (prognátʹ) | pf (imperfective прогоня́ть)

  1. to drive (cattle)
  2. to chase away
  3. (colloquial) to cause to disappear, to banish
  4. (colloquial) to fire

Related terms[edit]

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
