промыва́ть (15899)


Etymology[edit]

промы́ть (promýtʹ) + | -вать (-vátʹ)

Verb[edit]

промыва́ть • (promyvátʹ) | impf (perfective промы́ть)

  1. to wash (well/properly/thoroughly)
  2. to rinse, to bathe, to irrigate
  3. to jig
  4. to wash out, to wash away, to make (a hole, ravine, etc)
