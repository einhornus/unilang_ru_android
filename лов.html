лов (22538)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *lovъ, cognates include Czech lov.

Noun[edit]

лов • (lov) | m | inan (genitive ло́ва, nominative plural ло́вы, genitive plural ло́вов)

  1. catching, hunting

Related terms[edit]

  • лови́ть (lovítʹ)
  • ло́вля (lóvlja)
