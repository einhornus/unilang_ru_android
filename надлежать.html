надлежа́ть (6667)


Etymology

Since the late 17th century. над- (nad-) + | лежа́ть (ležátʹ), possibly a calque of German obliegen.

Verb

надлежа́ть • (nadležátʹ) impf

  1. (impersonal, formal) to have to (to do as a requirement)

Derived terms

  • надлежа́щий (nadležáščij)
