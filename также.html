та́кже (319)


Adverb[edit]

та́кже • (tákže)

  1. also, too, as well, likewise
    Он та́кже прихо́дит.
    On tákže prixódit.
    He too is coming.
  2. (in negative constructions) either, neither, nor

Synonyms[edit]

  • то́же (tóže)
