пурга́ (7725)


Etymology

Likely from a Finno-Ugric language. Cognate with Karelian purgu, Khanty пуркэ (purkè), Mansi па̄рк (pārk)

Noun

пурга́ • (purgá) f inan (genitive пурги́, nominative plural пурги́, genitive plural пург)

  1. blizzard, snowstorm

  2. перен., разг. чушь, ерунда́
