блинда́ж (6851)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French blindage.

Noun[edit]

блинда́ж • (blindáž) | m | inan (genitive блиндажа́, nominative plural блиндажи́, genitive plural блиндаже́й)

  1. (military) blindage, dugout
