скра́шивать (29090)


Etymology[edit]

скрасить (skrásitʹ) + | -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Verb[edit]

скра́шивать • (skrášivatʹ) | impf (perfective скрасить)

  1. to soften, to mitigate (something painful)
  2. to brighten, to make more attractive (something dull or boring)
