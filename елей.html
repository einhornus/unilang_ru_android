елей (15559)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowing from Ancient Greek ἔλαιον (élaion, “oil”)

Noun[edit]

елей • (jeléj) | m | inan (genitive еле́я, nominative plural еле́и, genitive plural еле́ев)

  1. holy oil, chrism
  2. (figuratively) balm, unction
Related terms[edit]
  • елейный (jeléjnyj)
  • елейность (jeléjnostʹ)
