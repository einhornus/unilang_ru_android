инстру́кция (3398)


Noun[edit]

инстру́кция • (instrúkcija) | f | inan (genitive инстру́кции, nominative plural инстру́кции, genitive plural инстру́кций)

  1. instruction (act of instructing or that which instructs, or with which one is instructed)
