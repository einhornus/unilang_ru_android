блин (4912)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *mlinъ.

Noun

блин • (blin) m inan (genitive блина́, nominative plural блины́, genitive plural блино́в)

  1. pancake (thin batter cake)
  2. (figuratively) a flat round object
  3. (computer slang) Cd

Related terms

  • бли́нный (blínnyj) бли́нчик (blínčik)

Interjection

блин • (blin)

  1. (euphemistic, colloquial, exclamation) shoot! (expression of any strong emotion, euphemism for блядь!)

Synonyms

  • ёлки-па́лки (jólki-pálki) ё-моё (jó-mojó) чёрт (čort)
