<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adjective">Adjective</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">interim</strong> (<i>not comparable</i>)
</p>
<ol><li>Transitional.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Iraq's government is <b>interim</b>.</i></div></dd></dl>
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>1960</b> June,  “Diesel locomotive operation on the Great Eastern Line”, in  <cite>Trains Illustrated</cite>, page 374:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">In a period of transition from steam to diesel, many of the schemes are inevitably of an <b>interim</b> nature and only on full dieselisation will the final pattern be determined and full benefit derived.</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li>
<li>Temporary.
<dl><dd><span class="nyms synonym"><span class="defdate">Synonyms:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">provisional</span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en">caretaker</span> <span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content qualifier-content">UK</span><span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">)</span></span></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">You are <b>interim</b> manager until he returns from hospital.</i></div></dd></dl>
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>2012</b> May 5,  Phil McNulty,  “Chelsea 2-1 Liverpool”, in  <cite>BBC Sport</cite> | <sup><a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17878435">[1]</a></sup>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">Drogba's goal early in the second half - his fourth in this Wembley showpiece - proved decisive as the remarkable turnaround in Chelsea's fortunes under <b>interim</b> manager Roberto di Matteo was rewarded with silverware.</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li></ol>
<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">interim</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">interims</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>A transitional or temporary period between other events.
<dl><dd><span class="nyms synonym"><span class="defdate">Synonyms:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">between-time</span>; <i>see also</i> Thesaurus:<span class="Latn" lang="en">interim</span></span></dd>
