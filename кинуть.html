ки́нуть (3657)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *kydnǫti.

Verb[edit]

ки́нуть • (kínutʹ) | pf (imperfective кида́ть)

  1. to throw, to cast, to fling
  2. (slang) to cheat, to swindle
