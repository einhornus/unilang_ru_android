старомо́дный (8195)


Adjective[edit]

старомо́дный • (staromódnyj)

  1. outmoded, antique
  2. old-fashioned, antiquated
  3. frumpy, frumpish
  4. fusty
  5. stuffy
  6. moldy
  7. primitive
  8. Victorian
  9. fogyish
