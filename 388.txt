</p>
<ol><li>Any of various Old-World rodent species belonging to the subfamily Cricetinae.
<ol><li>especially, the golden hamster, <i>Mesocricetus auratus</i>, and the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/dwarf_hamster" class="extiw" title="w:dwarf hamster">dwarf hamster</a>s of genus <i>Phodopus</i>, often kept as a pets and used in scientific research.

<li>Other rodents of similar appearance, such as the maned hamster or crested hamster, <span class="biota"><a href="https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Lophiomys_imhausi" class="extiw" title="wikispecies:Lophiomys imhausi"><i>Lophiomys imhausi</i></a></span>, mouse-like hamsters of genus <span class="biota"><a href="https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Calomyscus" class="extiw" title="wikispecies:Calomyscus"><i>Calomyscus</i></a></span>, and the white-tailed rat (<span class="biota"><a href="https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Mystromys_albicaudatus" class="extiw" title="wikispecies:Mystromys albicaudatus"><i>Mystromys albicaudatus</i></a></span>).</li></ol>
