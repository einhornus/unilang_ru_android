загну́ть (15801)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) + | гнуть (gnutʹ)

Verb[edit]

загну́ть • (zagnútʹ) | pf (imperfective загиба́ть)

  1. to turn up, to turn down, to bend
    загну́ть па́лец ― zagnútʹ pálec ― to bend/crook a finger
    загну́ть страни́цу ― zagnútʹ stranícu ― turn down [dog-ear] the page
    загну́ть це́ну ― zagnútʹ cénu ― to ask/charge an exorbitant price
  2. (colloquial) to exaggerate, to pull someone's leg
  3. (low | | colloquial) to say, to let loose
    загну́ть кре́пкое словцо́ ― zagnútʹ krépkoje slovcó ― to let loose an expletive; utter a strong/bad/swear word
    ну и вопро́с он загну́л! ― nu i voprós on zagnúl! ― some question he asked!

Related terms[edit]
