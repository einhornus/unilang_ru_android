невзирая (13822)


Etymology[edit]

From не- (ne-) + | взира́я (vzirája), present adverbial participle of взира́ть (vzirátʹ, “to look, to gaze”); compare regardless, literally "without looking".

Adverb[edit]

невзирая • (nevzirája)

  1. regardless

Usage notes[edit]

Only used in the expression невзирая на (nevzirája na, “regardless of, despite”).

Synonyms[edit]

  • несмотря́ (nesmotrjá)

Related terms[edit]

  • взира́ть (vzirátʹ)
  • зреть | impf (zretʹ), узре́ть | pf (uzrétʹ)
