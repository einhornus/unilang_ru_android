шныря́ть (15570)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from шнырить (šnýritʹ) + | -ять (-játʹ).

Verb[edit]

шныря́ть • (šnyrjátʹ) | impf

  1. (colloquial) to snoop around
  2. (colloquial) to scurry, to move quickly
  3. (colloquial, | idiomatic)
    шныря́ть глаза́ми ― šnyrjátʹ glazámi ― to look around,to cast one's eyes around
