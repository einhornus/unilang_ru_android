кок (14975)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Dutch kok ("cook")

Noun

кок • (kok) m anim or m inan (genitive ко́ка, nominative plural ко́ки, genitive plural ко́ков)

  1. ship cook
  2. quiff (hair style)

Etymology 2

Noun
