кишла́к (28965)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Turkic, cognates include Turkish kışlak, Azerbaijani qışlaq.

Noun[edit]

кишла́к • (kišlák) | m | inan (genitive кишлака́, nominative plural кишлаки́, genitive plural кишлако́в)

  1. qishlaq, kishlak (a rural settlement of nomadic people)
  2. (Central Asia, | Azerbaijan, | Afghanistan, | etc.) village, hamlet, a small settlement
