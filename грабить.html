гра́бить (6796)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *gràbiti.

Verb

гра́бить • (grábitʹ) impf (perfective огра́бить)

  1. to rob, to sack, to loot, to plunder

Related terms

  • грабёж (grabjóž)
