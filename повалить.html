повали́ть (5823)


Etymology

по- (po-) + | вали́ть (valítʹ, “to cut, to throw, to fell”)

Verb

повали́ть • (povalítʹ) pf (imperfective вали́ть)

  1. to bring down, to topple, to overthrow

Verb

повали́ть • (povalítʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) (people) to begin to throng; (snow) to begin to fall heavily, to begin to fall in thick flakes; (smoke) to begin to belch
