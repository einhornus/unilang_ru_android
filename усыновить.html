усынови́ть (19568)


Verb[edit]

усынови́ть • (usynovítʹ) | pf (imperfective усыновлять)

  1. to adopt, to affiliate (to take by choice into relationship as child)

Usage notes[edit]

  • The term is derived from сын (syn, “son”), so originally it meant to make someone one's son. It is also used as a generic term for adoption. To refer to female adoption specifically, use удочерить (udočerítʹ)
