горн (14987)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *gъrnъ.

Noun[edit]

горн • (gorn) | m | inan (genitive го́рна, nominative plural го́рны, genitive plural го́рнов)

  1. fireplace, hearth
  2. forge, furnace
Related terms[edit]
  • гончар (gončár)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from German Horn.

Noun[edit]

горн • (gorn) | m | inan (genitive го́рна, nominative plural го́рны, genitive plural го́рнов)

  1. bugle, clarion, horn
