пика́п (22571)


Etymology

Borrowed from English pickup.

Noun

пика́п • (pikáp) m inan (genitive пика́па, nominative plural пика́пы, genitive plural пика́пов, diminutive пика́пчик)

  1. pickup truck (truck with an open cargo bed)
  2. pickup (seduction technique, especially as practiced by pickup artists)
