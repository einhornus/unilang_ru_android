зя́бнуть (25025)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *zębnǫti.

Verb[edit]

зя́бнуть • (zjábnutʹ) | impf (perfective озя́бнуть)

  1. to be cold, to freeze, to feel cold
