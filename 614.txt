<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">overdose</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">overdoses</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>An excessive and dangerous dose of a drug.
<dl><dd><i>die of a heroin <b>overdose</b>.</i></dd></dl></li></ol>
<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Verb">Verb</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
