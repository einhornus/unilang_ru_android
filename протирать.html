протира́ть (8411)


Etymology[edit]

про- (pro-) + | -тирать (-tirátʹ)

Verb[edit]

протира́ть • (protirátʹ) | impf (perfective протере́ть)

  1. to wear through, to wear into holes, to fray, to rub a hole (in)
  2. to wipe, to rub
    протира́ть глаза ― protirátʹ glazá ― to rub one's eyes open
  3. to rub through, to grate

Related terms[edit]

  • протёртый (protjórtyj)
  • тёртый (tjórtyj)
  • тёрка (tjórka)
