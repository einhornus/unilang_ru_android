mortality (4111)


mor-tal-i-ty

Существи́тельное.

Ко́рень: -mortal-.

Значе́ние

  1. неисч. сме́ртность; подверженность сме́рти | Is the inevitability of our mortality the ultimate source of existential suffering?
  2. неисч. сме́ртность; коли́чество слу́чаев сме́рти | High ambient temperature is associated with a significant increase in mortality from cocaine overdose.
  3. исч. слу́чай сме́рти; собы́тие сме́рти | Two hundred two mortalities from blunt trauma from the Coroner's Office of Ontario were reviewed and compared to 102 mortalities seen at the Regional Trauma Unit at Sunnybrook Medical Centre, Toronto, and its 36 referring hospitals (EMSS).
