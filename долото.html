долото́ (27621)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *dolto.

Noun[edit]

долото́ • (dolotó) | n | inan (genitive долота, nominative plural долота, genitive plural доло́т, diminutive долотцо)

  1. chisel
  2. boring bit, drill bit, gouge, rock-drill
