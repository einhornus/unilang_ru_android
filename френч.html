френч (13806)


Etymology

Named after Field Marshal John Denton French, British officer during the Second Boer War and First World War.

Noun

френч • (frenč) m inan (genitive фре́нча, nominative plural фре́нчи, genitive plural фре́нчей)

  1. service jacket (single-breasted jacket with a collar stand and four external pockets)
