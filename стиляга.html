стиля́га (19271)


Etymology[edit]

стиль (stilʹ, “style, fashion”) + | -яга (-jága)

Noun[edit]

стиля́га • (stiljága) | m | anim or f | anim (genitive стиля́ги, nominative plural стиля́ги, genitive plural стиля́г)

  1. (derogatory) dandy, fop, fashionista; member of the stilyagi youth subculture of the 1940s–1960s
