вандали́зм (31707)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French vandalisme.

Noun[edit]

вандали́зм • (vandalízm) | m | inan (genitive вандали́зма, nominative plural вандали́змы, genitive plural вандали́змов)

  1. vandalism
  2. indifference to the arts, lack of taste
  3. (figuratively) immoral action

Related terms[edit]

  • ванда́л (vandál)
