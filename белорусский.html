белору́сский (6784)


Etymology

From белору́ссия (Belorússija)

Adjective

белору́сский • (belorússkij)

  1. Belorussian (relating to Belorussia, former state of the Soviet Union)
  2. Belarusian (relating to Belarus, current nation formed from the same territory)

Noun

белору́сский • (belorússkij) m inan (genitive белору́сского, uncountable)

  1. Belarusian language
