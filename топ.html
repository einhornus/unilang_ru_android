топ (11725)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Dutch top, English top, or Low German Topp.

Noun[edit]

топ • (top) | m | inan (genitive то́па, nominative plural то́пы, genitive plural то́пов, diminutive топик)

  1. top (of the mast)
  2. cheese, note (pressed mass of tobacco in tobacco cutting machine)

Etymology 2[edit]

Onomatopoeic.

Interjection[edit]

топ • (top)

  1. stamp (sound of stamping when walking)
