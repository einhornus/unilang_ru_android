балл (10547)


Etymology

Borrowed from French balle.

Noun

балл • (ball) m inan (genitive ба́лла, nominative plural ба́ллы, genitive plural ба́ллов)

  1. point
  2. mark, grade, score
  3. (historical) a ball used in the process of voting
