си́нька (27249)


Noun

си́нька • (sínʹka) f inan (genitive си́ньки, nominative plural си́ньки, genitive plural си́нек)

  1. bluing (blue pigment used for coloring clothes when washing)
  2. blueprint (paper-based reproduction usually of a technical drawing), diazo print, ammonia print
  3. (colloquial) drunkenness, drinking
    замёрз по си́ньке ― zamjórz po sínʹke ― he got drunk and froze to death

Synonyms

  • диазокопия (diazokopija)

Related terms

  • си́ний (sínij)
