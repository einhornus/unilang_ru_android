се́меро (9736)


Numeral[edit]

се́меро • (sémero)

  1. seven (in a group together), seven of them
    се́меро дете́й ― sémero detéj ― seven children
    се́меро часо́в ― sémero časóv ― seven watches
    се́меро очко́в ― sémero očkóv ― seven pairs of glasses
    се́меро из нас ― sémero iz nas ― seven of us
    нас бы́ло се́меро ― nas býlo sémero ― there were seven of us

Usage notes[edit]

The nominative of a collective numeral governs the genitive plural of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. The other cases agree with that of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. Not used with feminine nouns. Used mostly when speaking of children and with a few nouns that are used in the plural only, such as часы́, очки́, or with pronouns.
