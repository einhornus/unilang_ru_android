помяну́ть (9403)


Verb[edit]

помяну́ть • (pomjanútʹ) | pf (imperfective помина́ть)

  1. (dated) to remember, to recall
  2. (colloquial) to mention
  3. (religion) to pray for the health living or the repose of the soul of the dead, to commemorate
