вале́т (12130)


Etymology

Borrowed from French valet (“servant”).

Noun

вале́т • (valét) m anim (genitive вале́та or вальта́*, nominative plural вале́ты or вальты́*, genitive plural вале́тов or вальто́в*) (* Colloquial.)

  1. (card games) jack (playing card)
