хмеле́ть (32453)


Etymology[edit]

хмель (xmelʹ) + | -еть (-étʹ)

Verb[edit]

хмеле́ть • (xmelétʹ) | impf (perfective охмелеть or захмеле́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to become intoxicated, to drunk, to tipsy or high
  2. (figuratively, | colloquial) to become exhilarated

Related terms[edit]

  • хмель (xmelʹ), хмелёк (xmeljók)
  • хмельной (xmelʹnój), хмелное (xmelnóje)
