front (785)


Etymology

From Old French front, from Latin frontem, accusative singular of frōns, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰron-t-, from *bʰren- (“project”).

Noun

front m (plural fronts)

  1. forehead
  2. (military) front, frontline

Related terms
