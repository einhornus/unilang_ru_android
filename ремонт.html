ремо́нт (3192)


Etymology[edit]

From French remonter.

Noun[edit]

ремо́нт • (remónt) | m | inan (genitive ремо́нта, nominative plural ремо́нты, genitive plural ремо́нтов)

  1. repair, overhaul
  2. maintenance
  3. mending, renovation, refurbishment
  4. refit
  5. remount service

Related terms[edit]

  • ремонти́ровать (remontírovatʹ)
