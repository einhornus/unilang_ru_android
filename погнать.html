погна́ть (6444)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) + | гнать (gnatʹ)

Verb[edit]

погна́ть • (pognátʹ) | pf (imperfective гнать)

  1. to drive
  2. (slang) to lie

Usage notes[edit]

Погна́ть and гнать (gnatʹ) are in the class of Russian concrete verbs. Their counterparts, погоня́ть (pogonjátʹ) and гоня́ть (gonjátʹ), are in the class of Russian abstract verbs.
