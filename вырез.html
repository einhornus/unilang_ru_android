вы́рез (17656)


Noun

вы́рез • (výrez) m inan (genitive вы́реза, nominative plural вы́резы, genitive plural вы́резов)

  1. cut, cutting
  2. cutout
  3. low neck, decollete
  4. (mathematics) excision

Related terms

  • выреза́ть (vyrezatʹ)
