снаря́д (2195)


Noun[edit]

снаря́д • (snarjád) | m | inan (genitive снаря́да, nominative plural снаря́ды, genitive plural снаря́дов)

  1. (military, | weaponry) shell, missile, projectile
  2. apparatus
  3. tool, equipment
  4. tackle
