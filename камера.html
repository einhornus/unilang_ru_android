ка́мера (1246)


Etymology[edit]

From Latin camera.

Noun[edit]

ка́мера • (kámera) | f | inan (genitive ка́меры, nominative plural ка́меры, genitive plural ка́мер)

  1. (photography) camera
  2. chamber, cell, compartment
  3. prison cell, ward
  4. room, office
  5. inner tube (of a tire)
  6. bladder (of a ball)
  7. barrel (of a pump)

Related terms[edit]

  • ка́мерный (kámernyj)
