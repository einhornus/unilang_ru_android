ча́кра (11172)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Sanskrit चक्र (cakra, “wheel, circle, point”).

Noun[edit]

ча́кра • (čákra) | f | inan (genitive ча́кры, nominative plural ча́кры, genitive plural ча́кр)

  1. chakra (spiritual energy nexuses)
  2. chakram (Indian throwing weapon)
