этот (30)


Etymology

тотъ (tot) with the expressive prefix э- (e-) + | , winning over сей (sej) in the nineteenth century.

Determiner

э́тот • (étot) m (demonstrative)

  1. proximal demonstrative adjective and pronoun, this

Synonyms

  • сей (sej) (dated or poetic)

Related terms
