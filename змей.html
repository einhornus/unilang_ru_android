змей (4609)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *zmьjь. Doublet of змий (zmij), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

Noun[edit]

змей • (zmej) | m | anim (genitive змея, nominative plural змеи, genitive plural зме́ев)

  1. (mythology) serpent, dragon
  2. (figuratively) snake (crafty, wily, evil man)
  3. kite (flying toy)
Related terms[edit]
  • возду́шный змей (vozdúšnyj zmej)
  • зелёный змий (zeljónyj zmij)
