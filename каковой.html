каково́й (8750)


Etymology[edit]

как (kak) + | -овой (-ovój)

Determiner[edit]

каково́й • (kakovój)

  1. (obsolete) which; such as
  2. what sort of?

Usage notes[edit]

  • The long forms are obsolete but the short forms are in common use.

Synonyms[edit]

  • како́й (kakój)
  • кото́рый (kotóryj)

Related terms[edit]

  • как (kak)
  • каково́ (kakovó)
