лот (17641)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Lot, from Middle High German lōt, from Proto-Germanic *laudą, from Proto-Celtic *loudom. Cognate with English lead (type of metal).

Noun

лот • (lot) m inan (genitive ло́та, nominative plural ло́ты, genitive plural ло́тов)

  1. (nautical) plummet, sounding line
  2. lot (old Russian unit of mass equal to 12.8 grams)
Derived terms
  • ло́товый (lótovyj)
  • лотово́й (lotovój)
