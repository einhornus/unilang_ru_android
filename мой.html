мой (53)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *mojь.

Pronoun[edit]

мой • (moj)

  1. (possessive) my, mine
    «Моя Москва» ― “Mojá Moskvá” ― "My Moscow"

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

мой • (moj)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of мыть (mytʹ)
