бели́ла (20670)


Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

бели́ла • (belíla) | n | inan | pl (genitive бели́л, plural only)

  1. ceruse, whitewash, whiting

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

бели́ла • (belíla)

  1. feminine singular past indicative imperfective of бели́ть (belítʹ)
