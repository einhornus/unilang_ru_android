войти́ (396)


Etymology[edit]

во- (vo-) + | -йти (-jtí)

Verb[edit]

войти́ • (vojtí) | pf (imperfective входи́ть)

  1. to enter, to go in(to) (by foot), to get in(to)
  2. to penetrate into
  3. to be included in
  4. (computing) to login

Related terms[edit]

  • вход (vxod)
