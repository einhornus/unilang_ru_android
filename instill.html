instill


= instil _v.
1 влива́ть по ка́пле (into)
2 _мед. пуска́ть по ка́пле
3 и́сподволь внуша́ть; вселя́ть (наде́жду, страх и т. п.) (in/into, with) Parents try to instil in(to) their children the best of moral principles. We must try to instil some order into the organization. It is easy to instil the minds of young children with fixed ideas, whether right or wrong.
