про́пись (28480)


Etymology[edit]

прописа́ть (propisátʹ) + | -ь (-ʹ)

Noun[edit]

про́пись • (própisʹ) | f | inan (genitive про́писи, nominative plural про́писи, genitive plural про́писей)

  1. (medicine) prescription
  2. (dated, | usually in the plural) cursive sample sheet
  3. (dated, | figuratively) cliche, platitude
  4. handwriting among printed text
  5. font imitating handwriting, such as true italics
  6. (painting) preliminary drawing on canvas, used to guide the finished painting
