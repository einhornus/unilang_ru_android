ка́чка (14051)


Etymology 1

Noun

ка́чка • (káčka) f inan (genitive ка́чки, nominative plural ка́чки, genitive plural ка́чек)

  1. (nautical) rolling, pitching
  2. (ornithology, regional) duck
Synonyms
  • у́тка (útka)

Etymology 2

Noun

качка́ • (kačká) m anim

  1. genitive singular of качо́к (kačók)
  2. accusative singular of качо́к (kačók)
