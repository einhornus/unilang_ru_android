Adjective[edit]

чу́ткий • (čútkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)чутче)

  1. sensitive (of a person, instrument, etc.)
  2. sharp, keen (sense of hearing, smell, etc.)
    чу́ткая соба́ка ― čútkaja sobáka ― keen-nosed dog
  3. sympathetic, thoughtful, tactful
    чу́ткий подхо́д ― čútkij podxód ― tactful approach
  4. light (sleep)
    чу́ткий сон ― čútkij son ― light sleep
