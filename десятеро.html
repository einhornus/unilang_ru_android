де́сятеро (27804)


Numeral[edit]

де́сятеро • (désjatero)

  1. ten (in a group together), ten of them
    де́сятеро дете́й ― désjatero detéj ― ten children
    де́сятеро часо́в ― désjatero časóv ― ten watches
    де́сятеро очко́в ― désjatero očkóv ― ten pairs of glasses
    де́сятеро из нас ― désjatero iz nas ― ten of us
    нас бы́ло де́сятеро ― nas býlo désjatero ― there were ten of us

Usage notes[edit]

The nominative of a collective numeral governs the genitive plural of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. The other cases agree with that of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. Not used with feminine nouns. Used mostly when speaking of children and with a few nouns that are used in the plural only, such as часы́, очки́, or with pronouns.
