ке́мпинг (28222)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English camping.

Noun[edit]

ке́мпинг • (kɛmpinɡ) | m | inan (genitive ке́мпинга, nominative plural ке́мпинги, genitive plural ке́мпингов)|or|кемпинг//kémping|}}

  1. camping ground, campground, campsite, camp
  2. camping
