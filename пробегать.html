пробега́ть (7444)


Etymology 1

про- (pro-) + | бе́гать (bégatʹ) + | -а́ть (-átʹ)

Verb

пробега́ть • (probegátʹ) impf (perfective пробежа́ть)

  1. to run past, to run through
  2. to run a certain distance or for some time
  3. to skim, to scan, to look through

Etymology 2

про- (pro-) + | бе́гать (bégatʹ)

Verb

пробе́гать • (probégatʹ) pf (imperfective бе́гать)

  1. to spend some time running
