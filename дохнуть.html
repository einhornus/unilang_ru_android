до́хнуть (12849)


Etymology 1[edit]

Ultimately from the same source as до́хнуть (doxnútʹ) below.

Verb[edit]

до́хнуть • (dóxnutʹ) | impf (perfective сдо́хнуть or издохнуть or подо́хнуть)

  1. (derogatory, | slang) to croak (to die)

Etymology 2[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *dъxnǫti.

Verb[edit]

до́хнуть • (doxnútʹ) | pf (imperfective дыхать)

  1. to take a breath, to breathe
  2. (figuratively) to blow (of a wind), to have a (single) blow
