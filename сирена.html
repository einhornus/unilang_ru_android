сире́на (6382)


Noun[edit]

сире́на • (siréna) | f | inan or f | anim (genitive сире́ны, nominative plural сире́ны, genitive plural сире́н)

  1. (mythology, | animate) siren
  2. (mythology, | inanimate) siren (device)
