то́щий (4398)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tъščь, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *tuskjas, from Proto-Indo-European *tus-sḱ-yós, from *tews- (“to empty”), from *tew- (“to swell”). Cognate with Polish czczy, Lithuanian tùščias, Latvian tukšs, Vedic Sanskrit तुच्छ्य (tucchyá), Persian تهی‎ | (tohi).

Adjective[edit]

то́щий • (tóščij) (comparative (по)то́щее or (по)то́щей)

  1. gaunt, scrawny, thin

Related terms[edit]

  • тоска́ (toská)
