ма́терный (14389)


Etymology

From ма́тер- (máter-) + | -ный (-nyj), based off of the oblique stem of мать (matʹ, “mother”); compare мат (mat, “obscene language”), derived from the same word.

Adjective

ма́терный • (máternyj)

  1. (colloquial) obscene, bad, dirty, foul

Related terms

  • мат m (mat)
  • матерщи́на f (materščína)
  • мать f (matʹ)
