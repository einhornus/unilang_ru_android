па́пка (2147)


Etymology 1[edit]

па́па (pápa, “dad”) + | -ка (-ka).

Noun[edit]

па́пка • (pápka) | m | anim (genitive па́пки, nominative plural па́пки, genitive plural па́пок)

  1. daddy
Related terms[edit]
  • па́па (pápa)
  • па́почка (pápočka)
  • ба́тя (bátja)
  • оте́ц (otéc)

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from German Pappe (“cardboard”) + | -ка (-ka).

Noun[edit]

па́пка • (pápka) | f | inan (genitive па́пки, nominative plural па́пки, genitive plural па́пок)

  1. folder (an organizer for storing papers)
  2. file (things stored in a folder)
  3. (computing) folder (directory or virtual container in a file system)
