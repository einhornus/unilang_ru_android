пантало́ны (24694)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French pantalons.

Noun[edit]

пантало́ны • (pantalóny) | f | inan | pl (genitive пантало́н, plural only)

  1. (plural only) long underwear, long johns, that is worn either under normal clothing in cold weather, or as a night dress.
