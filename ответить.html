отве́тить (187)


Etymology[edit]

отве́т (otvét) + | -ить (-itʹ)

Verb[edit]

отве́тить • (otvétitʹ) | pf (imperfective отвеча́ть)

  1. to answer, to reply
    • 1886, Anton Chekhov, “The Chorus Girl”, in (Please provide the book title or journal name) | republished in Русский язы́к, 2002:
      Ба́рыня не сра́зу отве́тила.
      Bárynja ne srázu otvétila.
      The lady did not answer right away.
  2. to be responsible for, to be accountable for, to be liable for
  3. to account for, to answer for
  4. to be held to account for, to be called to account for

Related terms[edit]

  • отве́тственный (otvétstvennyj)
  • отве́тственность | f (otvétstvennostʹ)
