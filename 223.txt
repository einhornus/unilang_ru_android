<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">deer</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><strong class="selflink">deer</strong></b> <i>or</i> <span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content qualifier-content">nonstandard</span><span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">)</span> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">deers</b>)
</p>
<div class="sister-wikipedia sister-project noprint floatright" style="border: 1px solid aaa; font-size: 90%; background: f9f9f9; width: 250px; padding: 4px; text-align: left;"><div style="float: left;"><div class="floatnone"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/44px-Wikipedia-logo.png" decoding="async" width="44" height="44" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/66px-Wikipedia-logo.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/88px-Wikipedia-logo.png 2x" data-file-width="200" data-file-height="200" /></div></div><div style="margin-left: 60px;">English Wikipedia has an article on:<div style="margin-left: 10px;"><b class="Latn" lang="en"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/deer" class="extiw" title="w:deer">deer</a></b></div></div><span class="interProject"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/deer" class="extiw" title="w:deer">Wikipedia </a></span></div>
<ol><li>a ruminant mammal with antlers and hooves of the family Cervidae, or one of several similar animals from related families of the order Artiodactyla</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">in particular</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> one of the smaller animals of this family, distinguished from a <i>moose</i> or <i>elk</i>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">I wrecked my car after a <b>deer</b> ran across the road.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>the meat of such an animal; venison.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Oh, I've never had <b>deer</b> before.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">obsolete<span class="ib-comma">,</span> except in the phrase "small deer"</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> an animal, especially a quadrupedal mammal, as opposed to a bird, fish, etc.
<ul><li><b>1606</b>, William Shakespeare, <i>King Lear</i>, Act III. IV
<dl><dd>But mice and rats and such small <b>deer</b>, have been Tom's food for seven long year.</dd></dl></li></ul></li></ol>
