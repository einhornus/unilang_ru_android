мотострелко́вый (13145)


Etymology

мото- (moto-) + | стрелко́вый (strelkóvyj)

Adjective

мотострелко́вый • (motostrelkóvyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) motorized rifle (equipped with automatic weapons and armored vehicles highly capable of moving cross-country)
    101-й мотострелко́вый полк ― 101-j motostrelkóvyj polk ― 101st Motorized Rifle Regiment (military unit in the Soviet army)
