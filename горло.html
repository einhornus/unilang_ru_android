го́рло (1360)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic гърло (gŭrlo), from Proto-Slavic *gъrdlo.

Noun[edit]

го́рло • (górlo) | n | inan (genitive го́рла, nominative plural го́рла, genitive plural го́рл)

  1. throat
  2. gullet
  3. neck (of a vessel)
