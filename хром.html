хром (26205)


Etymology 1[edit]

Chemical element: Cr (atomic number 24)

Noun[edit]

хром • (xrom) | m | inan (genitive хрома, nominative plural хро́мы, genitive plural хро́мов, related adjective хро́мовый)

  1. chromium, Cr
  2. chrome
  3. box calf (a chrome-tanned calf skin)
