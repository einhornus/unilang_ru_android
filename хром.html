хром (26207)


Etymology 1

Chemical element: Cr (atomic number 24)

Noun

хром • (xrom) m inan (genitive хро́ма, nominative plural хро́мы, genitive plural хро́мов, related adjective хро́мовый)

  1. chromium, Cr
  2. chrome
  3. box calf (a chrome-tanned calf skin)
