москви́ч (2697)


Noun[edit]

москви́ч • (moskvíč) | m | anim or m | inan (genitive москвича́, nominative plural москвичи́, genitive plural москвиче́й, feminine москви́чка)

  1. Muscovite (resident of Moscow)
  2. Moskvich (a common private passenger car)

Related terms[edit]
