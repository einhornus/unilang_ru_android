жре́бий (13989)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic жрѣбии (žrěbii), from Proto-Slavic *žerbьjь. Doublet of жеребей (žérebej), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun[edit]

жре́бий • (žrébij) | m | inan (genitive жре́бия, nominative plural жре́бии, genitive plural жре́биев)

  1. lot
  2. fate, destiny
