разре́зать (7068)


Etymology 1[edit]

раз- (raz-) + | ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Verb[edit]

разре́зать • (razrézatʹ) | pf (imperfective разре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to bisect

Etymology 2[edit]

разре́зать (razrézatʹ) + | -ать (-átʹ)

Verb[edit]

разре́зать • (razrezátʹ) | impf (perfective разре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to bisect
