молочко́ (18194)


Etymology[edit]

молоко́ (molokó) + | -ко (-kó)

Noun[edit]

молочко́ • (moločkó) | n | inan (genitive молочка́, uncountable)

  1. endearing form of молоко́ (molokó)
  2. (biology) jelly (a liquid that forms in the goiter or in the special glands of some birds and insects for feeding nestlings or larvae)
    ма́точное молочко́ ― mátočnoje moločkó ― royal jelly
  3. (cosmetics) cream
