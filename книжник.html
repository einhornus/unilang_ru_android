кни́жник (21693)


Etymology[edit]

кни́га (kníga) + | -ник (-nik)

Noun[edit]

кни́жник • (knížnik) | m | anim or m | inan (genitive кни́жника, nominative plural кни́жники, genitive plural кни́жников, feminine книжница)

  1. (colloquial) bibliophile, book lover
  2. (colloquial) bookseller
  3. (colloquial) bookish person, person with only book knowledge
  4. (biblical, | archaic) scribe (expert in sacred texts)
  5. (archaic, | inanimate) wallet

Related terms[edit]

  • кни́га (kníga), кни́жный (knížnyj), кни́жно (knížno), книжность (knížnostʹ)
  • кни́жка (knížka)
