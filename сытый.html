сы́тый (4840)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *sytъ (“satiated, full”), from Proto-Indo-European *s(e)h₂tos, from *seh₂- (“to satiate”).

Adjective[edit]

сы́тый • (sýtyj) (comparative (по)сытее or (по)сытей)

  1. satiated
  2. (figuratively) satisfied, full, sated

Related terms[edit]
