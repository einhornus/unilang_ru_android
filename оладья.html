ола́дья (23373)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic ола́дья (oladĭja), from Ancient Greek ἐλάδιον (eládion), diminutive of ἔλαιον (élaion, “oil, olive-oil”).

Noun[edit]

ола́дья • (oládʹja) | f | inan (genitive ола́дьи, nominative plural ола́дьи, genitive plural ола́дий, diminutive оладик)

  1. thick pancake

Related terms[edit]

  • оладушек | m (oládušek)
