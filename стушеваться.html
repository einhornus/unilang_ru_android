стушева́ться (24956)


Etymology[edit]

с- (s-) + | тушева́ться (tuševátʹsja)

Verb[edit]

стушева́ться • (stuševátʹsja) | pf (imperfective тушева́ться or стушёвываться)

  1. (colloquial) to become unnoticeable
  2. (colloquial) to efface oneself, to retire to the background
  3. (colloquial) to become embarrassed
  4. (colloquial, | dated) to go away, to retire
