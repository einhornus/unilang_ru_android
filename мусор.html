Noun

  1. garbage, debris, refuse

Etymology 2

From the pre-1917 acronym Мус́ (MUS) for моско́вский уголо́вный сыск (Moskóvskij ugolóvnyj sysk, “Moscow Criminal Detective Department”).

Noun

му́сор • (músor) m anim (genitive му́сора, nominative plural мусора́, genitive plural мусоро́в)

  1. (slang, derogatory) cop, pig (policeman)
Synonyms
  • (cop, pejorative): мент (ment), лега́вый (legávyj), фарао́н (faraón)
  • (policeman, neutral term): полице́йский (policéjskij), милиционе́р (milicionér)
