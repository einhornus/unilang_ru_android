ткнуть (3554)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *tъknǫti.

Verb[edit]

ткнуть • (tknutʹ) | pf (imperfective ты́кать)

  1. to poke, to jab, to stick, to prod
  2. to hit

Related terms[edit]

  • тычо́к (tyčók)
