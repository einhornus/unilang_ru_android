compulsory (6373)


Значе́ние

  1. наси́льственный, принуди́тельный
    • The paper demonstrates that the abolition of slavery as an institution has not brought an end to compulsory labor.
  2. обяза́тельный
    • Compulsory education is education which children are required by law to receive and governments to provide.
    • For more than 80 years, Australia's compulsory voting system requires that people show up to their polling place on election day.
