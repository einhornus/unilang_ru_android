лялька (12569)


Etymology[edit]

From Ukrainian лялька (ljálʹka), cognates include Belarusian лялька (ljálʹka), Polish lalka.

Noun[edit]

лялька • (ljálʹka) | f | anim or f | inan (genitive ляльки, nominative plural ляльки, genitive plural лялек)

  1. (regional, | Ukraine, | Southern Russia) doll, puppet (a toy in the form of a human)
    Synonym: ку́кла (kúkla)
  2. (colloquial) baby
    Synonym: младе́нец (mladénec)
