лялька (12571)


Etymology

From Ukrainian ля́лька (ljálʹka), cognates include Belarusian ля́лька (ljálʹka), Polish lalka.

Noun

ля́лька • (ljálʹka) f anim or f inan (genitive ля́льки, nominative plural ля́льки, genitive plural ля́лек)

  1. (regional, Ukraine, Southern Russia) doll, puppet (a toy in the form of a human)
    Synonym: ку́кла (kúkla)
  2. (colloquial) baby
    Synonym: младе́нец (mladénec)
