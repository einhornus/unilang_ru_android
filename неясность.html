нея́сность (21358)


Etymology

нея́сный (nejásnyj) + | -ость (-ostʹ)

Noun

нея́сность • (nejásnostʹ) f inan (genitive нея́сности, nominative plural нея́сности, genitive plural нея́сностей)

  1. ambiguity (something liable to more than one interpretation)
  2. obscurity, opacity
  3. haziness, fuzziness, indistinctness
  4. nebulosity
