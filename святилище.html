святи́лище (28645)


Etymology

свято́й (svjatój) + | -и́лище (-ílišče)

Noun

святи́лище • (svjatílišče) n inan (genitive святи́лища, nominative plural святи́лища, genitive plural святи́лищ)

  1. (dated) church (in Orthodox Christianity)
  2. (figuratively) sanctuary (a revered place)
  3. (religion) holy place (in general)

Related terms

  • свято́й (svjatój)
  • свя́тость f (svjátostʹ)
