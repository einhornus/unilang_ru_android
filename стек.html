стек (25745)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from English stick.

Noun[edit]

стек • (stɛk) | m | inan (genitive сте́ка, nominative plural сте́ки, genitive plural сте́ков)

  1. riding crop

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from English stack.

Noun[edit]

стек • (stɛk) | m | inan (genitive сте́ка, nominative plural сте́ки, genitive plural сте́ков)

  1. (computing) stack (data structure)

Etymology 3[edit]

Borrowed from Italian stecca.

Noun[edit]

стек • (stɛk) | m | inan (genitive сте́ка, nominative plural сте́ки, genitive plural сте́ков)

  1. (scultping) Alternative form of сте́ка (stɛka)

Etymology 4[edit]

Noun[edit]

стек • (stɛk) | f | inan | pl

  1. genitive plural of сте́ка (stɛka)
