софа́ (15319)


Etymology

From French sofa or Italian sofà, ultimately from Arabic صُفَّة | (ṣuffa, “a long seat made of stone or brick”).

Noun

софа́ • (sofá) f inan (genitive софы́, nominative plural со́фы, genitive plural со́ф)

  1. sofa

Synonyms

  • дива́н (diván)
