зае́сть (11401)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) + | есть (jestʹ)

Verb[edit]

зае́сть • (zajéstʹ) | pf (imperfective заеда́ть)

  1. to jam, to stick (e.g. of equipment or zippers) (also impersonal)
    ключ в за́мке зае́ст ― ključ v zamké zajést ― the key is sticking in the lock
  2. to eat after a drink of alcohol or medicine
    зае́сть лека́рство са́харом ― zajéstʹ lekárstvo sáxarom ― to take sugar after the medicine
  3. to kill by eating to death
  4. (figuratively) to torment
