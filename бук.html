бук (23861)


Etymology 1

бук

From Proto-Slavic *buky, borrowed from Proto-West Germanic *bōkijā (“beech”).

Noun

бук • (buk) m inan (genitive бу́ка, nominative plural бу́ки, genitive plural бу́ков)

  1. beech
Related terms
  • бу́ковый (bukovyj)

Etymology 2

Noun

бук • (buk) f anim pl

  1. genitive plural of бу́ка (búka)
  2. accusative plural of бу́ка (búka)
