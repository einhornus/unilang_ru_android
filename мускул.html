му́скул (6357)


Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Latin musculus.

Noun[edit]

му́скул • (múskul) | m | inan (genitive му́скула, nominative plural му́скулы, genitive plural му́скулов)

  1. (anatomy) muscle
  2. (computing, | slang) MySQL

Synonyms[edit]

  • мы́шца (mýšca)

Related terms[edit]

  • мускулату́ра (muskulatúra)
  • мускулистый (muskulístyj)
  • мускульный (muskulʹnyj)
