варе́ник (29821)


Noun[edit]

варе́ник • (varénik) | m | inan (genitive варе́ника, nominative plural варе́ники, genitive plural варе́ников)

  1. a varenik/varenyk; a single vareniki/varenyky dumpling

Usage notes[edit]

  • This word is more commonly used in the plural, where it refers to the dish itself.
  • The dish is considered Ukrainian but Russians make it quite often and consider it their own.

Related terms[edit]

  • вареничная (varéničnaja, “vareniki/varenyky restaurant”)
