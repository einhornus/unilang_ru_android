обре́зать (12421)


Etymology 1[edit]

об- (ob-) + | ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Verb[edit]

обре́зать • (obrézatʹ) | pf (imperfective обре́зать or обрезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to clip, to pare, to trim, to prune, to square
    обре́зать но́гти ― obrézatʹ nógti ― pare one's fingernails
  2. to cut
    обре́зать па́лец ― obrézatʹ pálec ― to cut one's finger
  3. to circumcise
  4. (colloquial) to snub, to cut short

Etymology 2[edit]

обре́зать (obrézatʹ) + | -ать (-átʹ)

Verb[edit]

обре́зать • (obrezátʹ) | impf (perfective обре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to clip, to pare, to trim, to prune, to square
    обре́зать но́гти ― obrezátʹ nógti ― pare one's finger-nails
  2. to cut
    обре́зать па́лец ― obrezátʹ pálec ― to cut one's finger
  3. to circumcise
  4. (colloquial) to snub, to cut short

Related terms[edit]
