распра́виться (9859)


Etymology[edit]

распра́вить (rasprávitʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

распра́виться • (rasprávitʹsja) | pf (imperfective расправля́ться)

  1. to beat violently, to punish
  2. to get rid of (someone) by destroying them or rendering them harmless
  3. (figuratively, | colloquial) to deal with (something), to handle (something), to manage (something)
    распра́виться без суда́ ― rasprávitʹsja bez sudá ― to take the law into one's own hands
  4. (figuratively, | colloquial) to eat up (something) without leaving a trace
  5. to get smoothed out, to even out
  6. to become straight, to straighten out
    пле́чи распра́вились ― pléči rasprávilisʹ ― one's shoulders straightened up
  7. passive of распра́вить (rasprávitʹ)
