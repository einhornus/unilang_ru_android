иноро́дец (30185)


Etymology

From иноро́дный (inoródnyj) + | -ец (-ec).

Noun

иноро́дец • (inoródec) m anim (genitive иноро́дца, nominative plural иноро́дцы, genitive plural иноро́дцев)

  1. Non-Russian (the designation of minority nations in imperial Russia; usually of nomadic peoples of the East, such as Buryats, Kalmyks, Kirghiz, Yakuts, etc.)
