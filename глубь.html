глубь (6347)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *glǫb, from Proto-Indo-European *glewbʰ-, whence English cleave, cleft and glyph.

Noun

глубь • (glubʹ) f inan (genitive глу́би, uncountable)

  1. depth
    в глубь страны́ ― v glubʹ straný ― (towards) inland

Related terms

  • глубина́ (glubina)
