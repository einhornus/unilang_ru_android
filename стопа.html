стопа́ (9083)


Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

стопа́ • (stopá) | f | inan (genitive стопы́, nominative plural стопы́, genitive plural стоп)

  1. (anatomy) foot
  2. footstep
Usage notes[edit]

Russian usually does not distinguish between "leg" and "foot," and нога́ (nogá) is used for both. Use стопа́ only when it is vital to make the distinction, as in medical situations.

Synonyms[edit]
  • нога́ (nogá)
  • ступня́ (stupnjá)
  • шаг (šag)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

стопа́ • (stopá) | f | inan (genitive стопы́, nominative plural стопы́, genitive plural стоп)

  1. foot, metric foot, tonic foot (of a verse)
  2. ream, pile (of paper)
Synonyms[edit]
  • ки́па (kípa)
