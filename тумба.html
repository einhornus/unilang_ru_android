ту́мба (12016)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Tumbe (“grave, coffin”), from Vulgar Latin tumba (“grave”).

Noun[edit]

ту́мба • (túmba) | f | inan (genitive ту́мбы, nominative plural ту́мбы, genitive plural ту́мб)

  1. cupboard, cabinet, pedestal
    ку́хонная ту́мба ― kúxonnaja túmba ― base unit
    ту́мба пи́сьменного стол ― túmba písʹmennovo stol ― desk cupboard
    прикроватная ту́мба ― prikrovatnaja túmba ― bedside table
  2. advertising pillar
  3. (historical) stone (to bind horses)
  4. (colloquial) fat/rotund person, tub
