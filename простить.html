прости́ть (781)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic простити (prostiti), from Proto slavic root *prostъ-. Cognate to просто́й.

Verb

прости́ть • (prostítʹ) pf (imperfective проща́ть)

  1. to excuse, to forgive, to pardon

Synonyms

  • извини́ть (izvinítʹ)

Derived terms
