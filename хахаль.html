ха́халь (22099)


Etymology[edit]

Probably of expressive origin, also reminiscent of words like кохати (“to love”).

Noun[edit]

ха́халь • (xáxalʹ) | m | anim (genitive ха́халя, nominative plural ха́хали, genitive plural ха́халей, feminine хахалишка)

  1. (low | | colloquial) suitor, lover
