сплета́ть (32273)


Etymology[edit]

сплет- (splet-) + | -ать (-átʹ)

Verb[edit]

сплета́ть • (spletátʹ) | impf (perfective сплести́)

  1. to weave (to create by weaving)
  2. (figuratively) to create (something complicated or confusing)
  3. to weave together, to interweave, to plait, to braid, to interlace
  4. (figuratively) to connect, to interlock
  5. (figuratively, | colloquial) to invent, to compose

Related terms[edit]
