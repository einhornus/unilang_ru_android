бло́чный (27768)


Etymology 1[edit]

блок (blok, “block (cuboid object)”) + | -ный (-nyj)

Adjective[edit]

бло́чный • (blóčnyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) block (cuboid object)
    бло́чное мя́со ― blóčnoje mjáso ― block meat (meat shaped in blocks)
  2. (relational) block (prefabricated piece of a building)
    бло́чный дом ― blóčnyj dom ― block house (house made of prefabricated blocks)
  3. blocky
Related terms[edit]
  • блок (blok)

Etymology 2[edit]

блок (blok, “pulley”) + | -ный (-nyj)

Adjective[edit]

бло́чный • (blóčnyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) pulley
    бло́чная верёвка ― blóčnaja verjóvka ― pulley rope
Related terms[edit]
  • блок (blok)
