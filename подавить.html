подави́ть (4314)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) + | дави́ть (davítʹ)

Verb[edit]

подави́ть • (podavítʹ) | pf (imperfective подавля́ть)

  1. to suppress, to repress, to quell, to stifle
  2. to depress, to overwhelm
  3. to press (several times or continuously)
