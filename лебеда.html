лебеда́ (21254)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *lebeda or *elbeda.

Noun[edit]

лебеда́ • (lebedá) | f | inan (genitive лебеды́, uncountable)

  1. orach, saltbush (Atriplex)
  2. goosefoot (Chenopodium)
