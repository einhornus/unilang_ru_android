парово́зик (31354)


Etymology[edit]

парово́з (parovóz) + | -ик (-ik)

Noun[edit]

парово́зик • (parovózik) | m | inan (genitive парово́зика, nominative plural парово́зики, genitive plural парово́зиков)

  1. Diminutive of парово́з (parovóz, “steam locomotive”)
  2. (colloquial) chain, procession (of people, cars, etc.)
  3. (slang) method of smoking drugs involving blowing smoke from one person's mouth to another

Related terms[edit]

  • парово́з (parovóz), парово́зный (parovóznyj)
  • паровозник (parovóznik)
