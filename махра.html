махра́ (26424)


Etymology 1

Shortening of махо́рка (maxórka).

Noun

махра́ • (maxrá) f inan (genitive махры́, uncountable)

  1. (low colloquial) makhorka (a strong type of tobacco)
Synonyms
  • махо́рка (maxórka)

Etymology 2

Originally мохра́ (moxrá), from мох (mox); respelled according to pronunciation.

Noun

махра́ • (maxrá) f inan (genitive махры́, uncountable, related adjective махро́вый)

  1. (colloquial) specially made thread loops on fabric or knitwear
  2. (colloquial) terrycloth
Derived terms
  • махро́вый (maxróvyj)
