брата́н (15604)


Noun[edit]

брата́н • (bratán) | m | anim (genitive братана́, nominative plural братаны́, genitive plural братано́в)

  1. (colloquial) older brother
  2. (colloquial) brother
  3. (colloquial) bro, a form of address to a friend
  4. (slang) a member of a братва́ (bratvá) (a criminal group)

Synonyms[edit]

  • брато́к (bratók)

Related terms[edit]

  • брат (brat)
