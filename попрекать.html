попрека́ть (24982)


Etymology

From попрёк

Verb

попрека́ть • (poprekátʹ) impf (perfective попрекну́ть)

  1. (disapprovingly): to reproach for not having gratitude for sth. done by the reproacher to the reproached
    свои́м хле́бом-солью попрека́ть грешно.
    Svoím xlébom-solʹju poprekátʹ grešno.
    It's sinful to reproach one with your hospitality.

Usage notes

  • Requires instrumental or за (za) + accusative case for the indirect object of reproaching.

Synonyms

  • укоря́ть (ukorjátʹ), упрека́ть (uprekátʹ)
