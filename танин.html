тани́н (22528)


Etymology 1

Perhaps borrowed from French tannin.

Noun

тани́н • (tanín) m inan (genitive тани́на, nominative plural тани́ны, genitive plural тани́нов)

  1. (chemistry) tannin
Derived terms
