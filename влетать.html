влета́ть (15191)


Etymology[edit]

в- (v-) + | лета́ть (letátʹ)

Verb[edit]

влета́ть • (vletátʹ) | impf (perfective влете́ть)

  1. to fly into
  2. (colloquial) to run into (to enter while running), to burst into
  3. (colloquial, | impersonal) to get into trouble
    ему́ влета́ет ― jemú vletájet ― he is getting into trouble

Synonyms[edit]

  • (run into): вбега́ть (vbegátʹ)
  • (get into trouble): достава́ться (dostavátʹsja), попа́дать (popadátʹ), нагорать (nagorátʹ)
