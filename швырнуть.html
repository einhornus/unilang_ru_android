швырну́ть (4053)


Verb

швырну́ть • (švyrnútʹ) pf (imperfective швыря́ть)

  1. to toss, to hurl, to throw, to chuck
  2. (by extension) to put things in disorder, to not where they belong (e.g. clothing)
  3. to fling (money)
