су́мерки (3350)


Etymology[edit]

Related to Old East Slavic суморокъ (sumorokŭ), with vocalism change. See also су́мрак (súmrak).

Noun[edit]

су́мерки • (súmerki) | f | inan | pl (genitive сумерек, plural only)

  1. dusk, twilight
