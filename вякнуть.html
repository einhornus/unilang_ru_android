вя́кнуть (21955)


Etymology[edit]

вя́кать (vjákatʹ) + | -нуть (-nutʹ)

Verb[edit]

вя́кнуть • (vjáknutʹ) | pf

  1. (low | | colloquial) to make noise (e.g. of a pet; once)
  2. (low | | colloquial) to complain, to whimper (once)
  3. (low | | colloquial) to blather, to talk nonsense (once)
  4. (low | | colloquial) to blurt out (to say without thinking) (once)

Related terms[edit]

  • вя́кать (vjákatʹ)
  • вяканье (vjákanʹje)
