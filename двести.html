две́сти (1995)


Etymology[edit]

An Old East Slavic dual form from the Old Church Slavic dual двѣстѣ (dvěstě).

Numeral[edit]

две́сти • (dvésti)

  1. two hundred (200)

Usage notes[edit]

две́сти (dvésti) in the nominative case governs the genitive plural of the noun.

  • две́сти ма́льчиков ― dvésti málʹčikov ― two hundred boys
  • ма́льчиков две́сти ― málʹčikov dvésti ― approximately two hundred boys
  • две́сти кни́г ― dvésti knig ― two hundred books
  • Они говори́ли о свои́х двухста́х солда́тах. ― Oní govoríli o svoíx dvuxstáx soldátax. ― They were talking about their two hundred soldiers.

Related terms[edit]
