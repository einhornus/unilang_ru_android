ло́джия (24149)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian loggia.

Noun

ло́джия • (lódžija) f inan (genitive ло́джии, nominative plural ло́джии, genitive plural ло́джий)

  1. recessed balcony
  2. loggia

Related terms

  • ко́мната (kómnata)
  • балко́н (balkón)
