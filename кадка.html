ка́дка (16288)


Etymology[edit]

From кадь (kadʹ) + | -ка (-ka), from Ancient Greek κᾰδος (kádos, “pail, jar, cask”).

Noun[edit]

ка́дка • (kádka) | f | inan (genitive ка́дки, nominative plural ка́дки, genitive plural ка́док)

  1. tub, vat
