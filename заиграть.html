заигра́ть (8473)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) + | игра́ть (igrátʹ)

Verb[edit]

заигра́ть • (zaigrátʹ) | pf (imperfective заи́грывать)

  1. to begin to play
  2. to begin to sparkle
  3. to look to advantage, to stand out
  4. (a record, tape recorder etc.) to spoil/wear out by playing
  5. to run down, to exhaust, to overuse, to overwear (by repetition)
  6. (colloquial) to appropriate, to help oneself (to), to hook

Related terms[edit]
