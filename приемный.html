приёмный (3002)


Etymology

приём (prijóm) + | -ный (-nyj)

Adjective

приёмный • (prijómnyj)

  1. (relational) reception, waiting (of a room, etc.)
  2. (relational) receiving, consulting
  3. (relational) office (of hours)
  4. (relational) entrance (of an examination)
  5. (relational) foster (of a parent or child)

Related terms

  • приём (prijóm), приёмная (prijómnaja)
