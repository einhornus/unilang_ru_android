<hr><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">moss</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">mosses</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>Any of various small, green, seedless plants growing on the ground or on the surfaces of trees, stones, etc.; now specifically, a plant of the phylum Bryophyta (formerly division <span class="biota"><a href="https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Musci" class="extiw" title="wikispecies:Musci">Musci</a></span>).
<dl><dd><span class="nyms hypernym"><span class="defdate">Hypernym:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">bryophyte</span></span></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A kind or species of such plants.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">informal</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> Any alga, lichen, bryophyte, or other plant of seemingly simple structure.
<dl><dd><span class="nyms hyponym"><span class="defdate">Hyponyms:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">alga</span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en">cryptogam</span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en">lichen</span></span></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Spanish <b>moss</b></i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Irish <b>moss</b></i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">club <b>moss</b></i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">now  chiefly  <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Britain" class="extiw" title="w:Great Britain">Britain</a>  regional</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A bog; a fen.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">the <b>mosses</b> of the Scottish border</i></div></dd></dl></li></ol>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Usage_notes">Usage notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<ul><li>The plural form <i class="Latn mention" lang="en">mosses</i> is used when more than one kind of moss is meant. The singular <i class="Latn mention" lang="en">moss</i> is used referring to a collection of moss plants of the same kind.</li></ul>
