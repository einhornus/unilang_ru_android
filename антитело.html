антитело (23551)


Etymology[edit]

First attested in 1895. анти- (anti-) + | тело (télo), calque of French anticorps or German Antikörper.

Noun[edit]

антитело • (antitélo) | n | inan (genitive антитела, nominative plural антитела, genitive plural антител)

  1. (medicine) antibody (protein that binds to a specific antigen)

Related terms[edit]

  • тело (télo)
