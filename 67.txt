<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en"><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">bars</b>)
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Bar_square_steel.jpg/250px-Bar_square_steel.jpg" decoding="async" width="250" height="163" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Bar_square_steel.jpg/375px-Bar_square_steel.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Bar_square_steel.jpg/500px-Bar_square_steel.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3008" data-file-height="1960" />  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"></div>Two steel bars.</div></div></div>
<ol><li>A solid, more or less rigid object of metal or wood with a uniform cross-section smaller than its length.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">The window was protected by steel <b>bars</b>.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable<span class="ib-comma">,</span> uncountable<span class="ib-comma">,</span> metallurgy</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A solid metal object with uniform (round, square, hexagonal, octagonal or rectangular) cross-section; in the US its smallest dimension is <small><sup>1</sup><big>⁄</big><sub>4</sub></small> inch or greater, a piece of thinner material being called a strip.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Ancient Sparta used iron <b>bars</b> instead of handy coins in more valuable alloy, to physically discourage the use of money.</i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">We are expecting a carload of <b><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></b> tomorrow.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>A cuboid piece of any solid commodity.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en"><b><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></b> of chocolate</i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en"><b><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></b> of soap</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>A broad shaft, or band, or stripe.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">a <b><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></b> of light</i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">a <b><a href = vnd.unilang:бар.html>bar</a></b> of colour</i></div></dd></dl></li>
