зме́йка (16784)


Etymology[edit]

Diminutive of змея (zmejá).

Noun[edit]

зме́йка • (zméjka) | f | anim or f | inan (genitive зме́йки, nominative plural зме́йки, genitive plural зме́ек)

  1. (animate) little snake
  2. (inanimate) spiral or helical object, such as the condenser of a distillation apparatus
