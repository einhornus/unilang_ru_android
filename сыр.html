сыр (5016)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *syrъ (“cheese”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *sūˀras, from Proto-Indo-European *SuHrós.

Noun[edit]

сыр • (syr) | m | inan (genitive сыра, nominative plural сыры, genitive plural сыро́в)

  1. cheese

Related terms[edit]

  • сырник (syrnik)
  • сырный (syrnyj)

Adjective[edit]

сыр • (syr)
