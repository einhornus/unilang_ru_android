разли́в (16170)


Participle[edit]

разли́в • (razlív)

  1. short past adverbial perfective participle of разли́ть (razlítʹ): having been poured

Noun[edit]

разли́в • (razlív) | m | inan (genitive разли́ва, nominative plural разли́вы, genitive plural разли́вов)

  1. flood, overflow
  2. bottling
  3. spill
