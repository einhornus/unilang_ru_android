ви́ться (7765)


Etymology[edit]

вить (vítʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

ви́ться • (vítʹsja) | impf

  1. (hair) to wave, to curl
  2. (plants) to twine (about, round)
  3. (birds, insects etc.) to hover
  4. (sut etc.) to eddy, to whirl
  5. (river, road) to wind, to meander
  6. (snake) to twist, to writhe
  7. to flutter
  8. passive of вить (vítʹ)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (whirl): кружи́ться (kružítʹsja)

Related terms[edit]

  • вито́й (vitój)
  • вито́к (vitók)
  • витиеватый (vitijevátyj)
