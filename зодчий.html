зо́дчий (27722)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic зьдъчии (zĭdŭčii, “potter, builder, bricklayer, mason”), from зьдъ (zĭdŭ, “stone wall”), зьдати (zĭdati), from Proto-Slavic *zьdati (“to build”).

Noun[edit]

зо́дчий • (zódčij) | m | anim (genitive зо́дчего, nominative plural зо́дчие, genitive plural зо́дчих)

  1. architect
