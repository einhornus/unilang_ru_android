пропи́ть (17341)


Etymology[edit]

про- (pro-) + | пить (pitʹ)

Verb[edit]

пропи́ть • (propítʹ) | pf (imperfective пропива́ть)

  1. to spend/squander on drink
    пропи́ть ве́щи ― propítʹ véšči ― to sell one's things to get money for drink; swap one's things for drink
  2. (colloquial) to ruin through drinking
    пропи́ть го́лос ― propítʹ gólos ― to ruin/lose one's voice through drinking
