свекла (11289)


Etymology 1

Apparently from misreading свёкла written without the trema since the times of ликбе́з in the twentieth century.

Noun

свекла́ • (sveklá) f inan (genitive свеклы́, nominative plural свеклы́, genitive plural свеко́л)

  1. Alternative form of свёкла (svjókla)

Etymology 2

Noun

  1. Alternative spelling of свёкла (svjókla)
