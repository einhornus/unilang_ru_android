дире́кция (12971)


Noun[edit]

дире́кция • (dirékcija) | f | inan (genitive дире́кции, nominative plural дире́кции, genitive plural дире́кций)

  1. directorate, board of directors
  2. directorate, management
  3. director's office, manager's office
