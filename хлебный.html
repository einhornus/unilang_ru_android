хле́бный (8155)


Etymology

хлеб (xleb) + | -ный (-nyj)

Adjective

хле́бный • (xlébnyj)

  1. (relational) grain (Us), corn (UK), cereal
  2. (relational) bread
  3. baker's
  4. (colloquial) profitable

Related terms

  • хлеб (xleb)
