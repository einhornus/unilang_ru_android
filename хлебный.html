хле́бный (8153)


Etymology[edit]

хлеб (xleb) + | -ный (-nyj)

Adjective[edit]

хле́бный • (xlébnyj)

  1. (relational) grain (US), corn (UK), cereal
  2. (relational) bread
  3. baker's
  4. (colloquial) profitable

Related terms[edit]

  • хлеб (xleb)
