отня́ть (3490)


Etymology[edit]

от- (ot-) + | -нять (-njátʹ)

Verb[edit]

отня́ть • (otnjátʹ) | pf (imperfective отнима́ть or отымать)

  1. to take (from)
  2. to subtract; to take away; to abstract
  3. to deduct, to take away, to take from, to take off
  4. (also | figuratively) to take away (from); to bereave (of)
    Он отнял у неё после́днюю наде́жду.
    On ótnjal u nejó poslédnjuju nadéždu.
    He has taken away her last hope.
  5. to withdraw; to remove (hands)
  6. to rob (of), to deprive (of)
  7. (colloquial) to amputate

Related terms[edit]

  • отняться (otnjátʹsja), отниматься (otnimátʹsja)
  • отнятие (otnjátije)
