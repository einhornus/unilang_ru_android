<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Usage_notes">Usage notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h5>
<ul><li><i>Wide</i> is usually used instead of <i>long</i> when referring to a horizontal dimension (left to right).</li>
<li><i>Tall</i> or <i>high</i> are usually used instead of <i>long</i> when referring to positive vertical dimension (upwards), and <i>deep</i> when referring to negative vertical dimension (downwards).</li></ul>
