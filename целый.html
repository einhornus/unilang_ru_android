це́лый (305)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *cělъ; compare Old Church Slavonic цѣлъ (cělŭ).

Adjective

це́лый • (célyj) (comparative (по)целе́е or (по)целе́й)

  1. whole, entire
  2. intact, safe
  3. integer
