здра́вица (27381)


Noun

здра́вица • (zdrávica) f inan (genitive здра́вицы, nominative plural здра́вицы, genitive plural здра́виц)

  1. toast (salutation while drinking alcohol)

Synonyms

  • (salutation while drinking alcohol): тост (tost)
