хребе́т (4247)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *xrьbьtъ. Compare Ukrainian хребе́т (xrebét), Belarusian хрыбет (xrybjét), Bulgarian хръбет (hrǎbét), Polish grzbiet

Noun[edit]

хребе́т • (xrebét) | m | inan (genitive хребта́, nominative plural хребты́, genitive plural хребто́в)

  1. spine, spinal column, backbone (also figuratively)
  2. crest (of a wave or hill), ridge, range (a chain of mountains)
    Аляскинский хребе́т ― Aljáskinskij xrebét ― Alaska Range

Related terms[edit]

  • хребетный (xrebétnyj)
