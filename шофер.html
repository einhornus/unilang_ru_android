шофер (1563)


Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from French chauffeur.

Noun[edit]

шофер • (šófer) | m | anim (genitive шофера, nominative plural шофера, genitive plural шоферов)

  1. (professional | or | colloquial) chauffeur, driver
Usage notes[edit]
  • This pronunciation is often considered non-standard.
