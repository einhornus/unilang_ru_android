вла́га (7542)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic вла́га (vlaga), from Proto-Slavic *volga. Displaced the Old East Slavic inherited form волога (vológa).

Noun[edit]

вла́га • (vlága) | f | inan (genitive вла́ги, uncountable)

  1. moisture
