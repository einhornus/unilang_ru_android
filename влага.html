вла́га (7544)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic вла́га (vlaga), from Proto-Slavic *volga. Displaced the Old East Slavic inherited form воло́га (vológa).

Noun

вла́га • (vlága) f inan (genitive вла́ги, uncountable)

  1. moisture

Derived terms

  • вла́жный (vlážnyj)
    • вла́жность (vlážnostʹ)
