Etymology[edit]

Noun[edit]

тычо́к • (tyčók) | m | inan (genitive тычка́, nominative plural тычки́, genitive plural тычко́в)

  1. (colloquial) poke, jab
  2. (low | | colloquial) thing sticking up in the air
  3. (specialist) short, vertical brick face in masonry
