эшафо́т (22299)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French échafaud.

Noun[edit]

эшафо́т • (ešafót) | m | inan (genitive эшафо́та, nominative plural эшафо́ты, genitive plural эшафо́тов)

  1. (historical) scaffold (elevated platform on which a criminal is executed)

Synonyms[edit]

  • пла́ха (pláxa)

Related terms[edit]

  • эшафотик (ešafotik)
  • эшафотный (ešafotnyj)
