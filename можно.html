мо́жно (101)


Etymology[edit]

From мог- (mog-) + | -ный (-nyj) + | -о (-o) (but the form **можный does not exist).

Related terms[edit]

  • мочь (močʹ)

Usage notes[edit]

  • The forms "**неможно" or "**не мо́жно" are not used in standard Russian. To negate мо́жно, use нельзя́ (nelʹzjá), which doesn't have a positive form "льзя", or не возмо́жно (ne vozmóžno).
