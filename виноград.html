виногра́д (6599)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic виноградъ (vinogradŭ), calqued from Germanic (compare German Wingert).

Noun[edit]

виногра́д • (vinográd) | m | inan (genitive виногра́да, uncountable)

  1. (uncountable, | collectively) grape, grapes
  2. vintage
  3. vine

Related terms[edit]

  • виноградарство (vinográdarstvo)
  • виноградарь (vinográdarʹ)
  • виноградина (vinográdina)
  • виногра́дник (vinográdnik)
  • виногра́дный (vinográdnyj)
