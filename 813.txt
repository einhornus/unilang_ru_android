<hr><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">snow</strong> (<i>countable and uncountable</i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en">snows</b>)
</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The frozen, crystalline state of water that falls as precipitation.
<ul><li><b>1928</b>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A._A._Milne" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:A. A. Milne">A. A. Milne</a>, <i><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_House_at_Pooh_Corner" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The House at Pooh Corner">The House at Pooh Corner</a></i>,
<dl><dd>The wind had dropped, and the <b>snow</b>, tired of rushing around in circles trying to catch itself up, now fluttered gently down until it found a place on which to rest.<i></i></dd></dl></li></ul></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> Any similar frozen form of a gas or liquid.
<ul><li><b>2008</b>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neal_Asher" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Neal Asher">Neal Asher</a>, "Alien Archaeology"
<dl><dd>Clad in a coldsuit Jael trudged through a thin layer of CO<sub>2</sub> <b>snow</b> ...</dd></dl></li></ul></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">countable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A snowfall; a blanket of frozen, crystalline water.
<dl><dd><i>We have had several heavy <b>snows</b> this year.</i></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A shade of the color white.
<dl><dd><div class="color-panel">snow colour: <span style="background-color:F9F5E6; display:inline-block; width:80px;"> </span></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">uncountable</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The moving pattern of random dots displayed on a television, etc., when no transmission signal is being received.
<dl><dd><span class="nyms synonym"><span class="defdate">Synonym:</span> <span class="Latn" lang="en">shash</span></span></dd></dl></li>

</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">impersonal</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> To have snow fall from the sky.
<dl><dd><i>It is <b>snowing</b>.</i></dd>

<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">colloquial</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> To hoodwink someone, especially by presenting confusing information.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content">poker</span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> To bluff in draw poker by refusing to draw any cards.</li></ol>
