симули́ровать (26936)


Etymology[edit]

From German simulieren, from Latin simulō and German -ieren, with the Russian suffix -овать (-ovatʹ). Morphologically симул- + -ировать (-írovatʹ).

Verb[edit]

симули́ровать • (simulírovatʹ) | impf or pf

  1. to simulate
  2. to feign an illness or disability, especially in clinical or legal context; to malinger

Related terms[edit]

  • симуля́нт (simuljánt)
  • симуляция (simuljácija)
