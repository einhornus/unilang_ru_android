разбива́ться (12694)


Etymology[edit]

разбива́ть (razbivátʹ) + | -ся (-sja)

Verb[edit]

разбива́ться • (razbivátʹsja) | impf (perfective разби́ться)

  1. (intransitive) to break, to crash, to shatter
  2. (intransitive) to break up, to split, to divide
  3. to collapse, to die (e.g. of dreams)
  4. to get seriously hurt
  5. passive of разбива́ть (razbivátʹ)
