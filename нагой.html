наго́й (11527)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *nagъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nogʷós (“naked”); see there for the cognates.

Adjective[edit]

наго́й • (nagój) (no comparative)

  1. nude, naked, bare

Synonyms[edit]

  • го́лый (gólyj)

Related terms[edit]

  • нагота́ (nagotá)
  • нагишо́м (nagišóm)
