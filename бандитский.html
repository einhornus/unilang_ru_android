банди́тский (9618)


Etymology[edit]

банди́т (bandít) + | -ский (-skij)

Adjective[edit]

банди́тский • (bandítskij)

  1. (relational) thug, bandit, highwayman
  2. thug's, bandit's, highwayman's
  3. like a bandit, thug, or highwayman

Related terms[edit]

  • банди́т (bandít)
  • бандити́зм (banditízm)
