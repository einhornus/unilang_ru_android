то́кмо (20046)


Etymology[edit]

Cognate with Bulgarian тъкмо (tʺkmo, “just, exactly”), Macedonian токму (tokmu, “just, exactly”) and Serbo-Croatian такмен, takmen (“equal”). Related to то́чка (točka).

Adverb[edit]
